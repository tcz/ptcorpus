South Korean movie actresses born in the year 1947

Here are 5 famous actresses from South Korea were born in 1947:

Yoon Yeo-jeong

Yoon Yeo-jeong (June 19, 1947 Kaesong-) also known as Youn Yuh-jung, Yeo-jeong Yoon, Yun Yeo-jeong, Yun Yǒ-chǒng, Youn Yuh Jung, Yoon Yeo-jung, Yuh-Jung Youn, Yeo-Jong Yun, Yoon Yeo-Jeong, Yoon Yeojung or Youn Yu Jung is a South Korean actor. She has one child, Jo Eol.

Moon Hee

Moon Hee (July 16, 1947 Busan-) also known as Hie Mun, Lee Sunim, Mun Hui, Mun Hǔi, I Sunim or Ri Sunim is a South Korean actor. She has two children, Jang Jung-ho and Jang Seo-jeong.

Park Seung-Tae

Park Seung-Tae (November 15, 1947 South Korea-) also known as Seung-tae Park is a South Korean actor.

Jeong Yeong-Suk

Jeong Yeong-Suk (April 1, 1947 Sonchon County-) also known as Yeong-suk Jeong, Jeong Young-Sook, Jung Young-sook, Jung Youngsook, Jung Young Suk or Jeong Yeong-sook is a South Korean actor. Her children are called Jeon Yoo-kyung and Jeon Yoo-seong.

Hong Se-Mi

Hong Se-Mi (October 19, 1947 Incheon-) also known as Se-mi Hong is a South Korean actor.
