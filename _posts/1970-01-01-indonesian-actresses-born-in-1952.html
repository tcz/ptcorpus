Indonesian movie actresses born in the year 1952

Here are 7 famous actresses from Indonesia were born in 1952:

Jajang C. Noer

Jajang C. Noer (June 28, 1952 Paris-) also known as Jayang Pamoncak, Lidia Djunita Pamontjak or Jajang Pamuntjak is an Indonesian actor and film producer. Her children are called Nitta Nazyra Noer and Marah Laut Noer.

Tuti Kirana

Tuti Kirana (October 28, 1952 Jakarta-) a.k.a. Tutie Kirana is an Indonesian actor. Her children are called Djenar Maesa Ayu, Hyza and Panji.

Ratna Riantiarno

Ratna Riantiarno (April 23, 1952 Manado-) otherwise known as Ratna Karya Madjid Riantiarno is an Indonesian actor and artist.

Leily Sagita

Leily Sagita (December 19, 1952 Padang Pariaman Regency-) also known as Leyla Sagita is an Indonesian actor.

Purwaniatun

Purwaniatun (December 1, 1952 Surakarta-) is an Indonesian actor.

Debbie Cinthya Dewi

Debbie Cinthya Dewi (August 4, 1952 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor and model. She has two children, Arieya Bima and Fajar Faisal.

Farida Pasha

Farida Pasha (August 26, 1952 Tasikmalaya-) is an Indonesian actor.
