Hongkongese actresses born in 1967

Here are 5 famous actresses from Hong Kong were born in 1967:

Jay Lau

Jay Lau (January 15, 1967 Hong Kong-) otherwise known as Lau Kam Ling, Jinling Liu, Kam-Ling Lau or Jan Lau is a Hongkongese actor.

Monica Chan

Monica Chan (October 28, 1967 Hong Kong-) also known as Fa-Rong Chen, Monica Chan Fat-Yung or Fa-Yung Chen is a Hongkongese actor and model.

Charine Chan

Charine Chan (November 17, 1967 Hong Kong-) also known as Ka-Ling Chan is a Hongkongese actor.

Vivian Chow

Vivian Chow (November 10, 1967 Hong Kong-) also known as Chow, Vivian, Wai-Man Chow, Chow Wai Man or Huimin Zhou is a Hongkongese singer, songwriter, actor, master of ceremonies and presenter.

Regina Kent

Regina Kent (September 4, 1967 Hong Kong-August 16, 1999 Hong Kong) otherwise known as Gi, Gan Wai-jun, Gan Wai-Jan, Gan Wai-Chan, Jade Kan, Kan Wai-Chun, Virgina Kan or Regina Kan Wai Ling was a Hongkongese actor.
