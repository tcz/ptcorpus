Czech movie actresses born in the year 1971

Here are 6 famous actresses from Czech Republic were born in 1971:

Klára Melisková

Klára Melisková (November 29, 1971 Czechoslovakia-) also known as Klára Melíšková is a Czech actor.

Barbora Munzarová

Barbora Munzarová (December 28, 1971 Prague-) a.k.a. Bára Munzarová or Barbora Munzarová Trnavská is a Czech actor. Her child is called Anna Dvořáková.

Klára Pollertová-Trojanová

Klára Pollertová-Trojanová (September 17, 1971 Prague-) otherwise known as Klára Trojanová-Pollertová, Klara Pollertová-Trojanová, Klára Pollertová, Klára Trojanová Pollerotvá, K. Pollertová, Klárka Pollertová or Klára Trojanová is a Czech actor. Her children are called Josef Trojan, František Trojan, Václav Trojan and Anthony Trojan.

Alice Nellis

Alice Nellis (January 3, 1971 České Budějovice-) is a Czech film director, screenwriter, flautist, web designer, teacher, actor and translator. She has three children, Ella Nellis, Oliver Nellis and František Cibulka.

Regina Gabajová

Regina Gabajová (April 21, 1971 Liberec-) is a Czech actor.

Dana Morávková

Dana Morávková (July 29, 1971 Písek-) also known as Dana Morávková or Dana Moravková is a Czech actor and presenter. Her child is called Petr Malásek.
