Indian movie stars born in 1940

Here are 9 famous actors from India were born in 1940:

Amjad Khan

Amjad Khan (November 12, 1940 Peshawar-July 27, 1992 Mumbai) also known as Amzad Khan, Gabbar Singh, Late Amjad Khan, Amjad or Amjad Zakaria Khan was an Indian actor, film director and composer. His children are called Shadaab Khan, Ahlam Khan and Seemaab Khan.

Krishnam Raju

Krishnam Raju (January 20, 1940 Mogalthur-) a.k.a. Venkata Krishnam Raju Uppalapati, Krishnamraju, Krisham Raju, Rebel Star or Uppalapati Venkata Krishnam Raju is an Indian actor and politician.

Murali Mohan

Murali Mohan (June 24, 1940 Chataparru-) also known as Sri Murali Mohan, Murali Mohan Maganti or Maganti Raja Babu is an Indian businessperson, politician, actor and film producer. He has two children, Madhu Bindu and Ram Mohan.

Ram Gopal Bajaj

Ram Gopal Bajaj (March 5, 1940 Darbhanga-) also known as Gopal Ram Babaj, Bajju Bhai or Padmashree Professor Ram Gopal Bajaj is an Indian actor, theatre director, academician, professor and screenwriter. He has three children, Aseem Bajaj, Riju Bajaj and Prakash Bajaj.

Chinmoy Roy

Chinmoy Roy (January 16, 1940 Comilla-) also known as Chinmoy Ray is an Indian actor.

Vikram Gokhale

Vikram Gokhale (October 30, 1940 Pune-) also known as Vikram Gokhle is an Indian actor, film director and social worker.

Goga Kapoor

Goga Kapoor (December 15, 1940 Gujranwala-March 3, 2011 Malad) also known as Goga or Ravinder Kapoor was an Indian actor. He had one child, Payal Kapoor.

Kumar Shahani

Kumar Shahani (December 7, 1940 Larkana-) otherwise known as Kumar Sahni is an Indian film director, screenwriter, actor and visual artist.

Antony Thomas

Antony Thomas (July 26, 1940 Kolkata-) also known as Anthony Thomas or Antony R. Thomas is an Indian film director, television director, screenwriter, television producer and actor.
