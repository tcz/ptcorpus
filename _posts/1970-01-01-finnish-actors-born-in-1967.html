Finnish actors born in 1967

Here are 6 famous actors from Finland were born in 1967:

Kari-Pekka Toivonen

Kari-Pekka Toivonen (May 6, 1967 Turku-) also known as Kari Toivonen or K.-P. Toivonen is a Finnish actor and screenwriter.

Janne Virtanen

Janne Virtanen (July 25, 1967 Raisio-) also known as Janne Asseri Virtanen is a Finnish actor, television producer and television director.

Mikko Kouki

Mikko Kouki (September 14, 1967 Lohja-) is a Finnish actor and screenwriter.

Mikko Jurkka

Mikko Jurkka (November 18, 1967 Espoo-) is a Finnish actor.

Jaakko Saariluoma

Jaakko Saariluoma (July 25, 1967 Helsinki-) also known as Jaska or Eino Jaakko Saariluoma is a Finnish actor, screenwriter, film director, comedian, tv personality and television presenter.

Juha Vuorinen

Juha Vuorinen (March 31, 1967 Helsinki-) a.k.a. Juha Pekka Vuorinen is a Finnish newspaper publisher, actor, screenwriter and film producer.
