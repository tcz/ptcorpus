South Korean movie actresses born in the year 1993

Here are 7 famous actresses from South Korea were born in 1993:

IU

IU (May 16, 1993 Seongdong District-) also known as 아이유, 이지은, Lee Ji Eun, Ji-Eun Lee, Lee Ji-eun, Nation's Little Sister, A I-yu, I Ji-eun or Yi Chi-ŭn is a South Korean singer, actor, composer, songwriter, guitarist, dancer, television presenter and master of ceremonies.

Luna

Luna (August 12, 1993 Seoul-) a.k.a. Park Sunyoung, Park Sun-young, Bak Seon-yeong, Pak Sŏn-yŏng or Runa is a South Korean singer, model, actor and dancer.

Park Ji-yeon

Park Ji-yeon (June 7, 1993 Seoul-) otherwise known as Park Ji-Yeon, Ji Yeon, Jiyeon, Park Jiyeon, Bak Ji-yeon or Pak Chiyŏn is a South Korean actor, singer, model, dancer and master of ceremonies.

Kim Dasom

Kim Dasom (May 6, 1993 Seoul-) otherwise known as Dasom Kim, Kim Da-som or Dasom is a South Korean singer, dancer and actor.

Jeong Eun-ji

Jeong Eun-ji (August 18, 1993 Haeundae District-) also known as Jeong Hye-rim, Chŏng Hye-rim, Chŏng Ŭn-ji or Jeong Eun-ji is a South Korean actor, model, singer, dancer and spokesperson.

Bae Noo-ri

Bae Noo-ri (February 4, 1993 Anyang-) is a South Korean actor.

Hyoyoung

Hyoyoung (April 22, 1993 Gwangju-) a.k.a. 류효영, 한빛효영, Hyoyoung Ryoo, Ryu Hyo-young, Ryu Hyoyoung or One Light Hyoyoung is a South Korean singer, model, actor and rapper.
