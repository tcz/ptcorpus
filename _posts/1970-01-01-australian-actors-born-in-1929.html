Australian movie stars born in 1929

George Mikell

George Mikell (April 4, 1929 Lithuania-) also known as George Mikel or Jurgis Mikelaitis is an Australian actor and writer.

Richard Meikle

Richard Meikle (October 10, 1929 Australia-June 2, 1991) was an Australian actor. He had one child, Sam Meikle.

Ron Haddrick

Ron Haddrick (April 9, 1929 Adelaide-) a.k.a. Ronald Norman Haddrick, R. Haddrick, Ronald Norman Haddrick MBE or Ronald Norman Haddrick AM MBE is an Australian actor, voice actor and cricketer. He has two children, Greg Haddrick and Lynette Haddrick.

Kenneth J. Warren

Kenneth J. Warren (September 25, 1929 Parramatta-August 27, 1973 Effingham, Surrey) also known as Kenneth Warren, Kenneth John Warren or Ken Warren was an Australian actor. His child is called Damian Warren.

Kevin Miles

Kevin Miles (April 17, 1929 Melbourne-) is an Australian actor.

Kevin Manser
