Spanish movie actresses born in the year 1958

Here are 10 famous actresses from Spain were born in 1958:

Sara Bilbatua

Sara Bilbatua (August 21, 1958 Eibar-) a.k.a. Sara Birbatua or Sara Bilbatúa is a Spanish casting director and actor.

María Casal (February 26, 1958 Madrid-) also known as María Consuelo Casal Mínguez, Maria Casal or Maria Consuelo Casal is a Spanish actor.

Sandra Mozarowsky

Sandra Mozarowsky (October 17, 1958 Tangier-September 14, 1977 Madrid) a.k.a. Sandra Mazarowski, Sandra Mozarosky, Sandra Mozarovski, Sandra Mozarovsky or Sandra Mozarowski was a Spanish actor.

Patricia Adriani

Patricia Adriani (June 2, 1958 Madrid-) a.k.a. María Asunción García Moreno is a Spanish actor.

Lolita Flores

Isabel María Pérez

Olga Viza

Olga Viza (August 18, 1958 Barcelona-) is a Spanish actor.

Ana Curra
