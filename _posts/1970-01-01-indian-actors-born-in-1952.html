As a journalist, he worked with The Indian Express and The Times of India before launching his own media platform, the Asian College of Journalism, in Chennai, India. Sashi Kumar has received numerous awards for his contributions to the film and media industries, including the National Film Award for Best Film on Family Welfare for his film, "Vamsha Vriksha," in 1972.
