Filipino actors who were born in 1950

Here are 6 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1950:

Jaime Fabregas

Jaime Fabregas (February 28, 1950 Iriga-) also known as Jaime G. Fabregas, Jaime B. Fabregas, Jamie Fabregas, Juan Fabregas, Jimmy Fabregas, Jim or Jimmy is a Filipino actor and film score composer. He has seven children, Minco Fabregas, Paolo Fabregas, Lara Fabregas, Mateo Fabregas, Leandro Fabregas, Emilio Fabregas and Inez Fabregas.

Leo Martinez

Leo Martinez (February 3, 1950 Philippines-) otherwise known as Leo Martin or Leo G. Martinez is a Filipino comedian, actor and film director.

Roxlee

Roxlee (August 16, 1950 Naga, Camarines Sur-) a.k.a. Roque Federizon Lee, ROXLEE, Roque Lee or Roque 'Roxlee' Lee is a Filipino film director, actor, cartoonist, painter and screenwriter.

Pen Medina

Pen Medina (August 27, 1950 Manila-) also known as Crispin Medina, Pen P. Medina, Pen, Crispin "Pen" Medina Sr. or Crispin Medina Sr. is a Filipino actor and screenwriter. He has seven children, Ping Medina, Alex Vincent Medina, John Medina, Karl Medina, Japs Medina, Victor Medina and Zeth Medina.

Roy Alvarez

Roy Alvarez (March 23, 1950 Pagbilao-February 11, 2014 Quezon City) a.k.a. Leandro Delantar Alvarez was a Filipino actor, screenwriter, film director and television director.

Richard King

Richard King (April 16, 1950 Santa Cruz, Manila-) otherwise known as Pepito Sy is a Filipino actor.
