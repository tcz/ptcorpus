Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1954

Here are 7 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1954:

George Rudy

George Rudy (October 3, 1954 Jember Regency-) also known as George Rudyanto is an Indonesian actor.

José Rizal Manua

José Rizal Manua (September 14, 1954 Padang-) also known as Jose Rizal Manua or Yose Rizal Manua is an Indonesian actor, artist and poet. He has five children, Shakti Harimurti, Sanca Khatulistiwa, Nuansa Ayu Jawadwipa, Nusa Kalimasada and Niken Flora Rinjani.

Ruhut Sitompul

Ruhut Sitompul (March 24, 1954 Medan-) is an Indonesian advocate, politician and actor. He has one child, Christian Husein Sitompul.

Noorca Marendra Massardi

Noorca Marendra Massardi (February 28, 1954 Subang-) is an Indonesian poet, writer, playwright, screenwriter, actor, television presenter and columnist. He has two children, Cassandra Massardi and Nakita Massardi.

Doyok

Doyok (August 17, 1954 Sidoarjo Regency-) also known as Sudarmadji or Doyok Sudarmadji is an Indonesian actor and comedian.

Boy Tirayoh

Boy Tirayoh (July 25, 1954 Semarang-) is an Indonesian actor.

Asrul Zulmi

Asrul Zulmi (September 25, 1954 Deli Serdang Regency-March 5, 2007 Medan) was an Indonesian actor.
