Filipino actors who were born in 1975

Here are 7 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1975:

Jeric Raval

Jeric Raval (September 30, 1975 San Fernando City-) a.k.a. Ricardo Buensuceso is a Filipino actor.

Ian Veneracion

Ian Veneracion (February 7, 1975 Metro Manila-) also known as Steven Ian Veneracion or Stephen Ian Veneracion is a Filipino actor.

Willy Santos

Willy Santos (October 1, 1975 Subic Bay-) otherwise known as Willy Monolato Santos, Willer, Will or Wet-Willy is a Filipino skateboarder and actor.

Kendrick Ong

Kendrick Ong (December 17, 1975 Manila-) is a Filipino actor.

Ian De Leon

Ian De Leon (December 11, 1975-) also known as Ian Christopher, Kristoffer Ian De Leon, Ian Kristoffer de Leon or Ian Christopher de Leon is a Filipino actor.

Bobby Andrews

Bobby Andrews (November 30, 1975 Manila-) also known as Robert Riv Andrews, Robert Riu Andrews, Robert Andrews, Bob Andrews, Bobby, Robert or Bob is a Filipino actor. He has two children, Arty Andrews and Gabbie Andrews.

Rico Yan

Rico Yan (March 14, 1975 Pasig-March 29, 2002 Puerto Princesa) also known as Ricardo Carlos Castro Yan, Corics or Ricardo Carlos Castro-Yan was a Filipino actor, model and businessperson.
