Argentine actors who were born in 1905

Here are 10 famous actors from Argentina were born in 1905:

Pedro Quartucci

Pedro Quartucci (July 30, 1905 Buenos Aires-April 20, 1983 Buenos Aires) was an Argentine actor and professional boxer.

Luis Sandrini

Luis Sandrini (February 22, 1905 Buenos Aires-July 5, 1980 Buenos Aires) also known as Luis Santiago Sandrini Lagomarsino or Luis Santiago Sandrini was an Argentine actor and film producer. He had one child, Sandra Sandrini.

George Rigaud

George Rigaud (August 11, 1905 Buenos Aires-January 17, 1984 Leganés) a.k.a. Jorge Rigaud, Georges Rigaud, Jorge Regaud, Giorgio Rigatto, Giorgio Rigato, Jorge Rigeaut, Pedro Jorge Rigato Delisset or Pedro Jorge Rigato Delissetche was an Argentine actor.

Barry Norton

Barry Norton (June 16, 1905 Buenos Aires-August 24, 1956 Hollywood) also known as Alfredo Carlos Birabén was an Argentine actor and ballroom dancer. He had one child, Sharon Biraben Rider.

Pascual Pelliciota

Pascual Pelliciota (July 20, 1905 Buenos Aires-October 28, 1985 Buenos Aires) was an Argentine actor.

Arturo García Buhr

Arturo García Buhr (December 16, 1905 Dolores, Buenos Aires-October 4, 1995 Buenos Aires) also known as Arturo Garcia Bourg was an Argentine film director and actor.

Fidel Pintos

Fidel Pintos (August 28, 1905 Argentina-May 11, 1974 Argentina) was an Argentine actor.

Charlo

Charlo (July 7, 1905 La Pampa Province-October 30, 1990 Buenos Aires) a.k.a. Carlos Pérez de la Riestra was an Argentine actor.

Lito Bayardo

Lito Bayardo (March 3, 1905 Rosario-March 7, 1986) also known as Juan Manuel García Ferrari was an Argentine actor, poet, singer, guitarist and composer.

Marcos Caplán

Marcos Caplán (March 15, 1905 Buenos Aires-October 3, 1979 Buenos Aires) was an Argentine actor.
