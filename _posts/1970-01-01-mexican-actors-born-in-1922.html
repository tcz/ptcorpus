Mexican actors who were born in 1922

Here are 5 famous actors from Mexico were born in 1922:

Gustavo Alatriste

Gustavo Alatriste (August 25, 1922 Mexico City-July 25, 2006 Houston) a.k.a. Gustavo Alatriste Rodríguez was a Mexican actor, film producer, film director and screenwriter. He had one child, Viridiana Alatriste.

Mario Almada

Mario Almada (January 7, 1922 Huatabampo-) a.k.a. Mario Almada Otero is a Mexican actor, film producer and screenwriter.

José Angel Espinoza

José Angel Espinoza (August 2, 1922 Choix-) also known as Jose Angel Espinoza, Ferrusquilla, J. A. Espinosa 'Ferrusquilla', José A. Espinoza 'Ferrusquilla', José A. Espinosa 'Ferrusquilla', José Ángel Espinoza 'Ferrusquilla', Miguel A. Espinosa 'Ferusquilla', Jose Angel Espinosa, Ángel Espinoza, José Ángel Espinoza Ferrusquilla, José Ángel Espinoza Aragón, José Ángel Espinosa 'Ferrusquilla' or José Ángel Espinoza is a Mexican actor and songwriter. His child is called Angélica Aragón.

Antonio Raxel

Antonio Raxel (April 13, 1922 San Cristóbal de las Casas-November 25, 1999 Mexico City) a.k.a. Antonio S. Raxel, Antonio Raxell, Antonio Salazar, Alejos Antonio Salazar, Raxel Antonio or Antonio Salazar Alejos was a Mexican actor and voice actor.

Tom Fears

Tom Fears (December 3, 1922 Guadalajara-January 4, 2000 Palm Desert) a.k.a. Thomas Jesse Fears or Tom Sildari Fears was a Mexican american football player, coach and actor.
