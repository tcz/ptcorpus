Brazilian movie actresses born in the year 1976

Here are 11 famous actresses from Brazil were born in 1976:

Emanuelle Araújo

Emanuelle Araújo (July 21, 1976 Salvador-) also known as Emanuelle Araujo is a Brazilian actor and singer. She has one child, Bruna Bulhões.

Luana Piovani

Luana Piovani (August 29, 1976 São Paulo-) a.k.a. Luana Elídia Afonso Piovani is a Brazilian actor and model. Her child is called Dom Piovani.

Lavínia Vlasak

Lavínia Vlasak (June 14, 1976 Rio de Janeiro-) also known as Lavínia Gutmann Vlasak or Lavínia Vlazak is a Brazilian actor. She has two children, Felipe Neto Vlasak and Estella Neto Vlasak.

Karina Bacchi

Karina Bacchi (October 8, 1976 São Manuel-) is a Brazilian model and actor.

Giovanna Antonelli

Giovanna Antonelli (March 18, 1976 Rio de Janeiro-) also known as Giovana Antonelli or Giovanna Antonelli Prado is a Brazilian actor, television producer and presenter. She has three children, Pietro Antonelli Benício, Antônia Antonelli Nogueira and Sofia Antonelli Nogueira.

Mariana Loureiro

Mariana Loureiro (July 15, 1976 São Paulo-) is a Brazilian actor.

Vanessa da Mata

Vanessa da Mata (February 10, 1976 Alto Garças-) a.k.a. Vanessa de Mata or Vanessa Sigiane da Mata Ferreira is a Brazilian singer, songwriter, musician, actor and novelist.

Adriana Alves

Adriana Alves (December 12, 1976 São Paulo-) otherwise known as Adriana Alves Oliveira is a Brazilian actor.

Ingrid de Souza

Ingrid de Souza (April 5, 1976 Belém-) is a Brazilian actor.

Carla Regina

Carla Regina (October 21, 1976 Limeira-) also known as Carla Regina Freitas Cabral is a Brazilian actor.

Karina Barum

Karina Barum (August 11, 1976 Brasília-) a.k.a. Karina Barum Lima is a Brazilian actor. Her child is called Manuela Luz.
