Argentine actors who were born in 1938

Here are 9 famous actors from Argentina were born in 1938:

Jorge D'Elía

Jorge D'Elía (April 28, 1938 La Plata-) also known as Jorge D'Elia is an Argentine actor. He has six children, Federico D'Elia, Marcelo D'Elía, Hernan D'Elía, Demian D'Elía, Diego D'Elía and Facundo D'Elía.

Leonardo Favio

Leonardo Favio (May 28, 1938 Luján de Cuyo-November 5, 2012 Buenos Aires) a.k.a. Leonardo Fabio, Sandro, Favio, Leonardo, Fuad Jorge Jury or El juglar de américa was an Argentine singer, screenwriter, film director, actor and songwriter. His children are called Leonardo Jury and Nico Jury.

Roberto Carnaghi

Roberto Carnaghi (May 13, 1938 Villa Adelina-) also known as Roberto Carnagui or Roberto Luis Carnaghi Fernández is an Argentine actor. His child is called Pablo Carnaghi.

Norman Briski

Norman Briski (January 2, 1938 Santa Fe-) also known as Norman Brisky or Naún Normando Briski is an Argentine actor. His child is called Gastón Briski.

Horacio Dener

Horacio Dener (November 27, 1938 Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine actor.

Rodolfo Bebán

Rodolfo Bebán (May 25, 1938 Ciudadela, Buenos Aires-) otherwise known as Rodolfo Tilli is an Argentine actor and film director. He has six children, Rodrigo Bebán, Diego Bebán, Facundo Bebán, Maria Daniela Bebán, Pedro Emiliano Bebán and Dolores Bebán.

Claudio García Satur

Claudio García Satur (February 22, 1938 Buenos Aires-) also known as Claudio Garcia Satur, Claudio Luis García or Claudio Luis Garcia is an Argentine actor. He has two children, Celeste García Satur and Guillermina García Satur.

Atilio Pozzobon

Atilio Pozzobon (March 24, 1938 Llavallol-) also known as Atilio Pozzobón, Atilio Pozobon, Atilio Ariel Pozzobón or Aitilio Pozzobon is an Argentine actor and comedian.

Carlos Moreno

Carlos Moreno (August 29, 1938 La Plata-March 9, 2014 Buenos Aires) was an Argentine actor and theatre director.
