Filipino actors who were born in 1968

Here are 7 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1968:

Benjie Paras

Jett Pangan (June 21, 1968 Manila-) also known as Pangan, Jett or Reginald Pangan is a Filipino singer, voice actor, actor, guitarist and musician.

Anjo Yllana
