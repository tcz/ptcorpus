Russian actors who were born in 1948

Aleksei Zharkov

Aleksei Zharkov (March 27, 1948 Moscow-) also known as Aleksei Dmitriyevich Zharkov, A. Zharkov, Aleksandr Zharkov or Alyosha Zharkov is a Russian actor and voice actor.

Vyacheslav Bogachyov

Boris Cherdyntsev

Boris Cherdyntsev (October 31, 1948 Saint Petersburg-) also known as B. Cherdyntsev is a Russian actor.

Gérard Depardieu

Gérard Depardieu (December 27, 1948 Châteauroux-) also known as Gerard Depardieu, Gérard Xavier Marcel Depardieu, Depardieu or G. de Pardieu is a Russian actor, film producer, businessperson, film director and television producer. Mikhail Baryshnikov

Aleksandr Romantsov (February 23, 1948 Saint Petersburg-December 6, 2005 Saint Petersburg) a.k.a. A. Romantsov, Aleksandr Ivanovich Romantsov or Anatoly Romantsov was a Russian actor.
