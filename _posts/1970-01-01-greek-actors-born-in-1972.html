Sakis Rouvas

Sakis Rouvas (January 5, 1972 Corfu-) also known as Sakis Roubas, Anastasios Rouvas, Anastasios "Sakis" Rouvas II or Sakis is a Greek record producer, songwriter, athlete, actor, composer, singer, businessperson, film producer, dancer, choreographer, model, fashion designer, author, entrepreneur, voice actor, multi-instrumentalist and television presenter. He has three children, Anastasia Rouvas, Alexandros Rouvas and Ariadne Rouvas.
