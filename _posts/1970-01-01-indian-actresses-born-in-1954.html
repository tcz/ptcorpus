Indian movie stars born in 1954

Here are 9 famous actresses from India were born in 1954:

Mallika Sarabhai

Mallika Sarabhai (May 9, 1954 Ahmedabad-) is an Indian actor and civil rights activist. She has two children, Revanta Sarabhai and Anahita Sarabhai.

Rekha

Rekha (October 10, 1954 Chennai-) a.k.a. Bhanurekha Ganesan, Bhanurekha Gemini Ganesan, Bhanurekha, Baby Bhanurekha, The Bollywood Queen, Madame X, Rekhaji, Re, the reigning Queen of Indian Cinema, Cher, Madonna or Ugly Duckling is an Indian actor and playback singer.

Beena Kak

Beena Kak (February 15, 1954 Bharatpur-) also known as Bina Kak is an Indian politician and actor. Her children are called Amrita Kak and Ankur Kak.

Priya Tendulkar

Priya Tendulkar (October 19, 1954 India-September 19, 2002 Mumbai) a.k.a. Rajni was an Indian actor, social activist and writer.

Sulakshana Pandit

Sulakshana Pandit (July 12, 1954 Mumbai-) also known as Pandit, Sulakshana, Sulakshana, Sulakshna Pandit, Sulakshana Pratap Narain Pandit, Sulakshana Pratapnarayan Pandit or Sulu is an Indian singer and actor.

Chetana Das

Chetana Das (November 4, 1954 Shillong-) a.k.a. Chetana, Chetna, Chetanaa, Chetana D., Madhavi, Chetonaa Das, Chetona Das, Chetna Das, Chetona or Mrs.Chetana Das is an Indian actor. She has two children, Anindita Das and Dipannita Das.

Anita Ratnam

Anita Ratnam (May 21, 1954 Madurai-) a.k.a. Anitha Rathnam is an Indian actor, dancer and choreographer.

Vidhubala

Vidhubala (May 24, 1954 India-) is an Indian actor and television show host. She has one child, Arjun Kumar.

Smita Talvalkar

Smita Talvalkar (September 5, 1954-August 6, 2014 Mumbai) otherwise known as Smita Govilkar was an Indian actor, film producer and film director. She had two children, Amber Talwalkar and Arti Talwalkar.
