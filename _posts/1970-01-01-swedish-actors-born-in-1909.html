Swedish actors who were born in 1909

Gunnar Björnstrand

Oscar Ljung

Oscar Ljung (September 6, 1909 Landskrona-April 29, 1999 Lidingö) a.k.a. Oscar Albin Ljung was a Swedish actor.

Mauritz Hugo (January 12, 1909 Sweden-June 16, 1974 Woodland Hills) otherwise known as Gustaf Folke Maurtiz Hugoo or Mauritz E. Hugo was a Swedish actor.

Lauritz Falk
