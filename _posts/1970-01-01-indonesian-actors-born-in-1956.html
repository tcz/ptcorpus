Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1956

Here are 6 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1956:

Basuki

Basuki (March 5, 1956 Surakarta-December 12, 2007 Jakarta) also known as Agus Basuki Bin Suwito Hadiwiryono was an Indonesian comedian and actor.

Didi Petet

Didi Petet (July 12, 1956 Surabaya-) also known as Didi Widiatmoko is an Indonesian actor, film director, teacher and television director.

Adisoerya Abdi

Adisoerya Abdi (August 29, 1956 Medan-) a.k.a. Adi Surya Abdi is an Indonesian actor and film director. He has two children, Albert Surya and Nigel Pino.

Leroy Osmani

Leroy Osmani (April 23, 1956 Makassar-) is an Indonesian actor.

Boyke Dian Nugraha

Boyke Dian Nugraha (December 14, 1956 Bandung-) a.k.a. Dr. Boyke or Dr. Boyke Nugraha is an Indonesian physician and actor. He has three children, Dhima Paramitha, Dhila Puspitha and Dhitya Prasetya Dian Nugraha.

Sys NS

Sys NS (July 18, 1956 Semarang-) also known as Sys Ns., Hajji Raden Mas Haryo Heroe Syswanto Ns. Soerio Soebagio, Haryo Heroe Syswanto Ns. Soerio Soebagio or Sys Ns is an Indonesian actor, disc jockey, film director, politician and broadcaster.
