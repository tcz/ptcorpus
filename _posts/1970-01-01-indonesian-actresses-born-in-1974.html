Indonesian movie actresses born in the year 1974

Here are 6 famous actresses from Indonesia were born in 1974:

Sarah Sechan

Sarah Sechan (May 9, 1974 Bandung-) is an Indonesian actor, vj, journalist, model and presenter. She has one child, Rajata.

Tamara Bleszynski

Tamara Bleszynski (December 25, 1974 Bandung-) also known as Tamara Bleszynski binti Zbignew Bleszinski, Tamara Natalia Christina Mayawati Bleszynski, Tamara Blezinski or Tamara Blezinsky is an Indonesian actor and model. She has two children, Rassya Islamay Passya and Kenzou Leon Blezynski Lewis.

Rieke Diah Pitaloka

Rieke Diah Pitaloka (January 8, 1974 Garut-) also known as Rieke Dyah Pitaloka or Rieke Diah Pitaloka Intan Purnamasari is an Indonesian actor, writer, presenter, politician, author and tv personality. She has three children, Misesa Adiansyah, Jalumanon Badrika and Sagara Kawani Hadiasyah.

Sita Nursanti

Sita Nursanti (August 27, 1974 Sumedang-) also known as Indah Sita Nursanti, Sita Nursanti RSD, Sita RSD or Indah Sita is an Indonesian actor, singer and broadcaster.

Monica Oemardi

Monica Oemardi (June 16, 1974 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor.

Claudia Hidayat

Claudia Hidayat (September 27, 1974 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor and model.
