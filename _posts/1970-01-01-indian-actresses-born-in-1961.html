Indian movie stars born in 1961

Here are 6 famous actresses from India were born in 1961:

Arundhati Roy

Arundhati Roy (November 24, 1961 Shillong-) also known as arundhati_roy, Arundhati or Suzanna Arundhati Roy is an Indian writer, novelist, screenwriter, actor, author and essayist.

Suhasini Maniratnam

Suhasini Maniratnam (August 15, 1961 Chennai-) a.k.a. Suhasni, Suhasini Manirathnam, Suhasini Mani Rathnam, Suhasini, Suhasini Maniratnam, Suhasini Charuhasan or Suhasini Mani Ratnam is an Indian actor, film director, film producer, screenwriter and writer. She has one child, Nandhan Ratnam.

Supriya Pathak

Supriya Pathak (January 7, 1961 Mumbai-) otherwise known as Supriya Pathak Kapur or Supriya Pathak Kapoor is an Indian actor. She has three children, Sanah Kapoor, Ruhaan Kapoor and Shahid Kapoor.

Bindiya Goswami

Bindiya Goswami (August 8, 1961 Mumbai-) also known as Bindiya Goswami Dutta, Bindya, Bindiya or Bindiya Dutta is an Indian costume designer and actor. She has two children, Nidhi Dutta and Siddhi Dutta.

Debashree Roy

Debashree Roy (June 13, 1961 Kolkata-) also known as Debasree Roy, Debashree Ray, Chumki, Debosri Rae, Shrimati Debashree Roy, Chintamani, Kumari Chumki or Deboshree Roy is an Indian actor, politician, dancer and social activist.

Angela D'Angelo

Angela D'Angelo (March 19, 1961 Albuquerque-) also known as Sukoya, Angela D'angelo, Angela, Sequoia, Holly Davidson, Sukowa, Angela DeAngelo, Angela De'Angelo or Amber Midnight is an Indian film director and actor. She has one child, Ice La Fox.
