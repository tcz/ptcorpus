Chinese actors who were born in 1977

Here are 6 famous actors from China were born in 1977:

Huang Xiaoming

Huang Xiaoming (November 13, 1977 Qingdao-) also known as Xiaoming Huang, China's Number One Heartthrob or Huang Xiao-Ming is a Chinese singer, actor, model and film producer.

Ning Hao

Ning Hao (September 9, 1977 Shanxi-) a.k.a. Hao Ning is a Chinese film director, screenwriter, film producer and actor.

Jian

Jian (March 4, 1977 Guangzhou-) also known as Jian Peng Zhu, Jian Leonardo, Leonardo or Leo is a Chinese actor and model.

Ailei Yu

Ailei Yu (November 29, 1977 Tangshan-) is a Chinese actor.

Peng Bo

Peng Bo (April 14, 1977 Chongqing-) otherwise known as Bo Peng is a Chinese actor.

Sze-Ming Lu

Sze-Ming Lu (May 8, 1977 Taipei-) also known as Johnny Lu Sze-Ming, Johnny Chen, Johnny Lu Si-ming or Johnny Lu is a Chinese actor.
