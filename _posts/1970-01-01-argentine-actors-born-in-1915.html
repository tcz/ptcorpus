Argentine actors who were born in 1915

Here are 5 famous actors from Argentina were born in 1915:

Ángel Magaña

Ángel Magaña (August 24, 1915 Villa Crespo-November 13, 1982 Buenos Aires) was an Argentine actor. He had one child, Julieta Magaña.

Jorge Salcedo

Jorge Salcedo (June 2, 1915 Buenos Aires-April 12, 1988 Buenos Aires) a.k.a. Pedro Codicimo di Paola or Pedro Jorge di Paola Codicimo was an Argentine actor and voice actor.

Fernando Lamas

Fernando Lamas (January 9, 1915 Buenos Aires-October 8, 1982 Los Angeles) also known as Fernando Álvaro Lamas or Fernando Álvaro Lamas y de Santos was an Argentine actor, television director and writer. He had three children, Lorenzo Lamas, Alexandra Lamas and Cristina Lamas.

Oscar Valicelli

Oscar Valicelli (July 1, 1915 Buenos Aires-October 11, 1999 Buenos Aires) was an Argentine actor.

Osvaldo Miranda

Osvaldo Miranda (November 3, 1915 Villa Crespo-April 20, 2011 Almagro, Buenos Aires) was an Argentine actor.
