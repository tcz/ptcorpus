Finnish actors born in 1923

Here are 7 famous actors from Finland were born in 1923:

Martti Pennanen

Martti Pennanen (December 8, 1923 Tervola-December 17, 2010 Tampere) was a Finnish actor.

Olavi Ahonen

Olavi Ahonen (July 21, 1923 Tikkakoski-September 1, 2000 Helsinki) was a Finnish actor.

Osmo Harkimo

Osmo Harkimo (October 7, 1923 Kymi, Finland-April 8, 2007 Sipoo) also known as O. Harkimo or Ossi Harkimo was a Finnish cinematographer, actor and television director. He had one child, Harry Harkimo.

Aarne Tarkas

Aarne Tarkas (December 19, 1923 Pori-October 7, 1976 Spain) also known as Aarne Saastamoinen, Paukku-Arska or Bang-Arska was a Finnish actor, film director, screenwriter and television director.

Sakari Jurkka

Sakari Jurkka (September 16, 1923 Oulu-December 13, 2012 Espoo) otherwise known as Sakari Heikki Jurkka or Heikki Sakari Jurkka was a Finnish actor, film director and television director. He had two children, Timo Jurkka and Mikko Jurkka.

Pehr-Olof Sirén

Pehr-Olof Sirén (January 10, 1923 Porvoo-November 24, 1986) also known as Per Siren, Per-Olof Sirén, Pehr-Olof Alfred Sirén or Pärre was a Finnish actor.

Alpo Vammelvuo

Alpo Vammelvuo (August 26, 1923 Riihimäki-) a.k.a. Alpo Hilpas Vammelvuo is a Finnish actor.
