Thai movie stars born in 1965

Here are 5 famous actors from Thailand were born in 1965:

Mum Jokmok

Mum Jokmok (June 24, 1965 Yasothon Province-) a.k.a. Mum Jok Mok, Perttary Wongkamlao, Mum, Petthai Wongkhamlao, Humlae, Mom Jok Mok, Mum Jokemok, Phetthai Vongkumlao, Phettai Wongkamlao, Mom Jokmok, Phetthai Wongkhamlao, Mam Chokmok or Petchtai Wongkamlao is a Thai screenwriter, film director, actor, comedian, film producer and voice actor. He has two children, Bussarakam Wongkamlao and Paytaai Wongkamlao.

Pakphum Wongjinda

Pakphum Wongjinda (May 13, 1965-) also known as Pakphum Wonjinda or Peurn is a Thai screenwriter, film director and actor.

Sataporn Nakwilairoj

Sataporn Nakwilairoj (January 16, 1965 Chonburi Province-) also known as Sataporn Nakwilai is a Thai model, singer, actor and television director.

Surasak Wongthai

Surasak Wongthai (August 10, 1965-) also known as M is a Thai actor and singer.

Nukkid Boonthong

Nukkid Boonthong (July 18, 1965 Bangkok-) also known as Nueng is a Thai actor. He has one child, Panthorthong Boonthong.
