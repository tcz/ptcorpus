Indian movie stars born in 1966

Here are 8 famous actresses from India were born in 1966:

Shobana

Shobana (March 21, 1966 Thiruvananthapuram-) a.k.a. Shobhana Chandrakumar, Shobhana, Baby Sobhana, Shobana Chandrakumar or Shobana Chandrakumar Pillai is an Indian actor, dancer, choreographer and musician. She has one child, Narayani Chandrakumar.

Bhanupriya

Bhanupriya (January 15, 1966 Rangampeta-) a.k.a. Beauty Queen Bhanupriya, Bhanu Priya, Manga Bham, Manga Bhama or the 'other Sridevi' is an Indian actor, model and dancer. She has one child, Abhinaya.

Revathi

Revathi (July 8, 1966 Kochi-) also known as Asha Kelunni Kutty, Revathy, Revathy Menon, Revathi Menon, Revati, Asha Kelunni, Asha or Ms. Revathi is an Indian actor, film director, social worker, dancer and television producer.

Vijayashanti

Radha

Radha (June 3, 1966 Kilimanoor-) also known as Udaya Chandrika, Radha Devi, Glamour Queen Radha, Radha Nair, Chandrika or Chandrika Rama Kurup Nair is an Indian actor. She has three children, Karthika Nair, Vignesh Nair and Thulasi Nair.

Renuka Shahane

Renuka Shahane (October 7, 1966 Maharashtra-) is an Indian presenter and actor. She has two children, Shouryaman Rana and Satyendra Rana.

Priya Dutt

Priya Dutt (August 28, 1966 Mumbai-) also known as Priya Dutt Roncon is an Indian politician and actor. She has two children, Sumair Roncon and Siddharth Roncon.
