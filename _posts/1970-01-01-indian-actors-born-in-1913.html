Indian movie stars born in 1913

Here are 14 famous actors from India were born in 1913:

Balraj Sahni

Balraj Sahni (May 1, 1913 Rawalpindi-April 13, 1973 Mumbai) also known as Yudhishthir Sahni, Balraj Sahani, Balraj, Shri Balraj Sahni or Sahni was an Indian actor, writer and teacher. He had two children, Parikshit Sahni and Shabnam Sahni.

Bhagwan Dada

Bhagwan Dada (August 1, 1913 Mumbai-February 4, 2002 Dadar) a.k.a. Bhagwan Abhaji Palav, Master Bhagwan, Bhagwan, Bhagwandada or Bhagwan Dada was an Indian film director, actor, film producer, writer and screenwriter.

Ustad Bismillah Khan

Ustad Bismillah Khan (March 21, 1913 Dumraon-August 21, 2006 Varanasi) also known as Bismillah Khan or Qamaruddin Khan was an Indian musician and actor. He had one child, Soma Ghosh.

Gope

Gope (April 11, 1913 Hyderabad-November 4, 2014 India) was an Indian actor.

Najam Naqvi

Najam Naqvi (April 11, 1913 Moradabad-January 26, 1982 Lahore) a.k.a. Anwar, Hasan Naqvi, Najmul Hasan, Syed Najmul, Najam H. Naqvi, Najmul Hassan Naqvi, Najamul Hassan or Syed Najmul Hasan Naqvi was an Indian film director and actor.

Tripathi S.N.

Tripathi S.N. (March 14, 1913 Varanasi-March 28, 1988 Mumbai) also known as S.N. Tripathi, Shri Nath Tripathi or Tripathi S was an Indian film score composer, actor, film director, screenwriter and writer.

Balakrishna

Balakrishna (November 2, 1913 Arsikere-July 19, 1995 Bangalore) also known as Balanna, Hasya Brahma Balakrishna, Hasya Brahma, Balkrishna, T.N. Balkrishana, T. N. Balakrishna or Balakrishna was an Indian actor and film director. He had one child, Ganesh Balakrishna.

Datta Dharmadhikari

Datta Dharmadhikari (December 2, 1913 Kolhapur-December 30, 1982 Pune) was an Indian film director and actor. He had one child, Alhaad.

Rajnath

Rajnath (October 22, 1913 Lahore-May 25, 1992 Mumbai) also known as Raj Nath, Raju, Ramlal, Rajnath Singh or Ramlal Melaram Arora was an Indian actor.

Devan

Devan (September 8, 1913 Thiruvidaimarudur-May 5, 1957 Chennai) also known as R. Mahadevan was an Indian writer and actor.

C. H. Narayana Rao

C. H. Narayana Rao (September 13, 1913 Karnataka-February 14, 1984 Chennai) also known as Narayana Rao C.H. was an Indian actor.

Asit Baran

Asit Baran (November 19, 1913 Kolkata-November 27, 1984) otherwise known as Asitbaran or Kalo was an Indian actor.

Majnu

Majnu (November 2, 1913 Amritsar-) is an Indian actor and film director.

S.V. Sahasranamam

S.V. Sahasranamam (November 29, 1913 Coimbatore-) otherwise known as S. V. Sahasranamam is an Indian actor, film director and theatre director. He has one child, S. V. S. Kumar.
