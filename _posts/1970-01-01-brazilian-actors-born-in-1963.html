Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1963

Here are 7 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1963:

Walter Casagrande

Walter Casagrande (April 15, 1963 São Paulo-) also known as Wálter Casagrande Jr., Casagrande, Wálter Casagrande or Casão is a Brazilian football player, commentator and actor. His child is called Ugo Casagrande.

Marcos Palmeira

Marcos Palmeira (August 19, 1963 Rio de Janeiro-) also known as Marcos Palmeira de Paula or Marcos Viana is a Brazilian actor and film producer. He has one child, Júlia Palmeira.

Alexandre Frota

Alexandre Frota (October 14, 1963 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Alexandre Frota de Andrade is a Brazilian actor, model, presenter, tv personality, american football player and pornographic film actor. His child is called Mayã Gondim Frota de Andrade.

Nelson Diniz

Nelson Diniz (July 23, 1963 Porto Alegre-) a.k.a. Nélson Diniz is a Brazilian actor.

Ilya São Paulo

Ilya São Paulo (September 13, 1963 Feira de Santana-) also known as Ilya Flaherty Santana São Paulo or Illy Flaherpy Santana São Paulo is a Brazilian actor.

Roberto Bomtempo

Roberto Bomtempo (May 27, 1963 Rio de Janeiro-) also known as Roberto Bomtempo de Castro Júnior is a Brazilian television director, actor, film director, theatre director and teacher. He has two children, Joana Bomtempo and Miguel Bomtempo.

William Bonner

William Bonner (November 16, 1963 Ribeirão Preto-) also known as William Bonemer Júnior is a Brazilian presenter, journalist, publicist, actor, television editor and television producer. He has three children, Laura Bonner, Beatriz Bonner and Vinícius Bonner.
