Indian movie stars born in 1964

Here are 10 famous actresses from India were born in 1964:

Sonu Walia

Sonu Walia (February 19, 1964 New Delhi-) a.k.a. Sonu Waliya, Sonu Pratap Walia, Sonia Waliya, Sonia Prakash Sharma, Sonu Valia or Sonia Walia is an Indian actor and model.

Zeba Bakhtiar

Zeba Bakhtiar (November 5, 1964 Quetta-) also known as Zeba is an Indian actor and television director. She has one child, Azaan Sami Khan.

Sudha Chandran

Sudha Chandran (September 21, 1964 Kannur-) otherwise known as Sudha, Mayuri Sudha or Mayuri girl is an Indian actor and dancer.

Shanthi Krishna

Shanthi Krishna (January 2, 1964 Mumbai-) otherwise known as Shanti Krisha, Shanti Krishna, Santhi Krishna or Shantikrishna is an Indian actor and dancer. She has two children, Mithul and Mithali.

Navni Parihar

Navni Parihar (September 23, 1964 India-) also known as Navni Perihar, Navani Parihar or Navnee Parihar is an Indian actor.

Sumathi

Sumathi (August 19, 1964 Madurai-) also known as Baby Sumathi, Baby Sumati or Sumi is an Indian nurse and actor.

Ponnamma Babu

Ponnamma Babu (January 26, 1964 Palai-) is an Indian actor. She has three children, Pinki, Deepthi Nirmala and Mathew Damien.

Zeenath

Zeenath (December 29, 1964 Nilambur-) also known as Zeenat or Zeenath A. P. is an Indian actor. Her children are called Jithin Muhammed and Nithin Anil.

Rajalakshmi

Rajalakshmi (December 18, 1964 Tenali-) also known as Raja Lakshmi, Rajalakshmi, Rajyalakshmi, Rajya Lakshmi or Rajalakhsmi is an Indian actor. Her children are called Rohit Krishnan and Rahul Krishnan.

Mamta Kaash

Mamta Kaash (March 29, 1964-) also known as Mamta Kash is an Indian actor and film producer.
