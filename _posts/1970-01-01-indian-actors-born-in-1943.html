Indian movie stars born in 1943

Here are 7 famous actors from India were born in 1943:

Vijayakumar

Vijayakumar (August 29, 1943 Nattuchalai-) also known as Vijayakumar Seemaandurai, Vijaykumar, Panchatcharam, Panchaksharam Rangasamy or Vijaya Kumar is an Indian actor and politician. He has seven children, Sridevi Vijaykumar, Vanitha Vijaykumar, Arun Vijay, Anitha Vijayakumar, Kavitha Vijayakumar, Preetha Vijayakumar and Vanitha Vijayakumar.

Krishna

Krishna (May 31, 1943 Burripalem-) also known as Ghattamaneni Sivarama Krishna Murthy, Gnantamneni Krishna, Ghantamneni Sivarama Krishna, Super Star, Ghantamneni Krishna, Krishna SuperStar, Krishna Ghattamaneni, Ghattamaneni Siva Rama Krishna, Ghattamaneni Krishna, Superstar Krishna, Nata Shekara, Nata Shekhar, Super Star Krishna, Siva Rama Krishna Ghattamaneni or Krishna is an Indian actor, film director, screenwriter, film editor and film producer. He has six children, Mahesh Babu, Manjula Ghattamaneni, Padmavathi Ghattamaneni, Priyadarshini Ghattamaneni, Ramesh Babu and Naresh.

Giri Babu

Giri Babu (June 8, 1943 Ravinuthala-) also known as Seshagiri Rao Yerra, Yerra Seshagiri Rao or Giribabu is an Indian actor, film director, comedian and film producer. His children are called Raghu Babu, Bose Babu and Madhavi.

Ilaiyaraaja

Ilaiyaraaja (June 2, 1943 Pannaipuram-) a.k.a. Ilayaraaja, Ilayaraja, Illayaraja, Illaiyaraja, Daniel Rajayya, Maestro Ilaiya Raaja, Isaignani Ilaiyaraaja, Illaya Raja, Ilaiya Raaja, Raasaiya, Gnanadesikan, Raja, Raajayya, Rajaiya, Raasayya, Raaja, The Maestro, Ilaaiyaraaja, Ilaiyaraja, Illayiraja, Daniel Rasaiah, King, Ilaiyaraaja, Ilaya Raja, Ilaiyaraaja, Raja or Isaignani is an Indian singer, lyricist, conductor, songwriter, film producer, film score composer, music director, musician, multi-instrumentalist, music artist, composer, playback singer, writer and actor. He has three children, Yuvan Shankar Raja, Karthik Raja and Bhavatharini.

Prem Prakash

Prem Prakash (July 19, 1943 Kochi-) also known as Prem is an Indian actor, film producer, playback singer and television producer. He has three children, Bobby, Prakash Thankam and Prakash Sanjay.

Nikhil Baran Sengupta

Nikhil Baran Sengupta (December 13, 1943 Chittagong-February 18, 2014 Bhubaneswar) also known as Nikhilbaran Sengupta, Nikhil Baran Sen Gupta or Nikhilbaran Sen Gupta was an Indian film art director, art director, actor, production designer, illustrator, visual artist and painter. He had two children, Neel Sengupta and Biman Sengupta.

Manikka Vinayagam

Manikka Vinayagam (December 10, 1943 Mayiladuthurai-) a.k.a. Manicka R. Vinayagam, Manicka Vinayagam or Vazhuvoor Manikka Vinayagam Ramaiya Pillai is an Indian actor and playback singer.
