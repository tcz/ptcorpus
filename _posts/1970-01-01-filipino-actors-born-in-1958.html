Filipino actors who were born in 1958

Here are 6 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1958:

Lav Diaz

Lav Diaz (December 30, 1958 Datu Paglas-) also known as Lavrente Indico Diaz, Laurente Diaz or Lavindico Diaz is a Filipino film director, film producer, actor, film editor, screenwriter, cinematographer, film score composer and writer.

Allan K.

Allan K. (December 13, 1958 Bacolod-) also known as Allan Jovenes Quilantang, Allan Quilantang, Pambansang Ilong Ng Pilipinas or A.K. is a Filipino actor.

Lope V. Juban Jr.

Lope V. Juban Jr. (April 15, 1958 Philippines-) a.k.a. Lope Juban, Lope Juban Jr., Jun, Lope V. Juban or Lopezinio Valencia Juban Jr. is a Filipino film producer, television producer and actor. He has four children, Nikolo Juban, Poksi Juban, Stefano Juban and Schinina Juban.

Julio Diaz

Julio Diaz (November 18, 1958 Manila-) is a Filipino actor.

Isko Salvador

Isko Salvador (May 21, 1958-) also known as Isko 'Brod Pete' Salvador or Brod Pete is a Filipino actor and screenwriter.

Dagul

Dagul (October 5, 1958 Victorias-) also known as Dagul, Romeo Pastrana or Romy 'Dagul' Pastrana is a Filipino actor and comedian.
