Indonesian movie actresses born in the year 1963

Here are 7 famous actresses from Indonesia were born in 1963:

Dorce Gamalama

Dorce Gamalama (July 21, 1963 Solok-) is an Indonesian singer-songwriter, comedian, presenter and actor.

Dewi Irawan

Dewi Irawan (June 13, 1963 Indonesia-) is an Indonesian actor.

Lydia Kandou

Lydia Kandou (February 21, 1963 Jakarta-) also known as Lydia Ruth Elizabeth Kandou or Lidya Kandow is an Indonesian actor and model. She has four children, Naysila Mirdad, Nana Mirdad, Kenang Kana and Nathana Ghaza.

Debby Sahertian

Debby Sahertian (April 5, 1963 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor, presenter and film director.

Uci Bing Slamet

Uci Bing Slamet (February 8, 1963 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor and singer. She has one child, Galih Satria Permadi.

Itje Trisnawati

Itje Trisnawati (March 27, 1963 Tasikmalaya-) is an Indonesian actor and singer. She has two children, Eka Sutrisna and Ety Suryawati.

Ida Iasha

Ida Iasha (May 14, 1963 Zwijndrecht, Netherlands-) also known as Ida Albertina van Suchtelen van de Haere or Ida Aisyah is an Indonesian actor. Her children are called Rendy Syahputra, Baskara Syahputra, Remyzard Syahputra and Badra Syahputra.
