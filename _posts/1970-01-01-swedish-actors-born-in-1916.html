Swedish actors who were born in 1916

Here are 11 famous actors from Sweden were born in 1916:

Georg Årlin

Georg Årlin (December 30, 1916 Rödeby-June 27, 1992 Lövestad) also known as Georg Adolf Wilhelm Årlin, Georg Görlin or Georg Åhrlin was a Swedish actor.

George Fant

George Fant (July 11, 1916 Stockholm-February 21, 1998 Stockholm) otherwise known as George Frederick Michael Fant or Georg Fant was a Swedish actor. He had one child, Christer Fant.

Rune Lindström

Rune Lindström (April 28, 1916 Västanfors-April 25, 1973 Leksand) was a Swedish actor, screenwriter, poet and film director.

Claes Thelander

Claes Thelander (January 14, 1916 Helsingborg-December 12, 1999 Bromma) also known as Klas Fredrik Vilhelm Thelander was a Swedish actor.

Karl-Birger Blomdahl

Karl-Birger Blomdahl (October 19, 1916 Växjö-June 14, 1968 Kungsängen) was a Swedish actor, film score composer, composer and conductor.

Sture Sjöstedt

Sture Sjöstedt (May 8, 1916 Stockholm-July 5, 2008) a.k.a. Nils Sture Sjöstedt, Nils Sture Carl Gustaf Sjöstedt or Sture N. Sjostedt was a Swedish actor, film producer and cinematographer. He had one child, Per Sjostedt.

Anders Ek

Anders Ek (April 7, 1916 Gothenburg-November 17, 1979 Stockholm) was a Swedish actor. He had seven children, Mats Ek, Malin Ek, Niklas Ek, Johan Ek, Marion Ek, Maria Ek and Judit Ek.

Gunnar 'Knas' Lindkvist

Gunnar 'Knas' Lindkvist (August 24, 1916 Stockholm-May 27, 1990 Stockholm) also known as Gunnar Knas, Gunnar Knas Lindkvist, Gunnar Lindkvist or Gunnar Lindqvist was a Swedish actor.

Gunnar Biörck

Gunnar Biörck (April 4, 1916 Gothenburg-October 28, 1996) was a Swedish actor.

Henry Nyberg

Henry Nyberg (September 16, 1916 Stockholm-October 14, 2004 Stockholm) also known as Henry 'Nypan' Nyberg or Henry Gustaf Vilhelm Nyberg was a Swedish actor.

Bertilo

Bertilo (March 4, 1916 Stockholm County-October 9, 1985 Stockholm) was a Swedish actor.
