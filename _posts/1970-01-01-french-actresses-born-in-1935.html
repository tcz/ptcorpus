French actresses who were born in 1935

Here are 10 famous actresses from France were born in 1935:

Mylène Demongeot

Mylène Demongeot (September 29, 1935 Nice-) a.k.a. Marie-Hélène Demongeot, Mylène Nicole, Myléne Demongeot, M.H. Demongeot, Mylène-Nicole Demongeot, Mylene Nicole, Mylene Demongeot, Marie-Helene Demongeot or Marielle Demongeot is a French actor and writer.

Isabelle Collin Dufresne

Isabelle Collin Dufresne (September 6, 1935 La Tronche-June 14, 2014 Manhattan) also known as Isabelle Collin Dufresne, Isabelle Dufresne or Ultra Violet was a French actor and author.

Claudine Berg

Claudine Berg (September 19, 1935 Paris-) also known as Claudine Weisberg is a French actor.

Karen Blanguernon

Karen Blanguernon (October 24, 1935 Paris-November 22, 1996 New York) was a French actor. She had one child, Leslie Bedos.

Irène Tunc

Irène Tunc (September 25, 1935 Lille-January 16, 1972 Versailles) was a French actor and model.

Claude Godard

Claude Godard (June 21, 1935 Castets-) is a French actor.

Yvonne Constant

Yvonne Constant (June 15, 1935 Paris-) is a French actor, ballet dancer and singer.

Andréa Parisy

Andréa Parisy (December 4, 1935 Levallois-Perret-April 27, 2014 France) also known as Andrée Parizy, Andrée Marcelle Henriette Parisy, Andrea Parisy, Andrée Pariz or Andrée Marcelle Henriette Parizy was a French actor.

Odile Poisson

Odile Poisson (March 4, 1935 Nantes-) is a French actor.

Anne-Marie Peysson

Anne-Marie Peysson (July 24, 1935 Saint-Disdier-) is a French actor, journalist, author and television presenter. Her child is called Jean-Pierre Falloux.
