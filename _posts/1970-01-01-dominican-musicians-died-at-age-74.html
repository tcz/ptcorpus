Morales Troncoso served as the vice president of the Dominican Republic from 1996 until 2000, under the presidency of Leonel Fernández. He was also a senator for the National District and served as Minister of Foreign Affairs for over a decade, from 2004 until his death in 2014. Troncoso played a significant role in the strengthening of diplomatic ties between the Dominican Republic and other countries, particularly in Latin America and the Caribbean.
