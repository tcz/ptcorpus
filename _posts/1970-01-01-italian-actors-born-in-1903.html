Italian movie actors born in the year 1903

Here are 9 famous actors from Italy were born in 1903:

Dino Diluca

Dino Diluca (May 5, 1903 Livorno-May 11, 1991 United States of America) also known as Dino Di Luca or Guido Di Luca was an Italian actor.

Mino Doro

Mino Doro (May 6, 1903 Venice-May 12, 2006 Bellagio, Lombardy) a.k.a. M. Doro, Ermino Doro di Costa di Vernassino or Mino D'Oro was an Italian actor.

Fausto Guerzoni

Fausto Guerzoni (January 13, 1903 Nonantola-June 1, 1967 Rome) was an Italian actor.

Peppino De Filippo

Peppino De Filippo (August 24, 1903 Naples-January 27, 1980 Rome) also known as Giuseppe De Filippo was an Italian actor, screenwriter, playwright and film producer. His child is called Luigi De Filippo.

Ermanno Roveri

Ermanno Roveri (October 5, 1903 Milan-December 27, 1968 Milan) was an Italian actor.

Ronald R. Rondell

Ronald R. Rondell (September 21, 1903 Naples-May 14, 1999 Woodland Hills) also known as Ronnie Rondell, Ronald Rondell, Ronnie R. Rondell, Ronny Rondell or Ron Rondell was an Italian actor. He had one child, Ronnie Rondell Jr..

Eduardo Passarelli

Eduardo Passarelli (July 20, 1903 Naples-December 9, 1968 Naples) also known as Eduardo De Filippo, Edoardo Passarelli or Passarelli was an Italian actor.

Guido Morisi

Guido Morisi (February 8, 1903 Bologna-January 4, 1951 Rome) also known as Enrico Morisi was an Italian actor.

Dino Di Luca

Dino Di Luca (May 5, 1903 Livorno-) also known as Guido Di Luca is an Italian actor.
