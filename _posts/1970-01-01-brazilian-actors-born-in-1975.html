Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1975

Here are 15 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1975:

Anderson Silva

Anderson Silva (April 14, 1975 São Paulo-) otherwise known as The Spider, The Dancing Spider, Spider Man or Anderson da Silva is a Brazilian mixed martial artist and actor. He has five children, Kalyl Silva, Joao Vitor Silva, Kaory Silva, Gabriel Silva and Kauana Silva.

Hélio Castroneves

Hélio Castroneves (May 10, 1975 Ribeirão Preto-) otherwise known as Helio Castroneves or Hélio Castro Neves is a Brazilian race car driver and actor.

Rodrigo Santoro

Rodrigo Santoro (August 22, 1975 Petrópolis-) a.k.a. Rodrigo Junqueira dos Reis Santoro or Rodrigo Junqueira Reis dos Santos is a Brazilian actor and voice actor.

Jair Oliveira

Jair Oliveira (March 17, 1975 São Paulo-) also known as Jairzinho Oliveira, Jair Oliviera, Jair Rodrigues M. Oliveira, Jairzinho or Jair de Oliveira is a Brazilian singer, actor, film score composer and composer. He has two children, Laura Oliveira and Isabella Oliveira.

Rod Carvalho

Rod Carvalho (January 6, 1975 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Rodrigo Fonseca or Rodrigo Carvalho Fonseca is a Brazilian actor and screenwriter.

Danton Mello

Danton Mello (May 27, 1975 Passos, Minas Gerais-) also known as Danton Figueiredo Mello is a Brazilian actor. He has two children, Luísa Mello and Alice Mello.

Alexandre Chen

Alexandre Chen (July 15, 1975 São Paulo-) a.k.a. Alexander Chen or Alex Chen is a Brazilian actor, cinematographer and film producer.

Douglas Barcellos

Douglas Barcellos (March 5, 1975 Rio de Janeiro-December 24, 2008 Cascais) was a Brazilian actor and model.

Criolo

Criolo (September 5, 1975 São Paulo-) otherwise known as Kleber Cavalcante Gomes is a Brazilian actor, film score composer, singer-songwriter and rapper.

André Gonçalves

André Gonçalves (November 16, 1975 Natal-) also known as André Gonçalves Barbosa is a Brazilian actor. He has three children, Manuela Gonçalves, Pedro Arthur Gonçalves and Valentina Gonçalves.

André Frateschi

André Frateschi (March 29, 1975 São Paulo-) also known as Andre Frateschi is a Brazilian actor.

Marat Descartes

Marat Descartes (May 17, 1975 São Paulo-) is a Brazilian actor. He has three children, Anita Descartes, Ligia Descartes and Juliet Descartes .

Ken Chang

Ken Chang (May 8, 1975 Taiwan-) also known as Ken Cheung Chi-Yiu, Ken Chong Chi-Yiu, Chang Tzu-Yao, Chang Chi Yao, Ken Cheung Chi Yun, Chong Chi Yun, Zhang Zhi Yao or Ken Chong is a Brazilian actor.

Rodrigo Penna

Rodrigo Penna (August 31, 1975 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Rodrigo Penna Santos or Rodrigo Penna Santos Pereira is a Brazilian actor.

Ricardo Tozzi
