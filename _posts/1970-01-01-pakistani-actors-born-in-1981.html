Pakistani actors who were born in 1981

Here are 5 famous actors from Pakistan were born in 1981:

Ahsan Khan

Ahsan Khan (October 9, 1981 London-) also known as Ahson Khan is a Pakistani actor, model and presenter.

Fawad Afzal Khan

Fawad Afzal Khan (November 29, 1981 Lahore-) otherwise known as Fawad Afzal Khan, Fawad or Fawad Khan is a Pakistani guitarist, model, actor and singer. He has one child, Ayaan Khan.

Junaid Khan

Junaid Khan (November 2, 1981 Karachi-) is a Pakistani actor and singer-songwriter.

Mikaal Zulfiqar

Mikaal Zulfiqar (June 13, 1981 London-) also known as Mikaal Zulfikar or Meekal Zulfiqar is a Pakistani model and actor.

Goher Mumtaz

Goher Mumtaz (July 27, 1981 Lahore-) also known as Gohar Mumtaz is a Pakistani singer, musician, composer, guitarist, actor and lyricist.
