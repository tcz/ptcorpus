Turkish actors born in 1955

Here are 5 famous actors from Turkey were born in 1955:

Altan Erkekli

Altan Erkekli (January 18, 1955 Istanbul-) also known as Resit Altan Erkekli is a Turkish actor. His children are called Can Erkekli, Efe Erkekli and Ali Erkekli.

Ümit Acar

Ümit Acar (November 3, 1955 Kabataş, Istanbul-) is a Turkish actor and musician. He has one child, Doğa Acar.

Hasan Kaçan

Hasan Kaçan (December 10, 1955 İncesu, Kayseri-) is a Turkish actor, screenwriter and cartoonist.

Mahmut Hekimoğlu

Mahmut Hekimoğlu (October 25, 1955 Adana-) also known as Mahmut Hekimoglu is a Turkish actor.

Ahmet Arıman

Ahmet Arıman (November 27, 1955 Istanbul-) a.k.a. Ahmet Ariman is a Turkish actor.
