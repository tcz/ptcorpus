Serbian movie actors born in the year 1946

Here are 10 famous actors from Serbia were born in 1946:

Milan Gutović

Milan Gutović (August 11, 1946 Umka-) also known as Milan Gutovic, Milan Lane Gutovic, Lane Gutovic or Lane is a Serbian actor. He has one child, Milica Gutovic.

Marko Nikolic

Marko Nikolic (October 20, 1946 Kraljevo-) also known as Марко Николић is a Serbian actor.

Branko Cvejic

Branko Cvejic (August 25, 1946 Belgrade-) also known as Branko Cvejich or Branko Cvejić is a Serbian actor and theatre director.

Ivan Bekjarev

Ivan Bekjarev (April 6, 1946 Belgrade-) also known as Ivan Bekarev or Beki is a Serbian actor and professor.

Miroljub Leso

Miroljub Leso (June 27, 1946 Belgrade-) a.k.a. Leso Miroljub or Miodrag Neso is a Serbian actor.

Branko Milićević

Branko Milićević (April 3, 1946 Zemun-) a.k.a. Branko Kockica or Branislav Milićević is a Serbian actor.

Marinko Sebez

Marinko Sebez (August 27, 1946 Banatski Despotovac-April 2, 1989 Belgrade) was a Serbian actor. He had one child, Boris Sebez.

Branko Baletić

Branko Baletić (June 5, 1946 Belgrade-) also known as Branko Baletic is a Serbian film director, film producer, actor and screenwriter.
