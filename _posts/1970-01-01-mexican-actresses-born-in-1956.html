Mexican actresses who were born in 1956

Here are 5 famous actresses from Mexico were born in 1956:

Adriana Barraza

Adriana Barraza (March 5, 1956 Toluca-) is a Mexican actor, television director and acting coach. She has one child, Carolina Valsagna.

Laura Zapata

Laura Zapata (July 30, 1956 Mexico City-) a.k.a. Laura Guadalupe Zapata Miranda, Laura Guadelupe Sodi Miranda, Laura Guadalupe Zapata or Laura Zapata de Sodi is a Mexican actor. Her children are called Claudio Sodi and Patricio Sodi.

Martha Rueda Terrazas

Martha Rueda Terrazas (November 19, 1956 Mexico City-) also known as Martha Rueda, Martha Terrazas de Rueda or Martha Isabel Terrazas Revilla is a Mexican actor and film producer. She has one child, Rodrigo Rueda Terrazas.

Ana Alicia

Ana Alicia (December 12, 1956 Mexico City-) also known as Ana Alicia Ortiz or Ana-Alicia is a Mexican actor. Her child is called Cathryn Dylan Benz.

Leticia Perdigon

Leticia Perdigon (August 7, 1956 Mexico City-) also known as Leticia Perdigon, Lupe Leticia Perdigón, Guadalupe Leticia Perdigón Labrador or Leticia Perdigón is a Mexican actor.
