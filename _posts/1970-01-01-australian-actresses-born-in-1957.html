Australian movie stars born in 1957

Here are 6 famous actresses from Australia were born in 1957:

Debra Lawrance

Debra Lawrance (January 1, 1957 Melbourne-) a.k.a. Debra Lawrence is an Australian actor. She has two children, Grace Coard and William Coard.

Robyn Gibbes

Robyn Gibbes (January 2, 1957 Goroka-) also known as Robyn Gibs or Robyn Gibbs is an Australian actor.

Penny Cook

Penny Cook (July 13, 1957-) is an Australian actor and presenter. She has one child, Poppy Lynch.

Joanne Samuel

Joanne Samuel (August 5, 1957 Camperdown-) is an Australian actor.

Trisha Goddard

Trisha Goddard (December 23, 1957 Hackney Central-) otherwise known as Patricia "Trisha" Goddard is an Australian presenter, talk show host and actor. She has two children, Madison Greive and Billie Greive.

Anna Simone Scott

Anna Simone Scott (April 9, 1957 Sydney-) otherwise known as Anna Scott, Karin Scott, Karyn Scott or Kerry Anne Scott is an Australian actor, screenwriter and film producer.
