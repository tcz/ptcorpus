Filipino actresses who were born in 1971

Here are 8 famous actresses from Philippines were born in 1971:

Kris Aquino

Kris Aquino (February 14, 1971 Quezon City-) a.k.a. Kristina Bernadette Cojuangco Aquino, Kristina Bernadette Aquino-Yap, Kristina Bernadette C. Aquino, Tetay, Krissy, Philippines' Queen of Talk, Queen of All Media, Kristina Bernadette Aquino, Massacre Queen, Queen of Philippine Game Shows or Kipay ng Bayan is a Filipino presenter, actor, writer, politician, comedian and film producer. Her children are called James Carlos "Bimby" Aquino Yap, Jr and Joshua Aquino Salvador.

Elizabeth Arbilon

Elizabeth Arbilon (August 29, 1971 Philippines-) a.k.a. Noalyn is a Filipino film producer, actor and set decorator.

Cathy Garcia-Molina

Cathy Garcia-Molina (November 28, 1971 Quezon City-) also known as Cathy Molina, Catherine Garcia-Molina, Catherine Molina, Kathy Molina, Catherine R. Garcia, Cathy Garcia, Catherene R. Garcia, Catherine Garcia, Cathy R. Garcia, Catherine Rosales Garcia-Molina or Cathy Rosales Garcia is a Filipino film director, television director and actor.

Eugene Domingo

Eugene Domingo (July 23, 1971 Tondo-) a.k.a. Uge is a Filipino actor, comedian, presenter and performer.

Lotlot De Leon

Lotlot De Leon (February 16, 1971 Philippines-) a.k.a. Lot-lot, LotLot or Charlotte Jean V. De Leon is a Filipino actor. She has four children, Janine Gutierrez, Jessica Gutierrez, Maxine Gutierrez and Diego Gutierrez.

Lea Salonga

Lea Salonga (February 22, 1971 Angeles-) a.k.a. Lea Salonga-Chien, Lea Salonga Chien, Manang, Maria Lea Carmen Imutan Salonga, Lei, Pride of the Philippines or Tata is a Filipino singer, actor and voice actor. She has one child, Nicole Beverly Chien.

Syren

Syren (May 21, 1971 Philippines-) also known as Siren or Corina Millado is a Filipino pornographic film actor and actor.

Kristina Paner

Kristina Paner (August 1, 1971 Manila-) also known as Christina Paner, Cristina Paner or Tina Paner is a Filipino singer and actor.
