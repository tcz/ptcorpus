Polish movie actors born in the year 1938

Here are 10 famous actors from Poland were born in 1938:

Jerzy Skolimowski

Jerzy Skolimowski (May 5, 1938 Łódź-) is a Polish screenwriter, film director, actor, playwright, film producer, author, painter and film art director. He has two children, Michael Lyndon and Józef Skolimowski.

Zygmunt Malanowicz

Zygmunt Malanowicz (February 4, 1938 Vilnius County-) is a Polish actor, screenwriter and film director.

Bohdan Lazuka

Bohdan Lazuka (October 31, 1938 Lublin-) also known as Bohdan Łazuka or Łazuka, Bohdan is a Polish actor and singer. His children are called Adam Łazuka and Olga Łazuka.

Janusz Dymek

Janusz Dymek (February 1, 1938 Knurów-) is a Polish film director, screenwriter, actor and television director.

Jerzy Kamas

Jerzy Kamas (July 8, 1938 Łódź-) is a Polish actor. He has two children, Karol Kamas and Paweł Kamas.

Feridun Erol

Feridun Erol (December 29, 1938 Włodawa-) is a Polish television director, screenwriter and actor.

Andrzej Nowakowski

Andrzej Nowakowski (November 8, 1938 Warsaw-June 26, 1986 Poland) was a Polish actor.

Marek Piestrak

Marek Piestrak (March 31, 1938 Kraków-) is a Polish film director, actor and screenwriter.

Piotr Janczerski

Piotr Janczerski (September 8, 1938 Łódź-) is a Polish musician, actor and songwriter.

Tadeusz Kwinta

Tadeusz Kwinta (October 18, 1938 Kraków-) is a Polish actor. He has one child, Dorota Kwinta.
