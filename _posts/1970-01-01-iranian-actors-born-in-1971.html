Iranian actors born in 1971

Here are 6 famous actors from Iran were born in 1971:

Farshad Kholghi

Farshad Kholghi (May 17, 1971 Tehran-) also known as Farshad Kholgi or Farshad Kolgi is an Iranian actor.

Peyman Moaadi

Peyman Moaadi (March 5, 1971 New York City-) otherwise known as Peyman Moadi, Payman Maadi, Peman Moaadi or Peiman Ma'adi is an Iranian actor, screenwriter, costume designer and film director. He has two children, Atena Maadi and Baran Maadi.

Mansour

Mansour (July 28, 1971 Tehran-) also known as Mansour Jafari Mamaghaani is an Iranian singer, composer, actor and fashion designer.

Rambod Javan

Rambod Javan (December 22, 1971 Tehran-) is an Iranian actor, film director, tv journalist and journalist.

Saeed Aghakhani

Saeed Aghakhani (May 10, 1971 Bijar-) is an Iranian actor, television director and film director.

Koorosh Tahami

Koorosh Tahami (April 24, 1971 Tehran-) is an Iranian actor.
