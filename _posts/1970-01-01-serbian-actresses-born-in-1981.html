Serbian movie actresses born in the year 1981

Here are 6 famous actresses from Serbia were born in 1981:

Bojana Novakovic

Bojana Novakovic (November 17, 1981 Belgrade-) also known as Boyana Novakovich, Бојана Новаковић or Bonjana Novakovic is a Serbian actor.

Sloboda Micalovic

Sloboda Micalovic (August 21, 1981 Leskovac-) also known as Boba, Bobana, Sloboda Mićalović, Sloboda Mićalović Ćetković, Sloboda Mićalović-Ćetković, Sloboda Micalovic Cetkovic or Sloboda Micalovic-Cetkovic is a Serbian actor. Her children are called Vera Ćetković and Mila Ćetković.

Jovana Tirnanic

Jovana Tirnanic (November 30, 1981 Belgrade-) is a Serbian actor.

Bojana Bambic

Bojana Bambic (October 10, 1981 Belgrade-) is a Serbian actor.

Ana Franic

Ana Franic (October 7, 1981 Belgrade-) a.k.a. Franceska is a Serbian actor.

Seka Aleksić

Seka Aleksić (April 23, 1981 Zvornik-) also known as Seka Aleksic, Svetlana Aleksić, Seka or Svetlana Aleksic is a Serbian singer, writer and actor.
