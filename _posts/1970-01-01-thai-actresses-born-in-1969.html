Thai movie stars born in 1969

Here are 6 famous actresses from Thailand were born in 1969:

Mai Charoenpura

Mai Charoenpura (January 5, 1969 Bangkok-) a.k.a. Siriwimol Charoenpura, Siriwimol "Mai" Charoenpura, Queen of Pop Rock or Siriwimon Charoenpura is a Thai singer and actor.

Passorn Boonyakiart

Passorn Boonyakiart (February 8, 1969-) a.k.a. Honey, ภัสสร บุณยเกียรติ or ฮันนี่ is a Thai actor, singer and model.

Satawat Donlayawijid

Satawat Donlayawijid (April 8, 1969 Lamphun Province-) a.k.a. Satawat Dunyawichit or Satawat Dulayawejit is a Thai actor. Her child is called Parkin Jikitsinlapin.

Jintara Poonlarp

Jintara Poonlarp (March 6, 1969 Kaset Wisai District-) also known as Tongbai Janlueang, Chintara Phunlap or Jin is a Thai actor and singer.

Kokkorn Benjathikoon

Kokkorn Benjathikoon (November 6, 1969 Thailand-) a.k.a. Coco is a Thai actor.

Papassara Techapaibun

Papassara Techapaibun (November 24, 1969 Suphan Buri Province-) a.k.a. Papassara Chutanupong, Kob, Taveeporn Hoonsil or Papassara Techapaiboon is a Thai actor. She has one child, Disaraya Techapaibun.
