Czech movie actors born in the year 1945

Here are 6 famous actors from Czech Republic were born in 1945:

John Bok

John Bok (October 24, 1945 Pardubice-) is a Czech political activist and actor. His children are called Kristýna Boková and Jenovéfa Boková.

Michal Pavlata

Michal Pavlata (June 15, 1945 Kvasice-) is a Czech actor, author and journalist.

Ladislav Potměšil

Ladislav Potměšil (September 2, 1945 Prague-) is a Czech actor and voice actor. He has three children, Marie Potměšilová, Ladislav Potměšil and Jan Potměšil.

Karel Šíp

Karel Šíp (June 1, 1945 Prague-) is a Czech actor. He has two children, Karel Šíp and Jan Šíp.

Richard Tesařík

Richard Tesařík (December 25, 1945 Ostrava-) is a Czech actor and musician. His child is called Štěpán Tesařík.

Jaroslav Uhlíř

Jaroslav Uhlíř (September 14, 1945 Prague-) a.k.a. Uhlíř, Jaroslav or Jaroslav Uhlír is a Czech composer and actor. He has one child, Eliška Uhlířová.
