Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1949

Here are 8 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1949:

Slamet Rahardjo Djarot

Slamet Rahardjo Djarot (January 21, 1949 Serang-) also known as Slamet Rahardjo or Memet is an Indonesian film director, actor and screenwriter. He has two children, Laras Rahardjo Djarot and Kasih Rahardjo Djarot.

Eric Beekes

Eric Beekes (May 15, 1949 Surabaya-) a.k.a. Eric-Jan Beekes, Eric Jan Beekes or Erik Beekes is an Indonesian actor.

El Manik

El Manik (November 17, 1949 Bohorok-) a.k.a. Iman Ginting Manik or Emmanuel Ginting Manik is an Indonesian actor.

Nano Riantiarno

Nano Riantiarno (June 6, 1949 Cirebon-) a.k.a. Norbertus Riantiarno or N. Riantiarno is an Indonesian actor, theatre director, screenwriter and playwright.

Eddy Bakar Pare

Eddy Bakar Pare (October 10, 1949 Pare-Pare-) also known as Husein Bakar is an Indonesian actor.

Wawan Wanisar

Wawan Wanisar (December 13, 1949 Jakarta-) also known as Wawan Sarwani is an Indonesian actor.

George Kamarullah

George Kamarullah (July 30, 1949 Ambon, Maluku-) is an Indonesian actor, cinematographer and film editor.

Soultan Saladin

Soultan Saladin (October 21, 1949 Pematangsiantar-) is an Indonesian actor.
