Dutch actors who were born in 1966

Here are 8 famous actors from Netherlands were born in 1966:

Marco Borsato

Marco Borsato (December 21, 1966 Alkmaar-) also known as Marco Roberto Borsato or Borsato, Marco is a Dutch musician, singer and actor. He has three children, Jada Borsato, Senna Borsato and Luca Borsato.

Antonie Kamerling

Antonie Kamerling (August 25, 1966 Arnhem-October 6, 2010 Zevenhoven) otherwise known as Anthonie Willem Constantijn Gneomar Kamerling, Hero, Anthone Kamerling, Antonie, Anthonie Kamerling or Antonie Kamerling was a Dutch actor and musician. He had two children, Merlijn Kamerling and Vlinder Kamerling.

Theo Maassen

Theo Maassen (December 8, 1966 Oegstgeest-) otherwise known as Theo Maasen, Theodorus Wilhelmus Maassen, Maassen, Theo or Theodorus Wilhelmus (Theo) Maassen is a Dutch actor, comedian and screenwriter. His child is called Ruby Maassen.

Jasper Faber

Jasper Faber (May 17, 1966 Amsterdam-) is a Dutch actor.

Martijn Nieuwerf

Martijn Nieuwerf (December 10, 1966 Netherlands-) is a Dutch actor. He has two children, Hendrikje Nieuwerf and Kees Nieuwerf.

Fons Merkies

Fons Merkies (June 7, 1966 Zaandam-) is a Dutch film score composer, actor and composer. He has four children, Java Siegertsz, Redmar Siegertsz, Mayte Siegertsz and Tess Siegertsz.

Jorgen de Mey

Jorgen de Mey (June 20, 1966 Rotterdam-) is a Dutch actor.

Martijn Bosman

Martijn Bosman (October 8, 1966-) otherwise known as Bosman, Martijn is a Dutch actor, drummer, singer, record producer, disc jockey and presenter.
