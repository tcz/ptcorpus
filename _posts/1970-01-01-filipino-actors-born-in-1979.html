Filipino actors who were born in 1979

Mark Anthony Fernandez

Mark Anthony Fernandez (January 16, 1979 Manila-) also known as Mark Anthony Lacsamana Fernandez, M.A. Fernandez or Mark Anthony Moreno Fernandez is a Filipino actor and model.

Marvin Agustin

Jericho Rosales

Marc Abaya

Marc Abaya (November 6, 1979 Philippines-) also known as Ramon Marcelino Diaz Abaya or Ramon Marcelino "Marc" Diaz-Abaya is a Filipino singer, guitarist, actor, presenter and vj.

Neil Ryan Sese

Neil Ryan Sese (April 26, 1979 Lucena, Philippines-) a.k.a. Neil Sese is a Filipino actor.

Gino Paul Guzman
