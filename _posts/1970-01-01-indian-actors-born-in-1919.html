Indian movie stars born in 1919

Here are 7 famous actors from India were born in 1919:

Gajanan Digambar Madgulkar

Gajanan Digambar Madgulkar (October 1, 1919 Madgule-December 14, 1977) a.k.a. Ga Di Ma, GaDiMā, Gajānan Digambar Mādgulkar or G. D. Mādgulkar was an Indian screenwriter, actor and writer.

Om Prakash

Om Prakash (December 19, 1919 Jammu-February 21, 1998 Mumbai) also known as Om Prakash Bakshi, Om Parkash, Omprakash, Om Prakash Chibber or Fateh Din was an Indian actor.

M. N. Nambiar

M. N. Nambiar (March 7, 1919 Kannur-November 19, 2008 Chennai) also known as Manjeri Narayanan Nambiar, M.N. Nambiyar, Maha Guruswamy, Nambiar swami, Maha Guruswami, Nambiar Guruswami, Nambiar or M.N.Nambiar was an Indian actor. He had three children, Sukumar Nambiar, Mohan Nambiar and Sneha Nambiar.

Mahipal

Mahipal (November 11, 1919 Jodhpur-May 15, 2005 Mumbai) also known as Mahipal Singh was an Indian actor.

V. Dakshinamoorthy

V. Dakshinamoorthy (December 9, 1919 Alappuzha-August 2, 2013 Chennai) also known as Dakshinamoorthy, Dakshnamurti, Venkateshwara, Venkateswaran Dakshinamoorthy, Swamy, Venkateshwara Dakshinamoorthy or Sangeetha Saraswathy V. Dakshinamoorthy was an Indian singer, musician, film score composer and actor.

Jahor Roy

Jahor Roy (September 19, 1919 Barisal-August 11, 1977 Kolkata) a.k.a. Jahar Roy, Jahor Roy, Shri Jahar Roy or Jahar Ray was an Indian actor and comedian.

Purushottam Laxman Deshpande

Purushottam Laxman Deshpande (November 8, 1919 Mumbai-June 12, 2000 Pune) otherwise known as पुरुषोत्तम लक्ष्मण देशपांडे, पु. ल. or Pu. La. was an Indian film director, actor and writer.
