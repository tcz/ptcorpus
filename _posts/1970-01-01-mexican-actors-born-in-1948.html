He has three children, José Francisco Ortíz Noreña, Sara Ortíz Salazar and Marysol Estrella Ortíz Noreña.

Lance Hool (May 11, 1948 Mexico City-) is a Mexican film producer, actor and film director.

Pepe Romay

Pepe Romay (August 22, 1948 Mexico City-) also known as José Romay or Pepito Romay is a Mexican film producer, actor, film director, cinematographer and screenwriter.

His children are called Oscar Ortiz de Pinedo, Pedro Ortiz de Pinedo, Jesús Ortiz de Pinedo, Mariana Ortiz de Pinedo and Santiago Ortiz de Pinedo Tuccio.
