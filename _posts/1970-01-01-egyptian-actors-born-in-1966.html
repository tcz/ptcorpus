Egyptian actors who were born in 1966

Here are 5 famous actors from Egypt were born in 1966:

Khaled El Nabawy

Khaled El Nabawy (September 12, 1966 Mansoura, Egypt-) a.k.a. Khaled Nabawy, Khaled El Nabaoui, Khaled Nabawi, Khaled Muhammad El Nabawy or Khaled Nawawy is an Egyptian actor.

Moustafa Amar

Moustafa Amar (September 22, 1966 Alexandria-) also known as Amar, Moustafa, Mustafa Amar, Mostafa Amar, Amar, Mustafa, Moustafa Ahmed Mohamed Hassan Amar, Moustafa Qamar or Mustafa Qamar is an Egyptian singer, composer and actor. He has two children, Eyad Amar and Tayam Amar.

Khaled Abol Naga

Khaled Abol Naga (November 2, 1966 Cairo-) also known as Khaled Mohammed Sami Abol or Naga is an Egyptian actor.

Bassem Samra

Bassem Samra (October 16, 1966 Dakahlia Governorate-) is an Egyptian actor.

Hajjaj Abdul Azim

Hajjaj Abdul Azim (August 16, 1966-) is an Egyptian actor.
