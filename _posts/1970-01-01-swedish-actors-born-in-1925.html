Swedish actors who were born in 1925

Here are 14 famous actors from Sweden were born in 1925:

Folke Sundquist

Folke Sundquist (November 4, 1925 Falun-January 13, 2009 Malmö Municipality) a.k.a. Folke Sundqvist was a Swedish actor.

Nicolai Gedda

Nicolai Gedda (July 11, 1925 Stockholm-) also known as Nicolaï Gedda, Nicolai Ustinov, Gedda, Nicolai, Nicolai Gedda und die Kosaken, Nicolai Harry Gustav Gedda or Harry Gustaf Nikolaj Gadda is a Swedish singer and actor.

Hans Sundberg

Hans Sundberg (January 10, 1925 Enköping-April 26, 1995 Bålsta) also known as Hans Erik Vilhelm Sundberg or Hans Erik William Sundberg was a Swedish actor.

Olof Thunberg

Olof Thunberg (May 21, 1925 Västerås-) also known as Fritz-Olof Thunberg is a Swedish actor and film director. His child is called Svante Thunberg.

Rolf Ludwig

Rolf Ludwig (July 28, 1925 Stockholm-March 27, 1999 Berlin) a.k.a. Rolf Erik Ludewig was a Swedish actor and voice actor.

Åke Johansson

Åke Johansson (March 9, 1925 Stockholm-) is a Swedish actor.

Åke Falck

Åke Falck (April 3, 1925 Gothenburg-October 12, 1974 Danderyd Municipality) otherwise known as Åke Erik Lennart Falk was a Swedish film director, actor, screenwriter and television producer.

Bengt Eklund

Bengt Eklund (January 18, 1925 Stockholm-January 19, 1998 Stockholm) also known as Bengt Gunnar Eklund was a Swedish actor. His children are called Klas Eklund and Lena Eklund.

Gösta Krantz

Gösta Krantz (June 14, 1925 Stockholm-December 26, 2008 Stockholm) otherwise known as Gösta Clarence Isidor Krantz was a Swedish actor. His children are called Thomas Krantz and Tony Krantz.

Tom Dan-Bergman

Tom Dan-Bergman (March 7, 1925 Stockholm-February 19, 2009 Helsingborg) was a Swedish theatre director and actor. He had two children, Mikael Dan-Bergman and Kristofer Dan-Bergman.

Anders Jonason

Anders Jonason (March 19, 1925 Stockholm-) is a Swedish actor.

Stig Hallgren

Stig Hallgren (October 10, 1925 Stockholm-) is a Swedish cinematographer and actor.

Lars Elwin

Lars Elwin (December 19, 1925 Stockholm-) also known as Karl Lars Elwin is a Swedish actor.

Olof Lindfors

Olof Lindfors (August 31, 1925 Visby-December 10, 1993 Helsingborg) was a Swedish actor.
