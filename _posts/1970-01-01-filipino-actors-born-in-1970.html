Filipino actors who were born in 1970

Here are 8 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1970:

Ely Buendia

Ely Buendia (November 2, 1970 Naga, Camarines Sur-) also known as Ely, Ely Eleandro Basino Buendia, Jesus "Dizzy" Ventura, Eraserheads or Eleandre Basiño Buendia is a Filipino singer, actor, film score composer, film director and musician. He has two children, Oona Aurea Buendia and Eon Drake Buendia.

Richard Bonnin

Richard Bonnin (June 5, 1970 Manila-) is a Filipino actor.

Robert Alonzo

Robert Alonzo (August 4, 1970 Manila-) also known as Rob Alonzo is a Filipino stunt performer and actor.

Ronnel Victor

Ronnel Victor (June 21, 1970 Dipolog-July 1, 2002 Chicago) otherwise known as Ronel Victor was a Filipino actor.

Marco Polo Garcia

Marco Polo Garcia (October 4, 1970 Quezon City-March 5, 2010 Quezon City) also known as Marco Polo Adriano - Garcia, Marco Polo, MPG or Marco was a Filipino actor and politician. He had one child, John Steven Garcia.

Bentot Jr.

Bentot Jr. (January 8, 1970-) also known as Bentot, Luisito "Louie" G. Medina or Luisito G. Medina is a Filipino actor.

Ogie Diaz

Ogie Diaz (January 2, 1970 Quezon City-) also known as Ogie, Roger Díaz-Pandaan or Pekto is a Filipino comedian, presenter, talent manager, radio personality and actor.

Andre Tiangco

Andre Tiangco (November 30, 1970 San Fernando City-) also known as Andrei Tiangco is a Filipino actor.
