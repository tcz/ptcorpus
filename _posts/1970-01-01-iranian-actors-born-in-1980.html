Iranian actors born in 1980

Here are 7 famous actors from Iran were born in 1980:

Rouzbeh Rashidi

Rouzbeh Rashidi (December 23, 1980 Tehran-) is an Iranian film director, screenwriter, film producer, film editor, cinematographer and actor.

Arian Moayed

Arian Moayed (April 15, 1980 Iran-) otherwise known as Arian Maayed is an Iranian actor, theatrical producer, screenwriter and film director. His children are called Olive Joon Moayed and Ivy Shireen Moayed.

Poulad Kimiayi

Poulad Kimiayi (July 14, 1980 Tehran-) is an Iranian actor and pianist.

Navíd Akhavan

Navíd Akhavan (June 9, 1980 Tehran-) otherwise known as Navid Akhavan, Navíd Akhavan or Navid Navid is an Iranian actor, singer, translator and music video director.

Ali Sadeghi

Ali Sadeghi (December 5, 1980 Tehran-) is an Iranian actor.

Shahrokh Estakhri

Shahrokh Estakhri (October 6, 1980 Tehran-) is an Iranian actor. His child is called Panah Estakhri.

Ebrahin Sheibani

Ebrahin Sheibani (October 18, 1980 Tehran-) is an Iranian film director and actor.
