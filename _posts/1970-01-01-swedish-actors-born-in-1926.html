Swedish actors who were born in 1926

Here are 12 famous actors from Sweden were born in 1926:

Arne Ragneborn

Arne Ragneborn (July 13, 1926 Hammarby, Stockholm-January 5, 1978 Stockholm) was a Swedish film director, actor and screenwriter. He had one child, Ann Zacharias.

Yngve Gamlin

Yngve Gamlin (March 17, 1926 Strömsund-February 1, 1995 Stockholm) also known as Yngve Daniel Petrus Jönsson was a Swedish film director, actor, production designer, film art director, screenwriter and costume designer.

Jan-Olof Strandberg

Jan-Olof Strandberg (September 9, 1926 Stockholm-) is a Swedish actor. He has one child, Mikael Strandberg.

Nils Nygren

Nils Nygren (September 13, 1926 Nässjö-September 21, 1976 Gothenburg) was a Swedish actor. He had two children, Johannes Nygren and Magnus Nygren.

Lars Ekborg

Lars Ekborg (June 6, 1926 Uppsala-October 7, 1969 Ängelholm) otherwise known as Ekborg, Lars or Lars Åke Rupert Ekborg was a Swedish actor. He had three children, Dan Ekborg, Anders Ekborg and Maud Ekborg.

Tord Peterson

Tord Peterson (April 21, 1926 Danderyd Municipality-) also known as Tord Greger Pettersson or Tord Pettersson is a Swedish actor.

Lennart Tellfelt

Lennart Tellfelt (May 24, 1926 Stockholm-November 19, 1991 Edsbro) was a Swedish actor.

John Harryson

John Harryson (April 17, 1926 Katrineholm-November 28, 2008) was a Swedish actor and voice actor. His child is called Peter Harryson.

Rolf Carlsten

Rolf Carlsten (December 17, 1926 Stockholm-) is a Swedish actor.

Sten-Göran Camitz

Sten-Göran Camitz (January 11, 1926 Västmanland-) a.k.a. Sten Göran Camitz is a Swedish film editor and actor.

Hans Bendrik

Hans Bendrik (April 16, 1926 Söderhamn-December 19, 2010 Stockholm) a.k.a. Hans Axel Bendrik was a Swedish actor and writer.

Arne Stivell

Arne Stivell (August 3, 1926 Rättvik-June 23, 1997 Stockholm) was a Swedish film director, screenwriter, actor and film producer.
