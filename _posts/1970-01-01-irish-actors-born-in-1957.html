Irish actors born in 1957

Here are 6 famous actors from Republic of Ireland were born in 1957:

Denis Leary

Denis Leary (August 18, 1957 Worcester-) a.k.a. Denis Colin Leeary, Dennis Leary, Denis Colin Leary or "Dr. Denis Leary" is an Irish comedian, actor, film producer, television producer, screenwriter, author, stand-up comedian, voice actor, film director and writer. He has two children, John Joseph Leary and Devin Leary.

Daniel Day-Lewis

Daniel Day-Lewis (April 29, 1957 Greenwich-) otherwise known as Daniel Michael Blake Day-Lewis, Daniel Day Lewis or DDL is an Irish actor. He has three children, Gabriel-Kane Day-Lewis, Ronan Cal Day-Lewis and Cashel Blake Day-Lewis.

Gerard Mannix Flynn

Gerard Mannix Flynn (May 1, 1957 Dublin-) otherwise known as Gerard Flynn or Mannix Flynn is an Irish actor, screenwriter, writer, playwright and politician.

Mark Tandy

Mark Tandy (February 8, 1957 Athlone-) is an Irish actor.

Jon Kenny

Jon Kenny (December 12, 1957 Hospital, County Limerick-) is an Irish actor, comedian, voice actor and musician. He has two children, Leah Kenny and Aaron Kenny.

Kevin Wallace

Kevin Wallace (June 19, 1957 Limerick-) is an Irish theatrical producer and actor.
