Indian actresses in New York City

Here are 5 famous actresses from India who live or lived in New York City:

Mira Nair

Mira Nair (October 15, 1957 Rourkela-) is an Indian film director, film producer, actor, teacher and writer. Her child is called Zohran Mamdani. She has been known to be living in Odisha and New York City.

Deepti Gupta

Deepti Gupta also known as दीप्ति गुप्तI is an Indian actor and model. She has been known to be residing in New York City.

Perizaad Zorabian

Perizaad Zorabian (October 23, 1973 Mumbai-) a.k.a. Perizaad Boman Irani is an Indian actor, entrepreneur and model. She has been living in New York City and Mumbai.

Padma Lakshmi

Padma Lakshmi (September 1, 1970 Chennai-) also known as Padma Parvati Lakshmi is an Indian model, actor and author. She has one child, Krishna Thea Lakshmi-Dell. She has been widely known to be residing in Chennai and New York City.

Jiah Khan

Jiah Khan (February 20, 1988 New York City-June 3, 2013 Juhu) also known as Nafisa Khan or Jiah Nafisa Khan was an Indian actor and model. She was living in New York City and London.
