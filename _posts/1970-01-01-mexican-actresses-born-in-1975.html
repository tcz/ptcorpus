Mexican actresses who were born in 1975

Here are 7 famous actresses from Mexico were born in 1975:

Aracely Arámbula

Aracely Arámbula (March 6, 1975 Chihuahua-) also known as Aracely Arambula, Aracely Arámbula Jaques or Arámbula, Aracely is a Mexican singer, model and actor. Her children are called Miguel Basteri and Daniel Basteri.

Itatí Cantoral

Itatí Cantoral (May 13, 1975 Mexico City-) otherwise known as Itati Cantoral, Itati Cantoral Zucchi or itati_cantoral is a Mexican actor and singer. She has three children, Jose Eduardo Santamarina Cantoral, Roberto Miguel Santamarina Cantoral and María Itatí Cruz Cantoral.

Bianca Marroquín

Bianca Marroquín (January 15, 1975 Monterrey-) a.k.a. Bianca Marroquin or Bianca Pamela Marroquín Pérez is a Mexican actor, singer and dancer.

Cecilia Tijerina

Cecilia Tijerina (July 26, 1975 Mexico City-) otherwise known as Ceci is a Mexican actor.

Marisol del Olmo

Marisol del Olmo (May 9, 1975 Mexico City-) is a Mexican actor. She has one child, Isabella Antonioli.

Sara Ramirez

Sara Ramirez (August 31, 1975 Mazatlán-) also known as Sara Elena Ramirez is a Mexican actor, songwriter and singer.

Elizabeth Cervantes

Elizabeth Cervantes (August 1, 1975 Mexico City-) is a Mexican actor.
