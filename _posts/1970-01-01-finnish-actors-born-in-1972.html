Finnish actors born in 1972

Here are 5 famous actors from Finland were born in 1972:

Jussi Chydenius

Jussi Chydenius (December 30, 1972 Helsinki-) also known as Chydenius, Jussi is a Finnish singer, drummer and actor.

Jukka Kärkkäinen

Jukka Kärkkäinen (January 20, 1972 Kerava-) a.k.a. Jukka-Pekka Kärkkäinen is a Finnish film director, screenwriter, film editor, cinematographer and actor.

Ville Haapasalo

Ville Haapasalo (February 28, 1972 Lahti-) a.k.a. Ville Juhana Haapasalo or Ville Khaapasalo is a Finnish actor, screenwriter and presenter.

Niko Saarela

Niko Saarela (February 28, 1972 Helsinki-) otherwise known as Niko Pietari Saarela is a Finnish actor and screenwriter.

Kristo Salminen

Kristo Salminen (August 21, 1972 Helsinki-) is a Finnish actor.
