Serbian movie actors born in the year 1958

Here are 9 famous actors from Serbia were born in 1958:

Vlada Divljan

Vlada Divljan (May 10, 1958 Belgrade-) a.k.a. Vladimir Divljan, Divljan, Vlada, Владимир Дивљан, Wladimir Divljan or Vladimir "Vlada" Divljan is a Serbian songwriter, singer, actor and film score composer. His child is called Stevan Divljan.

Srđan Šaper

Srđan Šaper (October 9, 1958 Belgrade-) also known as Šaper is a Serbian film director, musician, talent manager, film score composer and actor.

Radoje Cupic

Radoje Cupic (February 3, 1958 Novi Sad-) also known as Cupe or Radoje Čupić is a Serbian actor.

Zoran Cvijanović

Zoran Cvijanović (January 25, 1958 Belgrade-) also known as Zoran Cvijanovic, Cvija, Zoran Cvijanovic Cvija, Zoran Cvijanovik or Зоран Цвијановић is a Serbian film producer and actor.

Milan Mladenovic

Milan Mladenovic (September 21, 1958 Zagreb-November 5, 1994 Belgrade) a.k.a. Milan Mladenović was a Serbian singer, musician, actor, film score composer and guitarist.

Svetozar Cvetkovic

Svetozar Cvetkovic (June 20, 1958 Belgrade-) also known as Svetozar Cvetkovikj or Cvele is a Serbian actor, film producer and television producer. He has two children, Dea Cvetkovic and Clara Cvetkovic.

Nenad Ciric

Nenad Ciric (August 25, 1958 Belgrade-) a.k.a. Cira is a Serbian actor.

Radoslav Milenkovic

Radoslav Milenkovic (February 17, 1958 Novi Sad-) also known as Rale Milenkovic, Radoslav Milenković or Radoslav "Rale" Milenković is a Serbian actor and theatre director.

Predrag Bambić

Predrag Bambić (August 7, 1958 Belgrade-) is a Serbian cinematographer, film producer and actor. He has one child, Bojana Bambic.
