Indian movie stars born in 1950

Deepa Mehta

Deepa Mehta (January 1, 1950 Amritsar-) otherwise known as Deepa Mehta Saltzman or Deepa Saltzman is an Indian screenwriter, film director, film producer, actor and television producer. Her child is called Devyani Saltzman.

Leena Chandavarkar

Leena Chandavarkar (August 29, 1950 Dharwad-) also known as Leena Chandravarkar, Leena Abhas Ganguli, Leena Kishore Kumar, Leena Siddharth Bandodkar or Beauty is an Indian actor. She has two children, Amit Kumar and Sumit Kumar.

Suhasini Mulay

Suhasini Mulay (November 20, 1950 Patna-) also known as Suhasini Mule, Suhasini Muley or Suhasni Mule is an Indian actor.

Honey Irani

Honey Irani (January 17, 1950 Mumbai-) a.k.a. Honey Javed or Honey Irani [Ashwani] is an Indian screenwriter, actor and writer. She has two children, Zoya Akhtar and Farhan Akhtar.

Rehana Sultan

Rehana Sultan (November 19, 1950 Allahabad-) is an Indian actor.

Bharathi Vishnuvardhan

Bharathi Vishnuvardhan (August 15, 1950 Karnataka-) a.k.a. Bharathi, N. Bharathi, Bharati, Bharti or Bhartee is an Indian actor. Her children are called Keerthi Vishnuvardhan and Chandana Vishnuvardhan.

Pamela Salem

Pamela Salem (January 22, 1950 Mumbai-) is an Indian actor.

Jayashree Basavaraj
