Polish movie actresses born in the year 1965

Here are 7 famous actresses from Poland were born in 1965:

Małgorzata Hajewska

Agnieszka Kumor

Agnieszka Kumor (November 8, 1965 Warsaw-) is a Polish actor.

Aleksandra Konieczna

Aleksandra Konieczna (October 13, 1965 Prudnik-) is a Polish actor and theatre director.

Agnieszka Pilaszewska

Agnieszka Pilaszewska (March 19, 1965 Puławy-) is a Polish actor, screenwriter and voice actor. She has one child, Kornelia Maciejewska.

Marzena Kipiel-Sztuka

Marzena Kipiel-Sztuka (October 19, 1965 Legnica-) is a Polish actor.

Malgorzata Pienkowska

Malgorzata Pienkowska (May 19, 1965 Wrocław-) is a Polish actor.

Joanna Trzepiecińska
