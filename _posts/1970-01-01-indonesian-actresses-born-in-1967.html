Indonesian movie actresses born in the year 1967

Here are 5 famous actresses from Indonesia were born in 1967:

Ira Wibowo

Ira Wibowo (December 20, 1967 Berlin-) otherwise known as R.A. Ira Wibowo Wirjodiprodjo is an Indonesian actor and presenter. She has two children, Andhika Radya Bagaskara and Mario Arya Bagaskara.

Ayu Laksmi

Ayu Laksmi (November 25, 1967 Singaraja-) is an Indonesian actor, singer-songwriter and dancer.

Lia Warokka

Lia Warokka (July 8, 1967 Jakarta-) also known as Lia Waroka is an Indonesian actor. She has one child, Mohammad Arsyil Azhiim.

Lulu Dewayanti

Lulu Dewayanti (August 23, 1967 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor.

Ita Purnamasari

Ita Purnamasari (July 15, 1967 Surabaya-) is an Indonesian singer, songwriter and actor. She has one child, Muhammad Fernanda Dharmawan.
