Hungarian actors born in 1943

Here are 5 famous actors from Hungary were born in 1943:

Péter Halász

Péter Halász (August 20, 1943 Budapest-March 9, 2006 New York City) was a Hungarian actor, writer and theatre director. He had four children, Judith Halasz, Cora Fischer, Gabor Halasz and David Halasz.

Lajos Szűcs

Lajos Szűcs (December 10, 1943 Apatin-) also known as Lajos Szücs or Lajos Szûcs is a Hungarian actor. He has one child, Csaba Szűcs.

András Bálint

András Bálint (April 26, 1943 Pécs-) a.k.a. Bálint András or Andras Balint is a Hungarian actor and voice actor. He has one child, Dániel Bálint.

Andras Kozak

Andras Kozak (February 23, 1943 Gávavencsellő-February 24, 2005 Budapest) otherwise known as András Kozák or Andrew Kozak was a Hungarian actor. He had two children, Ágnes Kozák and Dénes Kozák.

Róbert Koltai

Róbert Koltai (December 16, 1943 Budapest-) also known as Koltay Róbert, Koltai Róbert or Koltai Robert is a Hungarian actor, film director, screenwriter and television director. His child is called Gabor Koltai.
