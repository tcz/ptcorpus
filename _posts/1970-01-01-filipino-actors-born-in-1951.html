Filipino actors who were born in 1951

Here are 9 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1951:

Mike Enriquez

Mike Enriquez (September 29, 1951 Santa Ana, Manila-) also known as Miguel Castro Enriquez or Miguel C. Enriquez is a Filipino journalist and actor.

Jim Paredes

Jim Paredes (August 30, 1951 Manila-) a.k.a. Jaime Ramon Paredes, Jaime Ramon "Jim" Paredes or Tado Jimenez is a Filipino musician, film producer, tv personality, activist, teacher and actor. His children are called Ala Paredes, Erica Paredes and Mio Paredes.

Yoyong Martirez

Yoyong Martirez (September 9, 1951 Pasig-) also known as Rosalio 'Yoyong' Martirez, Rosarlio 'Yoyong' Martirez, Rosalio D. Martirez, Rosalio Martirez or Rosalio "Yoyong" D. Martirez is a Filipino actor and basketball player.

Jimmy Santos

Jimmy Santos (October 8, 1951 Pateros, Metro Manila-) a.k.a. James R. Santos or Jaime R. Santos is a Filipino actor and comedian.

Junix Inocian

Junix Inocian (March 17, 1951 Iligan-) also known as Junix Nocian, Junix Inoxian or Rufino Duran Inocian Jr. is a Filipino actor.

Rey 'PJ' Abellana

Rey 'PJ' Abellana (March 21, 1951 Davao-) a.k.a. Rey PJ Abellana, PJ Avellana, Rey P.J. Abellana, PJ Abellana, Rey Abellana, Rey Abellena or Rey \"PJ\" Abellana is a Filipino actor. He has one child, Carla Abellana.

Danilo Jurado

Danilo Jurado (November 25, 1951 Manila-) also known as Jose I. Jurado III, Danny Jurado or Jose Jurado is a Filipino actor. His children are called Joshua Jurado, Josemari Jurado, Anthony Jose Jurado, Nino Jose Jurado, Jose Danilo Jurado and Josemina Jurado.

Boboy Garovillo

Boboy Garovillo (October 10, 1951-) also known as Buboy Garovillo or Boboy Garrovillo is a Filipino actor and singer. His children are called Fonz Garovillo and Anton Garovillo.

Shalala

Shalala (January 22, 1951-) a.k.a. Sha Lala Lala, Lito 'Tolits' Reyes, Lito 'Zhalala' Reyes, Lito Reyes, Tolites Reyes, Tolits 'Shalala' Reyes, Tolites or Carmelito Reyes is a Filipino actor.
