Russian actors who were born in 1927

Here are 11 famous actors from Russia were born in 1927:

Mikhail Ulyanov

Igor Dmitriev

Igor Dmitriev (May 29, 1927 Saint Petersburg-January 26, 2008 Saint Petersburg) a.k.a. Igor Dmitriyev, Igor Borisovich Dmitriev, I. Dmitriev or I.Dmitriev was a Russian actor and voice actor. His child is called Alexey Dmitriev.

Anatoly Adoskin

Anatoly Adoskin (November 23, 1927 Moscow-) a.k.a. Anatoli Adoskin or Anatoli Michajlovich Adoskin is a Russian actor.

Oleg Yefremov

Oleg Yefremov (October 1, 1927 Moscow-May 24, 2000 Moscow) also known as Oleg Nikolayevich Yefremov, Oleg Efremov, O. Efremov or Oleg Nikolaevich Efremov was a Russian actor, theatre director and film director. His children are called Mikhail Yefremov and Anastasiya Yefremova.

Igor Gorbachyov

Igor Gorbachyov (October 20, 1927 Saint Petersburg-February 19, 2003 Saint Petersburg) a.k.a. I. Gorbachyov, I. Gorbachev, Igor Gorbachyev or Igor Olegovich Gorbachyov was a Russian actor. He had one child, Igor Igorevich Gorbachyov.

Yevgeni Tashkov

Yevgeni Tashkov (January 1, 1927 Volgograd Oblast-February 15, 2012 Moscow) a.k.a. Yevgeni Ivanovich Tashkov was a Russian film director, screenwriter and actor. His child is called Andrei Tashkov.

Eldar Ryazanov

Eldar Ryazanov (November 18, 1927 Samara-) a.k.a. Eldar Alexandrovich Ryazanov, Eldar Rjasanow, E. Ryazanov or Eldar Aleksandrovich Ryazanov is a Russian film director, screenwriter, presenter, poet and actor. He has one child, Olga Ryazanova.

Yuri Kayurov

Yuri Kayurov (September 30, 1927 Cherepovets-) also known as Yu. Kayurov or Yuri Ivanovich Kayurov is a Russian actor. His child is called Leonid Kayurov.

Yevgeny Morgunov

Yevgeny Morgunov (April 27, 1927 Moscow-June 25, 1999 Moscow) also known as Yevgeny Alexandrovich Morgunov, Ye. Morgunov, Yevgeni Aleksandrovich Morgunov or Evgeniy Morgunov was a Russian screenwriter, actor and film director. He had two children, Anton Morgunov and Nikolay Morgunov.

Vsevolod Shestakov

Vsevolod Shestakov (July 15, 1927-April 29, 2011) was a Russian hydrogeologist and actor. He had one child, Sergey Shestakov.

Oleg Karavajchuk

Oleg Karavajchuk (December 28, 1927 Kiev-) also known as Oleg Karavaychuk or O. Karavajchuk is a Russian film score composer and actor.
