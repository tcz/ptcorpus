Brazilian movie actresses born in the year 1939

Here are 9 famous actresses from Brazil were born in 1939:

Araci Esteves (January 21, 1939 Porto Alegre-) is a Brazilian actor.

Yaratan

Yaratan (August 17, 1939 São Luís-) also known as Yaratan de Maria Lauletta Mattos or Yaratan Lauretta is a Brazilian actor.

Meiry Vieira

Meiry Vieira (June 2, 1939 Rio de Janeiro-) also known as Meire Vera, Meyry Vieira or Meiry Cordeiro Vieira is a Brazilian actor.

Thelma Reston

Thelma Reston (July 6, 1939 Piracanjuba-December 20, 2012 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Thelma Salim Reston or Telma Reston was a Brazilian actor.

Aizita Nascimento

Aizita Nascimento (July 14, 1939 Rio de Janeiro-) also known as Aizita Nascimento da Costa is a Brazilian actor and model.

Norma Blum

Norma Blum (October 11, 1939 Rio de Janeiro-) is a Brazilian actor.

Suely Franco

Suely Franco (October 16, 1939 Rio de Janeiro-) is a Brazilian actor and singer.
