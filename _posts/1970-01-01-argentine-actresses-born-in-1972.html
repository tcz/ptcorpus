Argentine actresses who were born in 1972

Here are 8 famous actresses from Argentina were born in 1972:

Valentina Bassi

Valentina Bassi (November 13, 1972 Trelew-) also known as Valentina Vassi is an Argentine actor. She has one child, Lisandro Rosell.

Vera Fogwill

Vera Fogwill (November 28, 1972 Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine screenwriter, film director and actor.

Luciana Pedraza

Luciana Pedraza (January 5, 1972 Salta-) also known as Luciana Duvall is an Argentine actor, film director, film producer and screenwriter.

Carolina Román

Carolina Román (April 9, 1972 Formosa-) is an Argentine actor.

Eliana Guercio

Eliana Guercio (October 12, 1972 Lanús-) is an Argentine model and actor. She has two children, Yazmin Romero and Chloe Romero.

Julieta Ortega

Julieta Ortega (October 6, 1972 Buenos Aires-) also known as Julieta Ortega Salazar, Juli or Juliette Ortega is an Argentine actor.

Florencia Raggi
