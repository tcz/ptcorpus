Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1936

Here are 18 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1936:

José Mojica Marins

José Mojica Marins (March 13, 1936 Vila Mariana-) a.k.a. Ze do Caixao, Coffin Joe, J. Avelar, J.Avelar, Mojica, Zé do Caixão or Brazilian Dario Argento is a Brazilian film director, actor, screenwriter, film producer and writer. His children are called Crounel Marins, Merisol Marins and Liz Marins.

Luis Sérgio Person

Luis Sérgio Person (February 12, 1936 São Paulo-January 7, 1976 São Paulo) also known as L. S. Person, Luis Sergio Person or Luiz Sergio Person was a Brazilian film director, actor, screenwriter and film producer. He had two children, Marina Person and Domingas Person.

Oduvaldo Vianna Filho

Oduvaldo Vianna Filho (July 4, 1936 São Paulo-July 16, 1974 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Vianinha was a Brazilian playwright, actor and screenwriter. He had one child, Vinícius Vianna.

Moacyr Franco

Moacyr Franco (October 5, 1936 Ituiutaba-) also known as Moacyr de Oliveira Franco is a Brazilian actor. He has six children, Domenico Franco, Paulo Augusto Franco, Moacyr Franco Jr, João Vitor Franco, Maria Cecília Franco and Ana Helena Franco.

Tom Zé

Tom Zé (October 11, 1936 Irará-) a.k.a. Tom Ze or Antônio José Santana Martins is a Brazilian songwriter, composer, multi-instrumentalist, singer-songwriter and actor.

Francisco Milani

Francisco Milani (November 19, 1936 São Paulo-August 13, 2005 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Francisco Ferreira Milani was a Brazilian actor and television director. He had one child, Carlo Milani.

Altair Lima

Altair Lima (July 8, 1936 Barretos-December 24, 2002 Angatuba) also known as Altair Vieira de Faria Naves Pinto was a Brazilian actor. He had one child, Ana Célia Camargo Pinto.

Maurice Capovila

Maurice Capovila (January 16, 1936 Valinhos-) otherwise known as Maurice Capovilla is a Brazilian film director, screenwriter, actor, television director and film producer. His children are called Matias Capovilla, Lia Capovilla and Adriana Capovilla.

Joel Barcellos

Joel Barcellos (November 27, 1936 Vitória, Espírito Santo-) also known as Joel Dias Barcellos, Joel Martins or Joel Barcelos is a Brazilian actor, screenwriter and film director.

Domingos de Oliveira

Domingos de Oliveira (September 28, 1936 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Domingos Oliveira is a Brazilian actor, film director, screenwriter, film producer, television director, film score composer and film editor. He has four children, Maria Mariana, Daniele de Oliveira, Igor de Oliveira and Frederico de Oliveira.

Olney São Paulo

Olney São Paulo (August 7, 1936 Riachão do Jacuípe-February 15, 1978 Rio de Janeiro) was a Brazilian actor and film director. His child is called Ilya São Paulo.

Carlos Alberto de Nóbrega

Carlos Alberto de Nóbrega (March 12, 1936 Niterói-) also known as Carlos A. Nóbrega is a Brazilian actor and screenwriter.

Emiliano Queiroz

Emiliano Queiroz (January 1, 1936 Aracati-) is a Brazilian actor and screenwriter.

Jean-Claude Bernardet

Jean-Claude Bernardet (August 2, 1936 Charleroi-) is a Brazilian film director, screenwriter, actor, writer, film critic and film editor.

Arduíno Colassanti

Arduíno Colassanti (February 15, 1936 Livorno-February 22, 2014 Niterói) a.k.a. Arduino Colasanti was a Brazilian actor and businessperson.

Milton Rodríguez

Milton Rodríguez (October 18, 1936 Rio de Janeiro-) otherwise known as Milton Morris, Milton Rodrigues or Milton Rodrígues is a Brazilian actor.

Dedé Santana

Dedé Santana (May 9, 1936 São Gonçalo-) also known as Manfried Santana or Manfried Sant'Anna is a Brazilian actor, screenwriter, film director and comedian. He has five children, Maurício Santana, Maria Leone Santana, Marcos Santana, Yasmin Santana and Daynara Santana.

Rolando Boldrin

Rolando Boldrin (October 22, 1936 São Joaquim da Barra-) is a Brazilian actor, singer and songwriter.
