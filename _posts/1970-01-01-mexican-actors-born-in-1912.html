Mexican actors who were born in 1912

Here are 6 famous actors from Mexico were born in 1912:

Pedro Armendáriz

Pedro Armendáriz (May 9, 1912 Mexico City-June 18, 1963 Los Angeles) also known as Pedro Armendariz, Pedro Gregorio Armendáriz Hastings, Pete, The Clark Gable of Mexico or Pedro Gregorio Armendáriz was a Mexican actor. He had two children, Pedro Armendáriz, Jr. and Carmen Armendáriz.

Tito Davison

Tito Davison (November 14, 1912 Chillán-March 21, 1985 Mexico City) was a Mexican film director, screenwriter, actor and writer. His child is called Ricardo Alfredo Davison.

Víctor Urruchúa

Víctor Urruchúa (December 30, 1912 Mexico-November 5, 1981 Mexico) was a Mexican screenwriter, film director and actor.

Ramón Vallarino

Ramón Vallarino (August 31, 1912 Mexico City-) a.k.a. Ramon Vallarino or Ramon Vallarino Rodriguez is a Mexican actor.

Emilio Tuero

Emilio Tuero (April 5, 1912 Santander-July 22, 1971 Mexico City) a.k.a. El Barítono de Argel, Barítono de Argel or Emilio Tuero Cubillas was a Mexican singer, film producer and actor.

Alfredo Varela, Jr.

Alfredo Varela, Jr. (November 30, 1912 Mexico City-November 5, 1986 Mexico City) also known as Alfredo Varela hijo, Varelita, Alfredo Varela 'Varelita', Alfredo Varela Catalá or Alfredo Varela Jr. was a Mexican actor, screenwriter and writer.
