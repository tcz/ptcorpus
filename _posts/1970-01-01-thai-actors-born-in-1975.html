Thai movie stars born in 1975

Tao Ruspoli

Tao Ruspoli (November 7, 1975 Bangkok-) a.k.a. Teo Rispoli or Tao dei Principi Ruspoli is a Thai musician, photographer, film director, cinematographer, film producer, film editor and actor.

Eric Koston (April 29, 1975 Bangkok-) also known as Froston, Frosty, Carson Koston, Erik Coston, Ek or Champ is a Thai actor, film producer and skateboarder. He has one child, June Marie Koston.

Adisorn Tresirikasem

Adisorn Tresirikasem (November 2, 1975-) a.k.a. Adisorn Trisirikasem or Anusorn Trisirikasem is a Thai film director, screenwriter and actor.

Cheathavuth Watcharakhun

Joey Boy

Joey Boy (December 25, 1975 Thailand-) also known as Apisit Opasaimlikit or Joey is a Thai actor.

Pawalit Mongkolpisit

Pawalit Mongkolpisit (August 8, 1975-) a.k.a. Pawarith Monkolpisit is a Thai actor.
