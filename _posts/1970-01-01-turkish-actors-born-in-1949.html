Turkish actors born in 1949

Kadir İnanır

Kadir İnanır (April 15, 1949 Fatsa-) also known as Kadir Inanir is a Turkish actor and film director.

İlyas Salman

Aytaç Arman (June 22, 1949 Adana-) otherwise known as Aytac Arman is a Turkish actor.
