Thai movie stars born in 1981

Here are 11 famous actors from Thailand were born in 1981:

Dan Chupong

Dan Chupong (March 23, 1981 Kalasin Province-) a.k.a. Chupong Changprung, Chupong Chungpruk, Deaw, Danny Chupong, Dan Choopong, Dan Choupong, Deaw Chupong or Danny Choupong is a Thai actor and stunt performer.

Akkarin Akaranithimetrath

Akkarin Akaranithimetrath (October 20, 1981 Bangkok-) a.k.a. Teeradanai Suwanahom, Dreradanai Suwanhom, Joke, Theeradanai Suwannahom or Theeranai Suwanhom is a Thai actor.

Sunny Suwanmethanon

Sunny Suwanmethanon (May 18, 1981 Thailand-) a.k.a. Sunny Suwanmethanont is a Thai actor.

Pream Busala-Khamvong

Pream Busala-Khamvong (March 24, 1981-) is a Thai actor and businessperson.

Atichart Chumnanon

Atichart Chumnanon (February 20, 1981 Bangkok-) also known as Aum or Umm is a Thai actor and model.

Nithichai Yotamornsunthorn

Nithichai Yotamornsunthorn (September 17, 1981 Lampang Province-) is a Thai actor.

Hugo

Hugo (August 6, 1981 London-) a.k.a. Hugo Chakrabongse Levy, Chulachak Chakrabongse or Hugo Chula Alexander Levy is a Thai singer-songwriter and actor. He has one child, Tassanachak Chakrabongse.

Sorachai Sang-aakaat

Sorachai Sang-aakaat (July 18, 1981 Thailand-) otherwise known as Surachai Sangarkart or Surachai Sangagard is a Thai actor.

Poonyapat Soonkunchanon

Poonyapat Soonkunchanon (December 4, 1981 Bangkok-) a.k.a. Poonyaphat 'Lerm' Boonkoonchanok is a Thai actor.

Danai Samutkochorn

Danai Samutkochorn (January 17, 1981-) also known as Dan or Dan Danai is a Thai actor.

Taweesak Pamornpol

Taweesak Pamornpol (June 1, 1981 Suphan Buri Province-) a.k.a. Nattapol Pamornpol, Thaweesak Pamornphon, K or K AF1 is a Thai singer, actor and flight attendant.
