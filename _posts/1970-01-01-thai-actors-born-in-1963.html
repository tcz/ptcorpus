Thai movie stars born in 1963

Here are 10 famous actors from Thailand were born in 1963:

Amphol Lumpoon

Amphol Lumpoon (July 20, 1963 Rayong Province-) also known as Amphol Lumkool or Ampol Lampoon is a Thai singer and actor. He has one child, Nawapol Lumpoon.

Sakrat Ruekthamrong

Sakrat Ruekthamrong (February 25, 1963 Phrae-) also known as Sakarat Ritthumrong or Sakkaraj Rerkthamrong is a Thai actor, model, airman and basketball player.

Bin Bunluerit

Bin Bunluerit (May 27, 1963 Sa Kaeo Province-) is a Thai film director and actor.

Puttipong Pormsaka Na-Sakonnakorn

Puttipong Pormsaka Na-Sakonnakorn (May 13, 1963 Thailand-) a.k.a. Phuttipong Promasakha Na Sakolnakorn, Puttipong Promsaka Na Sakolnakorn, Petch or Sena Petch is a Thai film director and actor. He has one child, Khachamach Promsaka Na Skolnakorn.

Ekkapun Bunluerit

Ekkapun Bunluerit (May 27, 1963 Prachinburi Province-) also known as Tide Bunluerit or Tide is a Thai actor.

Santisuk Promsiri

Santisuk Promsiri (August 6, 1963 Tha Chang District, Sing Buri-) a.k.a. Santisuk Phromsiri is a Thai actor. His child is called Siwakorn Promsiri.

Kiat Kitjaroen

Kiat Kitjaroen (September 22, 1963 Thailand-) also known as Sumogig or Gig is a Thai actor and master of ceremonies. He has one child, Karn Kitjaroen.

Jaturong Mokjok

Jaturong Mokjok (December 22, 1963 Thailand-) also known as Jaturong Pollaboon or Jaturong Ponlaboon is a Thai actor, film director, screenwriter and comedian. His children are called Pimchanok Ponlabhun and Natchapa Ponlaboon.

Tongchai Prasongsanti

Tongchai Prasongsanti (February 26, 1963 Nakhon Ratchasima Province-) is a Thai actor.

Anant Boonnark

Anant Boonnark (April 14, 1963 Thailand-) is a Thai actor. He has three children, Kabin Boonnark, Korakosh Boonnark and Kannika Boonnark.
