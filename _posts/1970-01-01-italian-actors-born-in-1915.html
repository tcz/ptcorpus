Italian movie actors born in the year 1915

Here are 14 famous actors from Italy were born in 1915:

Mario Monicelli

Mario Monicelli (May 16, 1915 Prati-November 29, 2010 Rome) a.k.a. M. Monicelli, Michele Badiek, Monicelli or Mario Moncelli was an Italian film director, screenwriter, actor and film editor. He had three children, Martina Monicelli, Rosa Monicelli and Ottavia Monicelli.

Mario Carotenuto

Mario Carotenuto (June 29, 1915 Rome-April 14, 1995 Rome) was an Italian actor. He had one child, Claretta Carotenuto.

Mimo Billi

Mimo Billi (March 21, 1915 Imola-November 26, 1978 Rome) a.k.a. Giacomo Billi, Mimmo Billi, Giacomo Gilli or Mino Billi was an Italian actor.

Vittorio Sanipoli

Vittorio Sanipoli (October 17, 1915 Genoa-July 25, 1992 Rome) also known as Luciano Sanipoli or Vittorio Sanni was an Italian actor and voice actor.

Mario del Monaco

Mario del Monaco (July 27, 1915 Florence-October 16, 1982 Mestre) was an Italian singer and actor. His child is called Giancarlo del Monaco.

Aldo Fiorelli

Aldo Fiorelli (May 8, 1915 Calenzano-November 5, 1983) was an Italian actor.

Franco Castellani

Franco Castellani (November 21, 1915 Rome-August 4, 1983 Rome) a.k.a. F. Castellani was an Italian actor.

Luigi Tosi

Luigi Tosi (July 15, 1915 Verona-) a.k.a. Nat Coster or Gigi Tosi is an Italian actor.

Alfredo Adami

Alfredo Adami (July 2, 1915 Rome-August 2, 1989 Rome) also known as Vulzinio Adami or Vulsinio Adami was an Italian actor.

Aleardo Ward

Aleardo Ward (February 16, 1915 Ventimiglia-December 3, 1971 Rome) was an Italian actor. He had three children, Luca Ward, Monica Ward and Andrea Ward.

Galliano Sbarra

Galliano Sbarra (July 3, 1915 Livorno-September 9, 1987 Rome) a.k.a. Sbarra, Galiano Sbarra or Caliano Sbarra was an Italian actor.

Gian Carlo Fusco

Gian Carlo Fusco (July 1, 1915 La Spezia-September 17, 1984 Rome) also known as Giancarlo Fusco was an Italian actor, screenwriter, journalist and writer.

Mario Colli

Mario Colli (July 7, 1915 Rome-November 29, 1989 Rome) a.k.a. Nello Delli Colli or Mario Delli Colli was an Italian actor and voice actor.

Roberto Villa

Roberto Villa (December 2, 1915 Casablanca-June 30, 2002 Sutri) a.k.a. Giulio Sabetta was an Italian actor and voice actor.
