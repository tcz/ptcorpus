Turkish actresses born in 1948

Here are 5 famous actresses from Turkey were born in 1948:

Semiramis Pekkan

Semiramis Pekkan (September 30, 1948 Istanbul-) also known as Pekkan, Semiramis is a Turkish singer and actor.

Anta Toros

Anta Toros (January 12, 1948 Istanbul-) a.k.a. Antaram Torosyan is a Turkish actor. She has one child, İrna Büyüksakayan.

Tomris İncer (March 16, 1948 Bulgaria-) also known as Tomris Incer is a Turkish actor.

Mine Mutlu

Mine Mutlu (November 28, 1948 Istanbul-September 21, 1990 Istanbul) was a Turkish actor. Her children are called Çağkan Çulha and Büşra Çulha.

Defne Yalnız
