Hungarian actors born in 1925

Here are 15 famous actors from Hungary were born in 1925:

Ferenc Kállai

Ferenc Kállai (October 4, 1925 Gyomaendrőd-July 11, 2010 Budapest) also known as Kállay Ferenc, Ferenc Krampner or Francis Krampner was a Hungarian actor and voice actor.

Károly Makk

Károly Makk (December 22, 1925 Berettyóújfalu-) a.k.a. Karoly Makk is a Hungarian film director, screenwriter, television director, actor and film producer.

Emil Keres

Emil Keres (July 9, 1925 Szombathely-) is a Hungarian actor.

Gellért Raksányi

Gellért Raksányi (July 19, 1925 Szigetvár-May 20, 2008 Budapest) also known as Kutya or Kutyu was a Hungarian actor and voice actor.

György Kálmán

György Kálmán (March 6, 1925 Budapest-February 19, 1989 Budapest) was a Hungarian actor and voice actor.

László Inke

László Inke (January 16, 1925 Arad-August 19, 1992 Budapest) was a Hungarian actor and voice actor.

András Ambrus Sr.

András Ambrus Sr. (November 29, 1925 Joseni-) is a Hungarian actor, writer and voice actor. His children are called András Ambrus Jr. and Ákos Ambrus.

Iván Darvas

Iván Darvas (June 14, 1925 Tornaľa-June 3, 2007 Budapest) a.k.a. Ivan Darvas, Szilárd Darvas, Darvas Iván or Darvas Ivan was a Hungarian actor and voice actor. He had two children, Tatjána Ráchel Darvas and Olivér Benjámin Darvas.

György Miklósy

György Miklósy (May 30, 1925 Budapest-November 18, 2007 Budapest) also known as Miklósi György, Miklóssi György, Miklóssy György, György Miklósi or György Miklóssy was a Hungarian actor and voice actor. He had three children, Judit Miklósy, Katalin Miklósy and Anna Miklósy.

István Velenczei

István Velenczei (March 7, 1925 Budapest-April 19, 2006) also known as Velencei István, Velencey István, Velenczei István or Velenczey István was a Hungarian actor.

Sándor Szoboszlai

Sándor Szoboszlai (March 22, 1925 Dömös-January 4, 2013 Veszprém) also known as Sándor Szoboszlay, Szoboszlay Sándor or Szobi was a Hungarian actor.

András Kovács

András Kovács (June 20, 1925 Vultureni, Cluj-) is a Hungarian film director, screenwriter, television director and actor. He has one child, András Bálint Kovács.

István Szatmári

István Szatmári (July 30, 1925 Budapest-December 28, 1988 Budapest) also known as István Szathmáry, Szathmári István, Szathmáry István or Szatmáry István was a Hungarian actor and voice actor.

Tibor Liska

Tibor Liska (October 25, 1925 Békéscsaba-July 15, 1994 Budapest) was a Hungarian actor. His children are called Tibor F. Liska, Tibor Peter and Tibor Bence.

Lehel Ohidy

Lehel Ohidy (July 11, 1925 Budapest-January 1, 1977 Budapest) was a Hungarian actor.
