Turkish actresses born in 1963

Here are 7 famous actresses from Turkey were born in 1963:

Yaprak Özdemiroğlu

Yaprak Özdemiroğlu (August 14, 1963 Ankara-) otherwise known as Yaprak Ozdemiroglu is a Turkish actor and ballet dancer.

Hülya Avşar

Hülya Avşar (October 10, 1963 Edremit-) a.k.a. Hulya Avsar, Avşar, Hülya or Avşar Kızı is a Turkish singer, actor, businessperson, fashion designer, magazine editor, tennis player and talk show host. She has one child, Zehra Çilingiroğlu.

Ahu Tuğba

Çiğdem Mizrahi

Terri Youngblood

Nalan Yavuz
