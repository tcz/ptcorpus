Spanish movie actors born in the year 1924

Here are 7 famous actors from Spain were born in 1924:

Eduardo Fajardo

Juanito Navarro

Juanito Navarro (July 8, 1924 Madrid-January 10, 2011 Madrid) also known as Navarro or Juan Navarro Rubinos was a Spanish actor.

José Manuel Martín

Martín Pérez, J.M. Martín, José Manuel Martí, Jose Manuel Martín, Jose Manuel Martin, J. Manuel Martín, José M. Martin, Jose M. Martín or José M. Enrique Rambal

Enrique Rambal (May 8, 1924 Valencia-December 15, 1971 Mexico City) also known as Enrique Rambal Sacia or Enrique Rambal Jr. was a Spanish actor and television director. He had four children, Virginia Rambal, Rebeca Rambal, Enrique Rambal and Mario Rambal.

Prince Alfonso of Hohenlohe-Langenburg

Prince Alfonso Maximiliano Victorio Eugenio Alexandro Maria Pablo de la Santisima Trinidad y todos los Santos zu Hohenlohe-Langenburg, Alfonso Maximiliano Victorio Eugenio Alejandro María Pablo de la Santísima Trinidad y Todos los Santos, Alfonso of Hohenlohe-Langenburg, Alfonso Maximiliano Victorio Eugenio Alexandro Maria Pablo de la Santisima Trinidad y todos los Santos Hohenlohe-Langenburg, Alfonso Victorio Maximiliano Alejandro Eugenio Maria Paul of the Holy Trinity and All Saints Hohenlohe-Langenburg and Iturbe, Alfonso Victorio Maximiliano Alejandro Eugenio Maria Paul of the Holy Trinity and All Saints, Prinz Alfonso von und zu Hohenlohe-Langenburg or Alfonso Maximiliano Victorio Eugenio Alexandro Maria Pablo de la Santisima Trinidad y todos los Santos Prinz zu Hohenlohe-Langenburg was a Spanish actor and businessperson. Joaquín Pamplona

Joaquín Pamplona (November 8, 1924 León-November 15, 1981 Madrid) a.k.a. Joaquin Pamplona was a Spanish actor.

Juan Maján

Juan Maján (April 13, 1924 Malagón-November 4, 2014) also known as Maján, Juan Majan, Juan Majan Sosa, Juan Jose Majan, Juan José Maján, Juan José Majan or El Chulillo was a Spanish stunt performer and actor.
