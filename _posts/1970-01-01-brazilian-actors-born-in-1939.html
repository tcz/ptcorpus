Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1939

Here are 15 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1939:

Glauber Rocha

Glauber Rocha (March 14, 1939 Vitória da Conquista-August 22, 1981 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Glauber de Andrade Rocha or Pedro was a Brazilian film director, screenwriter, actor, film producer, writer and film editor. He had five children, Ava Patria Yndia Yracema Gaitan Rocha, Pedro Paulo Lima, Eryk Rocha, Daniel Jardim Gomes Rocha and Paloma Rocha.

Jonas Bloch

Jonas Bloch (February 8, 1939 Belo Horizonte-) is a Brazilian actor, radio personality, voice actor, film director and author. He has two children, Débora Bloch and Deni Bloch.

Fúlvio Stefanini

Fúlvio Stefanini (October 12, 1939 São Paulo-) also known as Fulvio Stefanini is a Brazilian actor. His children are called Fúlvio Filho and Leonardo Stefanini.

Sérgio Mamberti

Sérgio Mamberti (April 22, 1939 Santos, São Paulo-) is a Brazilian actor. He has three children, Carlos Mamberti, Duda Mamberti and Fabrício Mamberti.

Antonio Pitanga

Antonio Pitanga (June 13, 1939 Salvador-) also known as Antonio L.Sampaio, Antônio Sampaio, Antonio Luiz Sampaio, Antonio L. Sampaio, Antonio Sampaio, Antônio Luis Sampaio or Antônio Pitanga is a Brazilian actor. He has two children, Rocco Pitanga and Camila Pitanga.

João Batista de Andrade

João Batista de Andrade (December 1, 1939 Ituiutaba-) is a Brazilian film editor, screenwriter, film producer, film director, actor and television producer. He has two children, Fernando Andrade and Vinícius Andrade.

Carlos Vereza

Carlos Vereza (March 4, 1939 Rio de Janeiro-) otherwise known as Carlos Alberto Vereza de Almeida is a Brazilian actor. He has three children, Larissa Vereza, Diana Vereza and Luiz Sergio Vereza.

Perry Salles

Perry Salles (March 6, 1939 Rio de Janeiro-June 17, 2009 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Perilúcio José de Almeida was a Brazilian actor and film director. He had five children, Rafaela Salles, Rodrigo Salles, Renata Salles, Romeu Salles and Rômulo Salles.

Jair Rodrigues

Jair Rodrigues (February 6, 1939 Igarapava-May 8, 2014 Cotia) a.k.a. Jair Rodriguez or Jair Rodrigues de Oliveira was a Brazilian singer, actor, musician and businessperson. He had two children, Jair Oliveira and Luciana Mello.

Roberto Roney

Roberto Roney (January 13, 1939 Brazil-January 3, 2010 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Robert Roney was a Brazilian actor.

Luiz Pinto

Luiz Pinto (August 19, 1939 Sete Lagoas-August 23, 2012 São Paulo) was a Brazilian actor.

Harildo Deda

Harildo Deda (November 3, 1939 Simão Dias-) is a Brazilian actor.

Paulo Silvino

Paulo Silvino (July 27, 1939 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Paulo Ricardo Campos Silvino or Silvino Júnior is a Brazilian actor and screenwriter. He has three children, Flávio Silvino, João Paulo Silvino and Isabela Silvino.

Emílio Di Biasi

Emílio Di Biasi (May 29, 1939 São Paulo-) otherwise known as Costabile Emílio Di Biasi, Emilio Di Biasi, Emilio d'Biasi or Emílio d'Biasi is a Brazilian actor and television director.

Carlos Leonam
