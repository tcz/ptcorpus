Thai movie stars born in 1970

Here are 7 famous actors from Thailand were born in 1970:

Jetrin Wattanasin

Jetrin Wattanasin (October 28, 1970 Bangkok-) also known as เจตริน วรรธนะสิน or J Jetrin is a Thai musician, record producer, singer-songwriter, actor and dancer. His children are called Jidarin Na Lamliang, Jaonai Wattanasin, Jaokhun Wattanasin and Jaosamut Wattanasin.

Krissada Sukosol Clapp

Krissada Sukosol Clapp (December 26, 1970-) also known as Krissada Sukosol Clapp, Noi S. Clapp, Noi Pru, Noi, Noi Wongpru or Krissada Terrence is a Thai actor, singer, dancer and film director.

Thanayong Wongtrakul

Thanayong Wongtrakul (November 27, 1970 Thailand-) is a Thai actor.

Willy McIntosh

Willy McIntosh (April 4, 1970 Bangkok-) is a Thai actor. He has one child, Thadarit McIntosh.

Arnon Saisangchan

Arnon Saisangchan (June 6, 1970-) otherwise known as Poo Blackhead, Poo, Patapee Saifah or Anon Saisangchan is a Thai musician, singer, songwriter and actor.

Benjapol Cheuyaroon

Benjapol Cheuyaroon (September 6, 1970 Thailand-) is a Thai actor and singer.

Tatphong Phongtat

Tatphong Phongtat (January 4, 1970 Phitsanulok Province-) is a Thai actor.
