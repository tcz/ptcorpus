Indian movie stars born in 1944

Here are 7 famous actresses from India were born in 1944:

Sharmila Tagore

Sharmila Tagore (December 8, 1944 Hyderabad-) a.k.a. Ayesha Sultana Khan, Sharmila Tagore Khan, Ayesha Khan, Ayesha Sultana, Sharmila Khan, Begum Ayesha Sultana or Tagore is an Indian actor and model. She has three children, Saif Ali Khan, Soha Ali Khan and Saba Ali Khan.

Saira Banu

Saira Banu (August 23, 1944 Mussoorie-) otherwise known as Saira Bano, Saira Banoo, Saira, Mrs. Saira Banu, Sairabhanu or Sayra Banu is an Indian actor.

Tara

Tara (August 3, 1944 Imphal-May 19, 2007 Gauhati) a.k.a. Tahra, Thara or Tarah was an Indian actor.

Anjana Bhowmick

Anjana Bhowmick (December 30, 1944 Cooch Behar-) also known as Arati Bhowmik, Babin or Babli is an Indian actor. She has two children, Chandana Sharma and Nilanjana Sharma.

Zaheeda

Zaheeda (October 9, 1944 Mumbai-) also known as Zaheeda Sahay, Zahida, Zee, Zaheeda Hussain or Mrs. KN Sahay is an Indian actor. She has two children, Brajesh and Nilesh Sahay.

Tabassum

Tabassum (July 9, 1944 Mumbai-) a.k.a. Tabbassum, Tabussum, Baby Tabassum or Tabbasum is an Indian actor, presenter and film director. She has one child, Hoshang Govil.

Isla Blair

Isla Blair (September 29, 1944 Bangalore-) otherwise known as Mrs. Glover is an Indian actor. Her child is called Jamie Glover.
