Mexican actresses who were born in 1947

Here are 5 famous actresses from Mexico were born in 1947:

Ana Martín

Ana Martín (May 14, 1947 Guadalajara-) otherwise known as Ana Martin, Ana Beatriz Martínez Solórzano or Beatriz Martínez Solórzano is a Mexican actor and television producer.

Alicia Bonet

Alicia Bonet (April 26, 1947 Mexico City-) is a Mexican actor. Her children are called Juan Carlos Bonet, Mauricio Bonet, Gabriel Brook and Arturo Brook.

Verónica Langer

Verónica Langer (April 13, 1947 Argentina-) is a Mexican actor. Her child is called Pablo Aura.

Meche Carreno

Meche Carreno (September 15, 1947 Veracruz-) also known as Mercedes Carreño is a Mexican actor.

Socorro Bonilla

Socorro Bonilla (August 20, 1947 Camargo-) also known as María del Socorro Martínez Ortega or Socorro Martínez is a Mexican actor. She has two children, Leonor Bonilla and Sergio Bonilla.
