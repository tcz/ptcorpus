Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1977

Here are 9 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1977:

Deddy Mahendra Desta

Deddy Mahendra Desta (March 15, 1977 Surakarta-) a.k.a. Desta Club 80's, Desta Clubeighties, Desta or Desta Club 80s is an Indonesian actor, comedian, musician and presenter. His child is called Megumi Arrawda Sachi.

Tengku Firmansyah

Tengku Firmansyah (September 15, 1977 Bandung-) is an Indonesian actor, film director, screenwriter and presenter. His children are called Tengku Syaira Anataya, Tengku Omar Athallah and Tengku Keanu Zimraan.

Marcell

Marcell (September 21, 1977 Bandung-) also known as Marcell Siahaan is an Indonesian actor, musician and singer. His children are called Keenan Avalokita Kirana and Edga Ian Cook.

Edi Brokoli

Edi Brokoli (September 1, 1977 Bandung-) also known as Eddi Brokoli is an Indonesian actor, singer, presenter and musician.

Indra Bekti

Indra Bekti (December 28, 1977 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor, presenter and model. He has two children, Dafania Sahira Indrabekti and Anabell Eleanor.

Primus Yustisio

Primus Yustisio (August 17, 1977 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor, politician and model.

Oscar Lawalata

Oscar Lawalata (September 1, 1977 Pekanbaru-) is an Indonesian actor and fashion designer.

David Chalik

David Chalik (April 13, 1977 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor and presenter.

Denny Wahyudi

Denny Wahyudi (August 29, 1977 Bandung-) a.k.a. Denny Cagur is an Indonesian comedian, actor and television presenter.
