South Korean movie actresses born in the year 1952

Here are 6 famous actresses from South Korea were born in 1952:

Ahn In-sook

Ahn In-sook (October 18, 1952 Seoul-) otherwise known as An In-suk, In-suk Ahn or Ahn In-Suk is a South Korean actor.

Ginny Yang

Ginny Yang (April 22, 1952 South Korea-) is a South Korean actor.

Hwa-yeong Kim

Hwa-yeong Kim (March 26, 1952 South Korea-) also known as Kim Hwa-young is a South Korean actor. She has two children, Bae Doona and Bae Doo-han.

Nam Soo-Jeong

Nam Soo-Jeong (October 23, 1952 Yeosu-) also known as Su-jeong Nam, Nam Su Jeong, Nam Su-Jung, Nam Soo-jung or Nam Soojung is a South Korean actor.

Jong-rye Won

Jong-rye Won (July 28, 1952 South Korea-) a.k.a. Won Jong-rye is a South Korean actor.

Hee-soon Son

Hee-soon Son (September 16, 1952 South Korea-) also known as Son Hui-sun or Son Hee-soon is a South Korean actor.
