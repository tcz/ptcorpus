Danish movie stars born in 1969

Here are 9 famous actors from Denmark were born in 1969:

Thomas Vinterberg

Thomas Vinterberg (May 19, 1969 Copenhagen-) also known as Thomas Winterberg is a Danish film director, screenwriter, film producer and actor. He has three children, Nanna Vinterberg, Ida Vinterberg and Albert Charles Vinterberg.

Anders W. Berthelsen

Anders W. Berthelsen (September 28, 1969 Rødovre-) also known as Anders Wodskou Berthelsen, Anders Wodskou Bertelsen, Anders Berthelsen or Anders Bertelsen is a Danish actor and theatre director.

Claus Riis Østergaard

Claus Riis Østergaard (November 11, 1969 Vestbjerg-) also known as Claus Riis Ostergaard is a Danish actor.

Lars Ranthe

Lars Ranthe (August 26, 1969 Copenhagen-) is a Danish actor. His children are called Rigmor Ranthe and Betty Marie Ranthe.

Niels Martin Eriksen

Søren Hytholm Jensen

Mads Ole Erhardsen

Mads Ole Erhardsen (December 3, 1969-) is a Danish actor.

Thomas Hartmann
