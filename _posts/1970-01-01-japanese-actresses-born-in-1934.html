Japanese movie stars born in 1934

Here are 9 famous actresses from Japan were born in 1934:

Nobu McCarthy

Nobu McCarthy (November 13, 1934 Ottawa-April 6, 2002 Londrina) a.k.a. Nobu Atsumi or Nobu Atsumi McCarthy was a Japanese model, actor and theatre director. She had two children, Marlon McCarthy and Serena McCarthy.

Michiko Maeda

Michiko Maeda (February 27, 1934 Osaka-) is a Japanese actor.

Kyôko Anzai

Kyôko Anzai (September 27, 1934 Osaka-January 28, 2002 Tokyo) also known as Kyoko Anzai, 住野町子, 三橋町子, あんざい きょうこ or 安西 郷子 was a Japanese actor. She had one child, Tadao Mihashi.

Yôko Tsukasa

Yôko Tsukasa (August 20, 1934 Sakaiminato-) also known as Yoko Shoji, Youko Tsukasa, Tsukasa Yôko, Aizawa Yoko, Yoko Aizawa or Shoji Yoko is a Japanese actor and teacher. Her child is called Hiromitsu Aizawa.

Akemi Negishi

Akemi Negishi (March 26, 1934 Tokyo-March 11, 2008 Kawasaki) also known as Negishi Akemi, 根岸 明美 or ねぎし あけみ was a Japanese actor.

Michiko Ono

Michiko Ono (September 12, 1934 Osaka-) also known as Toshiko Hasegawa, Ono Michiko, Hasegawa Toshiko, おの みちこ, 小野 道子, はせがわ としこ or 長谷川 季子 is a Japanese actor.

Meiko Nakamura

Meiko Nakamura (May 13, 1934 Tokyo-) a.k.a. なかむら めいこ, Kôzu Satsuki, 中村 メイ子, Satsuki Kôzu, Nakamura Meiko, 神津 五月, 中村 メイコ, こうづ さつき or 中村五月 is a Japanese actor and voice actor. Her children are called Kanna Kôzu, Hazuki Kôzu and Yoshinosuke Kôzu.

Eiko Ando

Eiko Ando (May 8, 1934 Harbin-) is a Japanese actor.

Miyuki Takakura

Miyuki Takakura (March 28, 1934 Choshi-) is a Japanese actor.
