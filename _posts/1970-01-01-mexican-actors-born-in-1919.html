Mexican actors who were born in 1919

Here are 9 famous actors from Mexico were born in 1919:

Rico Alaniz

Rico Alaniz (October 25, 1919 Mexico-) also known as Americo Alaniz is a Mexican actor.

Rafael Baledón

Rafael Baledón (November 25, 1919 Campeche-May 6, 1994 Mexico) also known as Rafael Baledon, Rafael Baledón Cárdenas or Rafael Baledón padre was a Mexican actor, screenwriter, film producer, film director and writer. He had five children, Rafael Baledón, Leonor Baledón, Ana Laura Baledón, Lourdes Baledón and Lilia Baledón.

Antonio Aguilar

Antonio Aguilar (May 17, 1919 Villanueva-June 19, 2007 Mexico City) also known as José Pascual Antonio Aguilar Márquez Barraza, Atonio Aguilar y Antonio Aguilar, Toni Aguilar, Tony Aguilar, El Charro de Mexico, José Pascual Antonio Aguilar Márquez-Barraza, El Charro de México, Antonio Aguilar Barraza or The Horseman of Mexico was a Mexican singer, actor, songwriter, film producer, screenwriter, singer-songwriter and writer. His children are called Pepe Aguilar and Antonio Aguilar Jr..

Luis Aragon

Luis Aragon (August 19, 1919 Ejutla de Crespo-July 26, 1973 Mexico City) otherwise known as Luis Aragón was a Mexican actor and television director.

Alfonso Corona Blake

Alfonso Corona Blake (January 2, 1919 Autlán-January 21, 1999 Mexico City) a.k.a. Alfonso C. Black, A. Corona Blake or Alfonso C. Blake was a Mexican screenwriter, film director and actor.

Arturo Martínez

Arturo Martínez (January 21, 1919 San Luis Potosí-August 26, 1992 Mexico City) otherwise known as Arturo Martínez Chávez or Arturo Martinez was a Mexican actor, film director, screenwriter and film producer.

Genaro Hurtado

Genaro Hurtado (December 2, 1919 Morelia-) otherwise known as Genaro Hurtado Hernandez or Tiburón is a Mexican screenwriter, actor and cinematographer.

Marco Antonio Campos

Marco Antonio Campos (September 9, 1919 Tepito-February 19, 1996 Mexico City) a.k.a. Viruta, Marco Antonio Campos Contreras, Marco Antonio Campos 'Viruta' or Marco Antonio was a Mexican actor, comedian, singer, broadcaster and musician.

Enrique Llanes

Enrique Llanes (August 24, 1919 Otumba de Gómez Farías-September 18, 2004 Mexico City) also known as Enrique Juan Yañez González was a Mexican actor and wrestler. He had one child, Javier Llanes.
