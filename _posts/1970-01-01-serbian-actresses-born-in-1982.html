Serbian movie actresses born in the year 1982

Here are 7 famous actresses from Serbia were born in 1982:

Marija Karan

Marija Karan (April 29, 1982 Belgrade-) is a Serbian actor.

Hristina Popović

Hristina Popović (March 26, 1982 Belgrade-) otherwise known as Hristina Popovic is a Serbian actor. She has one child, Luca Navojec.

Emina Jahović

Emina Jahović (January 15, 1982 Novi Pazar-) also known as Emina Jahovic, Pop Princess, Penélope Cruz of the Balkans, Diva or Emina Jahović Sandal is a Serbian singer, actor and model. Her children are called Yaman Sandal and Yavuz Sandal.

Nada Macankovic

Nada Macankovic (August 5, 1982 Belgrade-) also known as Rukavica is a Serbian actor.

Marina Vodenicar

Marina Vodenicar (December 9, 1982 Pančevo-) is a Serbian actor.

Marija Omaljev-Grbić

Marija Omaljev-Grbić (December 5, 1982 Novi Sad-) also known as Marija Omaljev is a Serbian actor.

Mina Djukic

Mina Djukic (July 22, 1982 Sombor-) a.k.a. Mina Đukić or Mina Djukić is a Serbian actor, screenwriter, film director and film producer.
