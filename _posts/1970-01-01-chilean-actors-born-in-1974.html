Chilean movie stars born in 1974

Here are 5 famous actors from Chile were born in 1974:

Cristián de la Fuente

Cristián de la Fuente (March 10, 1974 Santiago-) a.k.a. Christian de la Fuente, Cristián de la Fuente Sabarots, Cristián De La Fuente or Christián de la Fuente is a Chilean actor, television producer, film producer, presenter, military officer and pilot. His child is called Laura de la Fuente.

Patricio Castillo

Patricio Castillo (February 4, 1974 Rancagua-) otherwise known as Patricio Fernando Castillo Espinoza or Patolo is a Chilean voice actor, singer-songwriter and actor. He has one child, Cassandra Castillo.

Felipe Avello

Felipe Avello (May 24, 1974 Concepción-) also known as Felipe Alejandro Avello Suazo is a Chilean actor.

Andrés Velasco

Andrés Velasco (June 11, 1974 Santiago-) also known as Andrés Velasco Reyes is a Chilean actor. He has one child, Alonso Velasco.

Felipe Ilabaca

Felipe Ilabaca (May 7, 1974 Santiago-) is a Chilean actor, composer, screenwriter and musician.
