French actresses who were born in 1918

Here are 11 famous actresses from France were born in 1918:

Lila Kedrova

Lila Kedrova (October 9, 1918 Saint Petersburg-February 16, 2000 Sault Ste. Marie) also known as Lila Kedrowa, Lila Kédrova or Elizaveta Nikolayevna Kedrova was a French actor.

Suzanne Flon

Suzanne Flon (January 28, 1918 Le Kremlin-Bicêtre-June 15, 2005 Paris) also known as Sizan Flon or Suzanne was a French actor.

Renée Le Calm

Renée Le Calm (September 18, 1918 France-) otherwise known as Renée Lecalm is a French actor.

Jacqueline Laurent

Jacqueline Laurent (August 6, 1918 Brienne-le-Château-December 18, 2009 Grasse) a.k.a. Jacqueline Suzanne Janin or Jacqueline Sylvère was a French actor.

Jacqueline Porel

Jacqueline Porel (October 14, 1918 Divonne-les-Bains-April 28, 2012 Passy, Haute-Savoie) also known as Jacqueline Renée Parfouru-Porel was a French actor. Her children are called Jean-Marie Périer, Jean-Pierre Périer-Pillu, Marc Porel and Anne-Marie Périer.

Moune de Rivel

Moune de Rivel (January 7, 1918 Bordeaux-) also known as Moune de Rivelle or Cécile Jean-Louis is a French actor.

Andrée Clément

Andrée Clément (August 7, 1918 Marseille-May 31, 1954 Paris) also known as Andrée Louise Boyer was a French actor. Her child is called Dominique Clément.

Patachou

Patachou (June 10, 1918 12th arrondissement-) also known as Henriette Ragon is a French singer and actor. She has one child, Pierre Billon.

Renée Faure

Renée Faure (November 4, 1918 Paris-May 2, 2005 Clamart) also known as Renée Faure Sociétaire de la Comédie Française, Renee Faure de la Comédie Française or Renée Paule Nanine Faure was a French actor.

Arlette Merry

Arlette Merry (March 18, 1918 14th arrondissement of Paris-) also known as Arlette Mery is a French actor and singer.

Jacqueline Plessis

Jacqueline Plessis (November 11, 1918 Le Havre-) is a French actor.
