Filipino actors who were born in 1929

Here are 5 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1929:

Eddie Garcia

Eddie Garcia (May 2, 1929 Sorsogon City-) is a Filipino actor and film director. He has one child, Lisa Ortega.

Max Alvarado

Max Alvarado (February 19, 1929 Manila-April 6, 1997 Metro Manila) also known as Maximo Pompling, Gavino Maximo Teodosio, Maxie Dudale, Pompleng or Pompling was a Filipino actor, dancer, singer and comedian.

Vic Silayan

Vic Silayan (January 31, 1929 Manila-August 30, 1987 Metro Manila) also known as Victor Silayan or Victor Payumo Silayan was a Filipino actor. He had one child, Chat Silayan.

Dencio Padilla

Dencio Padilla (May 7, 1929 Manila-October 10, 1997 Quezon City) also known as Prudencio Padilla, Tata Dens or Dencio Baldivia was a Filipino actor. He had five children, Dennis Padilla, Dencio Padilla, Jr., Richard Padilla, Gene Padilla and Glen Baldevia.

Joe Dennis

Joe Dennis (December 24, 1929 Osage City-) also known as Joseph Elliott Dennis Jr. is a Filipino actor.
