Filipino actresses who were born in 1965

Here are 5 famous actresses from Philippines were born in 1965:

Maricel Soriano

Maricel Soriano (February 25, 1965 Manila-) a.k.a. Maria Cecilia Dador Soriano, Diamond Star, Maricel, Maria, Taray Queen, Maricel Dador Soriano, Mary or Marya is a Filipino singer, actor, presenter, model and dancer. Her children are called Marron Soriano and Sebastien Soriano.

Jobelle Salvador

Jobelle Salvador (December 28, 1965 Manila-) also known as Jobel Salvador is a Filipino actor. She has one child, Mico Padilla.

Maila Gumila

Maila Gumila (December 25, 1965 Manila-) also known as Maristella Gumila is a Filipino actor.

Ruby Moreno

Ruby Moreno (September 22, 1965 Philippines-) is a Filipino actor.

Claudia Zobel

Claudia Zobel (February 27, 1965 Mandaue-February 10, 1984 Makati) also known as Thelma C. Maloloy-on was a Filipino actor.
