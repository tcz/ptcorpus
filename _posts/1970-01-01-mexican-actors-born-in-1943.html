Mexican actors who were born in 1943

Here are 8 famous actors from Mexico were born in 1943:

Arturo Ripstein

Arturo Ripstein (December 13, 1943 Mexico City-) also known as Arturo Ribstein, Arturo Ripstein y Rosen, Arturo Rossen, Arturo Ripstein Rosen or Arturo Rosen is a Mexican film director, screenwriter, actor, television director and author. He has one child, Alejandro Ripstein.

Enrique Guzmán

Enrique Guzmán (February 1, 1943 Caracas-) otherwise known as Enrique Guzman, Guzmán, Enrique, Enrique Sánchez Guzmán or Enrique Alejandro Guzmán Vargas is a Mexican presenter, singer and actor. He has two children, Alejandra Guzmán and Luis Enrique Guzmán.

Julián Pastor

Julián Pastor (October 18, 1943 Mexico City-) also known as Julián Pastor Llaneza or Julian Pastor Llaneza is a Mexican actor, film director, television director, screenwriter, film editor and film producer.

Rodolfo de Anda

Rodolfo de Anda (July 6, 1943 Mexico City-February 1, 2010 Aguascalientes) a.k.a. Rodolfo De Anda, Rodolfo de Anda S., Clint Warner or el niño Rodolfo de Anda was a Mexican actor, film director, screenwriter and film producer.

Octavio Ocampo

Octavio Ocampo (February 28, 1943 Celaya-) is a Mexican production designer, art director, film art director and actor.

Juan Ferrara

Juan Ferrara (November 8, 1943 Mexico City-) also known as Juan Félix Gutiérrez Puerta, Juan Guilmáin, Juan Felix Guilmain or Juan Félix Guilmain is a Mexican actor. He has two children, Juan Carlos Bonet and Mauricio Bonet.

Felipe Ehrenberg

Felipe Ehrenberg (June 27, 1943 Mexico City-) is a Mexican actor and writer.

Salvador Sánchez

Salvador Sánchez (October 28, 1943 Tehuacán-) otherwise known as Alfredo Salvador Sánchez Bolaños or Salvador Sanchez is a Mexican actor, film director, film producer and television director.
