Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1951

Here are 8 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1951:

Herson Capri

Herson Capri (November 8, 1951 Ponta Grossa-) a.k.a. Herson Capri Freire is a Brazilian actor. He has four children, Lucas Capri, Pedro Freire, Laura Capri and Luiza Capri.

Arrigo Barnabé

Arrigo Barnabé (September 14, 1951 Londrina-) also known as Arrigo Barnabe is a Brazilian musician, film score composer, actor, singer and composer.

Luiz Melodia

Luiz Melodia (January 7, 1951 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Melodia, Luiz, Luís Carlos dos Santos, Luis Melodia or Luiz Carlos dos Santos is a Brazilian songwriter, singer, actor and composer.

Gilberto Wagner

Gilberto Wagner (February 24, 1951 São Paulo-) also known as Gilberto Wagner Correa Pinto is a Brazilian film editor and actor.

Marcos Paulo

Marcos Paulo (March 1, 1951 São Paulo-November 11, 2012 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Marcos Paulo Simões was a Brazilian actor, television producer and television director. He had three children, Giulia Martins, Wanessa Simões and Mariana Simões.

Luis Guilherme

Luis Guilherme (August 18, 1951 São Paulo-) is a Brazilian actor.

Amado Batista

Amado Batista (February 17, 1951 Davinópolis-) a.k.a. Batista, Amado is a Brazilian singer, actor and entrepreneur.

Sérgio Stern

Sérgio Stern (October 3, 1951-) also known as Sergio Stern is a Brazilian voice actor and actor.
