Filipino actors who were born in 1948

Here are 5 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1948:

Tito Sotto

Tito Sotto (August 24, 1948 Manila-) also known as Vicente Castelo Sotto III, Sen. Tito Sotto, Tito, Tito Soto, Vicente "Tito" Sotto, Vicente Sotto III, Vicente "Tito" Castelo Sotto III, TVJ or Vicente C. Sotto III is a Filipino singer, comedian, actor, politician, presenter, musician, film score composer, songwriter and journalist. He has four children, Ciara Sotto, Gian Sotto, Diorella Sotto and Romina Sotto.

Johnny Delgado

Johnny Delgado (February 28, 1948 Manila-November 19, 2009 Quezon City) also known as Juan Marasigan Feleo, Juan Feleo or Josenaldo Mikael Delgado was a Filipino actor and writer. He had two children, Anna Feleo and Ina Feleo.

Conrad Poe

Conrad Poe (April 11, 1948 Manila-June 26, 2010 Meycauayan) also known as C.P., Conrad Mijares-Poe, Carlos Roldan, Carlo Roldan or Conrado Poe was a Filipino actor.

Roldan Aquino

Roldan Aquino (May 2, 1948 Alabat, Quezon-March 10, 2014 Metro Manila) otherwise known as Rolando Aquino or Rolando Desembrana Aquino was a Filipino actor.

Frank Rivera

Frank Rivera (February 28, 1948 Paete, Laguna-) a.k.a. Frank G. Rivera is a Filipino playwright, actor, production designer, screenwriter and film director.
