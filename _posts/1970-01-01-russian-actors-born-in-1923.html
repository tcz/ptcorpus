Russian actors who were born in 1923

Here are 11 famous actors from Russia were born in 1923:

Mikhail Pugovkin

Mikhail Pugovkin (July 13, 1923 Chukhlomsky District-July 25, 2008 Moscow) also known as Mikhail Ivanovich Pugonjkin, M. Pugovkin, Mikhail Ivanovich Pugon'kin or Mikhail Ivanovich Pugovkin was a Russian actor. He had one child, Elena Pugovkina.

Leonid Gaidai

Leonid Gaidai (January 30, 1923 Svobodny-November 19, 1993 Moscow) a.k.a. Leonid Iovich Gaidai, L. Gaidai or Leonid Gayday was a Russian film director, screenwriter and actor. His child is called Oksana Gaidai.

Nikolai Figurovsky

Nikolai Figurovsky (December 16, 1923 Chukhloma-June 14, 2003 Moscow) also known as Nikolai Nikolayevich Figurovsky or N. Figurovsky was a Russian screenwriter, actor and film director.

Yevgeny Vesnik

Yevgeny Vesnik (January 15, 1923 Saint Petersburg-April 10, 2009 Moscow) a.k.a. Yevgeni Yakovlevich Vesnik, Ye. Vesnik, E. Vesnik, Ye. Vestnik or Yevgeny Yakovlevich Vesnik was a Russian actor. His children are called Marina Vesnik, Yevgeny Vesnik and Anton Vesnik.

Viktor Khokhryakov

Viktor Khokhryakov (July 26, 1923 Ufa-September 20, 1986) a.k.a. V. Glazunov, V. Kokhryakov, V. Khokhryakov, Victor Khokhryakov or Viktor Ivanovich Khokhryakov was a Russian actor.

Adolf Ilyin

Adolf Ilyin (April 9, 1923 Leningrad Oblast-July 19, 1990 Moscow) a.k.a. A. Ilyin, Adolf Ilin or Adolf Alekseevich Ilyin was a Russian actor. He had two children, Vladimir Ilyin and Aleksandr Ilyin.

Yakov Segel

Yakov Segel (March 10, 1923 Rostov-on-Don-May 19, 1995 Moscow) also known as Яков Сегель, Yakov Aleksandrovich Segel or Iakov Segel was a Russian writer, screenwriter, film director and actor. He had one child, Aleksandr Aleshnikov.

Nikolai Yeryomenko St.

Nikolai Yeryomenko St. (June 20, 1923 Novosibirsk-July 1, 2000 Vitebsk) a.k.a. Nikolai Nikolayevich Yeryomenko, Nikolaj Eremenko, Nikolai Yeromenko, Nikolai Yeryomenko Sr. or Nikolai Yeryomenko was a Russian actor. He had one child, Nikolai Yeryomenko.

Gennadi Yudin

Gennadi Yudin (March 27, 1923 Moscow-November 13, 1989) a.k.a. G. Yudin, Ghennady Yudin or Gennadi Petrovich Yudin was a Russian actor.

Nikolai Pastukhov

Nikolai Pastukhov (May 13, 1923 Surazh-May 23, 2014 Moscow) also known as Nikolai Pastuhov, N. Pastukhov or Nikolai Isaakovich Pastukhov was a Russian actor.

Oleg Golubitsky

Oleg Golubitsky (June 7, 1923 Moscow-) otherwise known as Oleg Borisovich Golubitsky, O. Golubitsky or O. Golubitskiy is a Russian actor.
