German actors who were born in 1915

Here are 18 famous actors from Germany were born in 1915:

Ernst Schröder

Ernst Schröder (January 27, 1915 Herne-July 26, 1994 Berlin) also known as Ernst Schroder or Ernst Schroeder was a German actor and theatre director. He had one child, Christiane Schröder.

Curd Jürgens

Hans Christian Blech

Hans Christian Blech (February 20, 1915 Darmstadt-March 5, 1993 Munich) also known as H.C. Blech or Hans-Christian Blech was a German actor and soldier.

Georg Thomalla

Georg Thomalla (February 14, 1915 Katowice-August 25, 1999 Starnberg) a.k.a. Thomalla, Georg or Georg Valentin Thomalla was a German actor and voice actor.

Ullrich Haupt

Ullrich Haupt (October 10, 1915 Chicago-November 23, 1991 Munich) was a German actor.

Holger Hagen

Holger Hagen (August 27, 1915 Halle-November 16, 1996 Munich) was a German actor and voice actor.

Benny de Weill

Benny de Weill (March 6, 1915 Lübeck-December 17, 1977 Sylt) was a German actor and film score composer. His children are called Bernard de Weill and Kamille Lilly van Heel.

Georg Richter

Georg Richter (December 27, 1915 Berlin-May 10, 1972 Oslo) also known as Georg Alexander Richter was a German actor.

Erich Schellow

Erich Schellow (February 27, 1915 Berlin-November 25, 1995 Berlin) was a German actor.

Herbert Tiede

Herbert Tiede (March 3, 1915 Osnabrück-January 12, 1987 Munich) otherwise known as Herbert Salomon was a German actor.

Erwin Kohlund

Erwin Kohlund (December 23, 1915 Dortmund-March 1, 1992 Stäfa) was a German actor and theatre director. He had two children, Christian Kohlund and Franziska Kohlund.

Hans Quest

Karl-Heinz Kreienbaum

Karl-Heinz Kreienbaum (April 29, 1915 Hamburg-October 16, 2002 Germany) otherwise known as Heinz Kreienbaum, Karl Heinz Kreienbaum or Karlheinz Kreienbaum was a German actor and film director.

Til Kiwe
