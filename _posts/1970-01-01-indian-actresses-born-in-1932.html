Indian movie stars born in 1932

Here are 6 famous actresses from India were born in 1932:

Meena Kumari

Meena Kumari (August 1, 1932 Mumbai-March 31, 1972 Mumbai) also known as Mahjabeen Bano, Begum Mahjabeen Bux, Meena, Baby Meena, Meenakumari, Meenaji, Manju, The Tragedy Queen, Naaz or Mahjabeen was an Indian actor and poet. Her child is called Tajdar Amrohi.

Padmini

Padmini (June 12, 1932 Poojappura-September 24, 2006 Chennai) a.k.a. Padmini Ramachandran, Pappima or Natiya Peroli was an Indian actor.

Indira Bansal

Indira Bansal (December 3, 1932 Rawalpindi-) also known as Indra Bunsal, Indera Bansal, Indra Bansal, Indira, Indira Bhansal or Indra Bhansal is an Indian actor.

Miss Kumari

Miss Kumari (June 1, 1932 Bharananganam-June 9, 1969) also known as Thresiamma Thomas, Thresiamma or Kumari was an Indian actor. She had three children, Johny Thaliath, Thomas Thaliath and Babu Thaliath.

Purnima

Purnima (March 2, 1932 Mumbai-August 14, 2013) a.k.a. Poornima, Purnima Sen, Meher Bano, Purnima Das Verma or Meherbhano Mohammad Ali was an Indian actor. She had one child, Anwar Hashmi.

Malaya Sarkar

Malaya Sarkar (February 26, 1932 Kolkata-) is an Indian actor.
