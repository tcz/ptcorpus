His albums include VOCALIST, VOCALIST 2, VOCALIST 3, カガヤキナガラ, DEAR, Revolution, REALIZE, INTRO. Ⅱ, JUSTICE and Girl. Genres related to him: Kayōkyoku and Pop music.
