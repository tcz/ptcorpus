Filipino actors who were born in 1960

Here are 7 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1960:

Randy Santiago

Randy Santiago (November 26, 1960 Manila-) otherwise known as Randy Gerard Legaspi Santiago, Ratsky, Private Eyes, Mr. Shades, Kirat or Santiago, Randy is a Filipino singer, actor, songwriter, film producer, television director and entrepreneur. He has three children, Raphael Santiago, Ryan Santiago and Raiko Santiago.

Michael De Mesa

Michael De Mesa (May 24, 1960 Manila-) also known as Eduardo Mikael De Mesa Eigenmann, Michael Edward De Mesa Eigenmann or Michael Edward Gil Eigenmann is a Filipino actor, child actor, presenter and model. His children are called Geoff Eigenmann, Ryan Eigenmann and AJ Eigenmann.

Rody Vera

Rody Vera (December 5, 1960 Philippines-) a.k.a. Rudy Vera or Rodolfo C. Vera is a Filipino screenwriter, actor, playwright, singer and theatre director.

Von Flores

Von Flores (April 5, 1960 Malabon-) also known as Valentin Andres Tanga Flores IV is a Filipino actor.

Roderick Paulate

Roderick Paulate (April 4, 1960 Manila-) otherwise known as Roderick, Roderick Mendenilla Paulate or Roderick M. Paulate is a Filipino actor, politician, presenter and comedian.

Carlito Gonzaga

Carlito Gonzaga (February 5, 1960-) a.k.a. Carlito Bonoy Gonzaga is a Filipino actor. He has two children, Toni Gonzaga and Alex Gonzaga.

Dennis Roldan

Dennis Roldan (December 8, 1960-) a.k.a. Mitchell Gumabao, Denis Roldan or Mitchel Gumabao is a Filipino actor. He has six children, Marco Gumabao, Michele Gumabao, Katrina Gumabao, Margarita Gumabao, Mitchell Gumabao Jr. and Marielle Gumabao.
