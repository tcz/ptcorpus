Finnish actresses born in 1966

Here are 6 famous actresses from Finland were born in 1966:

Minna Aaltonen

Minna Aaltonen (September 17, 1966 Turku-) is a Finnish actor.

Milka Ahlroth

Milka Ahlroth (August 15, 1966 Turku-) a.k.a. Milka Tuuli Linnea Alroth. is a Finnish actor.

Susanna Indren

Susanna Indren (May 20, 1966 Helsinki-) a.k.a. Susanna Vasara or Susanna Indrén is a Finnish actor. She has two children, Alfred Akseli Hanski and Urho Kannon.

Johanna Kerttula

Johanna Kerttula (May 18, 1966 Helsinki-) also known as Johanna Emilia Kerttula is a Finnish actor.

Nina Jääskeläinen

Nina Jääskeläinen (March 27, 1966 Turku-) a.k.a. Nina Virpi Hannele Jääskeläinen is a Finnish actor.

Kaarina Hazard

Kaarina Hazard (February 1, 1966 Kuopio-) is a Finnish actor.
