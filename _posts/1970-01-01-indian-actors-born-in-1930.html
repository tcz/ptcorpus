Indian movie stars born in 1930

Here are 10 famous actors from India were born in 1930:

Kasinathuni Viswanath

Kasinathuni Viswanath (February 19, 1930 Repalle-) also known as K.Vishvanath, K. Viswanath, Viswanath, Kasinadhuni Vishwanath, K. Vishwanathan, Kalathapasvi, Kasi Viswanath, Kaasinathuni Viswanath, Kaisinadhuni Vishwanath, K. Vishwanath, Kashinadhuri Vishwanath, K. Vishvanath, Dr. K. Viswanath, Kasinathuni Viswanath, Kaashinathuni Vishwanath or Kasinadhuni Viswanath is an Indian film director, actor, screenwriter, writer and sound designer. His children are called Ravindranath Viswanath, Nagendranath Viswanath and Padmavathi Viswanath.

K. Balachander

K. Balachander (July 9, 1930 Nannilam-) a.k.a. K. Balchandra, K. Balachandra, Kailasam Balachander, K. Balchander, Iyakkunar Sikaram, K. Balachandran or Iyakkunar Sigaram is an Indian film producer, film director, screenwriter, actor, television director, conductor, teacher, playwright and writer. His children are called Prasanna Balachander, Kailasam Balachander and Pushpa Kandasamy.

Atma Ram

Atma Ram (August 21, 1930 Kolkata-November 5, 1994) also known as Atma Ram Padukone, Atmaram, Atma or Shri Atma Ram was an Indian film director and actor.

Mannava Balayya

Mannava Balayya (April 9, 1930 Chavupadu-) also known as Balayya, Mannava Balaiah, M. Balayya, Balaiah M., Balaiah or M. Balaiah is an Indian film producer, actor, writer and film director.

Anup Kumar

Anup Kumar (June 17, 1930 Kolkata-September 3, 1998 Kolkata) also known as Satyen Das, Anup Kumar Das, Master Anup or Anup Das was an Indian actor.

K. P. Ummer

K. P. Ummer (October 11, 1930 Kozhikode-October 29, 2001 Chennai) also known as Ummer or K.P. Oomer was an Indian actor. He had three children, Rasheed Ummer, Muhammed Ashraf Ummer and Mariyambi Ummer.

Bhaskar Roy Chowdhury

Bhaskar Roy Chowdhury (February 11, 1930 Chennai-August 4, 2003 New York City) a.k.a. Bhaskar Roy Chowdry, Bhaskar Choudhary, Bhaskar Chaudhari, Bhaskar Chaudhuri, Bhaskar, Bhaskar Roy Chaudhuri or Bhaskar Chowdhury was an Indian actor.

M. Balamuralikrishna

M. Balamuralikrishna (July 6, 1930 Sankaraguptam-) also known as M Balamuralikrishna (Dr.), Balamuralikrishna, Mangalampalli, Dr. Mangalampalli Balamuralikrishna, Mangalampalli Balamurali Krishna, Dr. Balamuralikrishna or Carnatic Classical is an Indian playback singer, multi-instrumentalist, actor, classical vocalist and composer.

Monu Mukherjee

Monu Mukherjee (March 1, 1930 British Raj-) also known as Manu Mukhopadhyay, Manu Mukopadhya, Monu Mukhopadhyay or Manu Mukherjee is an Indian actor.

Musari Krishnamurthy

Musari Krishnamurthy (March 10, 1930-March 16, 1985) also known as Musuri Krishnamurthy or Krishnamurthy was an Indian actor, film director and film producer. He had three children, Gurudutt Krishnamurthy, Jayasimha Krishnamurthy and Vishalakshi Krishnamurthy.
