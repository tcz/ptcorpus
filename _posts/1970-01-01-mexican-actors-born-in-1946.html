Mexican actors who were born in 1946

Here are 6 famous actors from Mexico were born in 1946:

Gonzalo Vega

Gonzalo Vega (November 29, 1946 Mexico City-) is a Mexican actor. He has three children, Zuria Vega, Marimar Vega and Gonzalo Vega Jr..

Rigo Tovar

Rigo Tovar (March 29, 1946 Matamoros-March 27, 2005) also known as Tovar, Rigo was a Mexican singer and actor.

Ruben Navarro

Ruben Navarro (August 5, 1946 Mexico-) is a Mexican professional boxer and actor.

Lico Reyes

Lico Reyes (April 4, 1946 Durango-) otherwise known as Federico Reyes Blanco or Frederick B. Reyes is a Mexican actor.

Corey Rand

Corey Rand (April 29, 1946 Mexico-December 22, 2002 United States of America) also known as Corey David Rand or Cory Rand was a Mexican actor.

Fernando Balzaretti

Fernando Balzaretti (June 10, 1946 Mexico City-September 5, 1998 Mexico City) otherwise known as Fernando Balzareti was a Mexican actor.
