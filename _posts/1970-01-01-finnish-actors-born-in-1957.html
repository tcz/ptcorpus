Finnish actors born in 1957

Here are 8 famous actors from Finland were born in 1957:

Aki Kaurismäki

Aki Kaurismäki (April 4, 1957 Orimattila-) also known as Aki Olavi Kaurismäki or Aki Kaurismaki is a Finnish film producer, film director, screenwriter, film editor and actor.

Seppo Pääkkönen

Seppo Pääkkönen (June 15, 1957 Kuhmo-) otherwise known as Seppo Paakkonen, Pääkkönen or Seppo Sakari Pääkkönen is a Finnish actor and voice actor. He has one child, Jasper Pääkkönen.

Pekka Valkeejärvi

Pekka Valkeejärvi (December 19, 1957 Helsinki-) otherwise known as Pekka Juhani Valkeejärvi or KOM-teatteri is a Finnish actor.

Mikko Kivinen

Mikko Kivinen (November 20, 1957 Helsinki-) also known as Mikko Juhani Kivinen or Lapinlahden Linnut is a Finnish actor, television director and screenwriter.

Tommi Auvinen

Tommi Auvinen (August 10, 1957 Tampere-) is a Finnish actor, television director, screenwriter and theatre director.

Tomi Salmela

Tomi Salmela (February 27, 1957 Helsinki-) otherwise known as Tomi 'Topi' Salmela or Tomi Salmela-Nikunen is a Finnish actor.

Sedu Koskinen

Sedu Koskinen (June 2, 1957 Pälkäne-) a.k.a. Seppo Koskinen, Mauno Seppo Antero "Sedu" Koskinen, Sedu or Seppo Mauno Antero ”Sedu” Koskinen is a Finnish restaurateur, businessperson, actor and film producer.

Tapio Rauvala

Tapio Rauvala (August 8, 1957 Saarijärvi-) is a Finnish actor.
