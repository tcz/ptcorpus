Indian movie stars born in 1938

Here are 11 famous actors from India were born in 1938:

Girish Karnad

Girish Karnad (May 19, 1938 Matheran-) also known as Girish Raghunath Karnad is an Indian film director, poet, playwright, actor, screenwriter and writer. His children are called Shalmali Radha Karnad and Raghu Amay Karnad.

Shashi Kapoor

Shashi Kapoor (March 18, 1938 Kolkata-) also known as Balbir Raj Kapoor, Sashi Kapoor, Shashi, Shashiraj, Padma Vibushan Shashi Kapoor, Shashi Raj, Shasha, Balbir Raj, Shashi Baba, Balbir, Padma Bhushan Shashi Kapoor, Shahsi or Balbir Raj Prithviraj Kapoor is an Indian actor, film producer and film director. He has three children, Sanjana Kapoor, Karan Kapoor and Kunal Kapoor.

Sanjeev Kumar

Sanjeev Kumar (July 9, 1938 Mumbai-November 6, 1985 Mumbai) a.k.a. Sanjeev, Harihar Zariwala, Shri Sanjeev Kumar, Haribhai, Godfather of Indian cinema, Doctor Kumar, Harihar Jariwala, Haribhai Jariwala, Sanjiv Kumar, Harihar Jethalal Zariwala or Harihar Jethalal Jariwala was an Indian actor.

Jaishankar

Jaishankar (July 12, 1938 Chennai-June 3, 2000 Chennai) a.k.a. Jai Shanker, Jay Shankar, Jaishanker, Subramaniam Shankar, Jai Shankar, Thennagathu James Bond, South Indian James Bond, Friday star, Jayashanker, Jayashankar, Makkal Kalingnar Jaishankar or Jai Sankar was an Indian actor and philanthropist. He had one child, Dr.Vijay Shankar.

Mac Mohan

Mac Mohan (April 24, 1938 Karachi-May 10, 2010 Andheri) also known as Mohan Makijany, Mackmohan, Macmohan, Mc. Mohan, Brij Mohan, Mac, Mack Mohan, Mac.Mohan, Mohan Makhijaney or Makijany Mohan was an Indian actor. He had three children, Manjari Makijany, Vinati Makijany and Vikrant Makijany.

Waris Hussein

Waris Hussein (December 9, 1938 Lucknow-) also known as Waris Habibullah is an Indian film director, television director, actor and screenwriter.

Sukhen Das

Sukhen Das (July 28, 1938 Kolkata-April 4, 2004 Kolkata) a.k.a. Sukhendranath Das or Sukhen was an Indian actor, film director and screenwriter.

Suruli Rajan

Suruli Rajan (January 14, 1938 Periyakulam-December 5, 1980) also known as Surulirajan or Sankaralingam Ponnaiya Pillai was an Indian singer, actor and comedian. His children are called Shanmugavel Rajan, Kumaravel Rajan and Senthilvel Rajan.

Manoj Mitra

Manoj Mitra (December 22, 1938 Satkhira District-) is an Indian actor, playwright, theatre director and writer.

Ajay Mehta

Ajay Mehta (July 3, 1938 New Delhi-) otherwise known as Ajay Metha is an Indian actor.

Ved Bandhu
