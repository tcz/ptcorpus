Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1947

Here are 6 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1947:

Jopi Burnama

Jopi Burnama (July 23, 1947 Ambon, Maluku-) also known as Jopijaya Burnama, Jon Bierium or Yopi Burnama is an Indonesian film director and actor.

Hendra Cipta

Hendra Cipta (October 2, 1947 Yogyakarta-September 24, 2011 Ciledug) was an Indonesian actor.

Gito Rollies

Gito Rollies (November 1, 1947 Biak-February 28, 2008 Jakarta) otherwise known as Bangun Sugito was an Indonesian actor and singer. He had one child, Galih Satria Permadi.

Adrian Nugraha

Adrian Nugraha (March 28, 1947 Tasikmalaya-) also known as A. Nugraha is an Indonesian film editor and actor.

Mangara Siahaan

Mangara Siahaan (April 19, 1947 Pematangsiantar-) also known as Mangara M. Siahaan is an Indonesian actor, politician and journalist.

Anwar Fuady

Anwar Fuady (March 14, 1947 Palembang-) also known as Anwar Fuadi is an Indonesian actor and politician. He has five children, Fery Senopat, Tillya Mavalda, Silvy Meutia, Farouk Fuady and Moudy Ambhita Love.
