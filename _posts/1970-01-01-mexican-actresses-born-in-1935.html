Mexican actresses who were born in 1935

Here are 7 famous actresses from Mexico were born in 1935:

Rosenda Monteros

Rosenda Monteros (August 31, 1935 Veracruz-) a.k.a. Rosa Mendez, Rosa Méndez Leza or Rosenda Montero is a Mexican actor.

Alicia del Lago

Alicia del Lago (January 11, 1935 Pátzcuaro-) also known as Alicia De Lago or Alicia Lago is a Mexican actor.

Elsa Cárdenas

Elsa Cárdenas (August 3, 1935 Tijuana-) also known as Elsa Cardenas Renteria, Elsa Cardenas, Elsie Cardenas or Elsa Cárdenas Rentería is a Mexican actor.

Maricruz Olivier

Maricruz Olivier (September 19, 1935 Tehuacán-October 10, 1984 Mexico City) also known as María de la Cruz Olivier Obergh, María de la Cruz Olivier, María de la Cruz or Mary Cruz Olivier was a Mexican actor.

Luz María Aguilar

Luz María Aguilar (March 26, 1935 Ojinaga-) otherwise known as Luz Maria Aguilar, Luz Ma. Aguilar or Luz María Aguilar Torres is a Mexican actor.

María Eugenia Ríos

María Eugenia Ríos (August 4, 1935 Mexico City-) is a Mexican actor. She has four children, Óscar Bonfiglio, Gustavo, María Eugenia and Andrés Bonfiglio.

Elvira Quintana

Elvira Quintana (November 7, 1935 Montijo, Spain-August 8, 1968 Mexico City) otherwise known as Elvira Quintana Molina was a Mexican actor, singer and poet.
