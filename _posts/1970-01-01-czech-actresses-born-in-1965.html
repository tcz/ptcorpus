Czech movie actresses born in the year 1965

Here are 6 famous actresses from Czech Republic were born in 1965:

Paulina Porizkova

Paulina Porizkova (April 9, 1965 Prostějov-) also known as Pavlína Pořízková, Paulina E. Poriskova, Paulina Porizkova-Otcasek, Pavlina Porizkova or Pavlína Porízková is a Czech model, actor, screenwriter and film director. She has two children, Jonathan Raven Ocasek and Oliver Orion Ocasek.

Miriam Chytilová

Miriam Chytilová (June 21, 1965 Prague-) also known as Miriam Chytilova is a Czech actor and singer.

Alena Mihulová

Alena Mihulová (May 4, 1965 Brno-) also known as Alena Mihulová-Kachynová is a Czech actor. She has one child, Karolína Kachyňová.

Martina Menšíková

Martina Menšíková (February 20, 1965 Prague-) is a Czech actor.

Stanislava Jachnická

Stanislava Jachnická (August 18, 1965 Prague-) is a Czech actor. Her children are called Matyáš Jachnická and Kristýna Jachnická.

Zuzana Slavíková

Zuzana Slavíková (May 13, 1965 České Budějovice-) a.k.a. Zuzana Cisáriková-Slavíková, Z. Slavíková or Zuzana Slavíková-Cisáriková is a Czech actor.
