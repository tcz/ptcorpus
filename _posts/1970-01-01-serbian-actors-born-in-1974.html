Serbian movie actors born in the year 1974

Here are 6 famous actors from Serbia were born in 1974:

Nikola Đuričko

Nikola Đuričko (July 9, 1974 Belgrade-) also known as Никола Ђуричко, Nikola Djuricko or Djura is a Serbian actor, screenwriter and television director. He has two children, Nađa Đuričko and George Đuričko.

Milos Samolov

Milos Samolov (March 31, 1974 Belgrade-) a.k.a. Misa Samolov or Miloš Samolov is a Serbian actor.

Goran Marjanovic

Goran Marjanovic (August 28, 1974 Smederevo-) also known as Goran Marjanovic' or Goran Marianovic-Goki is a Serbian actor and screenwriter.

Andrej Sepetkovski

Andrej Sepetkovski (March 5, 1974 Aleksinac-) is a Serbian actor. His children are called Aleksej Šepetkovski and Larisa Šepetkovski.

Vladimir Rajcic

Vladimir Rajcic (May 7, 1974 Peć-) a.k.a. Vlad or Vlado is a Serbian actor, film producer, screenwriter and singer.

Ivan Djordjevic

Ivan Djordjevic (January 17, 1974 Kruševac-) also known as Ivan Dordević is a Serbian actor.
