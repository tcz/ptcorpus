French actors who were born in 1900

Here are 14 famous actors from France were born in 1900:

Jacques Prévert

Jacques Prévert (February 4, 1900 Neuilly-sur-Seine-April 11, 1977 Omonville-la-Petite) also known as Jacques Prevert, Prévert, Jacques, Henri Marc Jacques Prévert or Jacques André Marie Prévert was a French writer, screenwriter, poet, actor and film score composer. He had one child, Michèle Prévert.

Marcel Duhamel

Marcel Duhamel (July 16, 1900-March 6, 1977) also known as M. Duhamel or Duhamel was a French actor.

Max Elloy

Max Elloy (May 5, 1900 Paris-January 16, 1975 Paris) also known as Max Eloy was a French actor.

Robert Florey

Robert Florey (September 14, 1900 Paris-May 16, 1979 Santa Monica) also known as Florian Roberts was a French actor, film director, screenwriter, journalist and television director.

Jean Nohain

Jean Nohain (February 16, 1900 9th arrondissement of Paris-January 25, 1981 16th arrondissement) also known as Jean-Marie Legrand or Jaboune was a French screenwriter, actor, songwriter, television producer, television director and soldier. He had one child, Dominique Nohain.

Pierre Mingand

Pierre Mingand (June 2, 1900 Besançon-August 19, 1982 Agen) also known as Joseph Yves Pierre Magnin was a French actor.

Fernand Rauzéna

Fernand Rauzéna (September 24, 1900 Rennes-August 20, 1976 Dijon) a.k.a. Rauzéna, Fernand Eugène Daniel Nazerau, F. Rauzena, Rauzena, Fernand Rauzena or Fernand Rauzenas was a French actor.

Camille Guérini

Camille Guérini (June 29, 1900 Lorient-April 15, 1963 Clichy) a.k.a. Camille Adolphe Georges Le Pape, Camille Guerini, Guérini or Guériny was a French actor.

Robert Le Vigan

Robert Le Vigan (January 7, 1900 Paris-October 12, 1972 Tandil) otherwise known as Robert-Charles-Alexandre Coquillaud, R. Le Vigan, Le Vigan, Robert Charles Alexandre Coquillaud or Robert Levigan was a French actor.

Edmond Beauchamp

Edmond Beauchamp (March 3, 1900 Montpellier-June 3, 1985 Saint-Brieuc) a.k.a. Beauchamp was a French actor.

André Daven

André Daven (March 16, 1900 Paris-November 17, 1981 Paris) also known as Andre Daven was a French actor and film producer.

Michel Duran

Michel Duran (April 22, 1900 Lyon-February 18, 1994 Rambouillet) also known as Michel Mourguet or Michel Durand was a French actor and screenwriter.

Jacques Chabannes

Jacques Chabannes (October 13, 1900 Bordeaux-June 16, 1994 Issy-les-Moulineaux) was a French actor, screenwriter, film producer, film director, journalist, presenter and novelist.

Roger Bourdin

Roger Bourdin (June 14, 1900 Levallois-Perret-September 14, 1973 Paris) was a French actor, opera singer and teacher.
