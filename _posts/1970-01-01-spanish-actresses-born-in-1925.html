Spanish movie actresses born in the year 1925

Here are 7 famous actresses from Spain were born in 1925:

Aurora Bautista

Aurora Bautista (October 15, 1925 Province of Valladolid-August 27, 2012 Madrid) a.k.a. Aura Batis, Aurora Batista, Aurora Battista, Aurora Bautista Zunel or Aurora Bautista Zúmel was a Spanish actor. Her child is called Hernan Cristerna Baptist.

Amparo Rivelles

Amparo Rivelles (February 11, 1925 Madrid-November 7, 2013 Madrid) a.k.a. Virgen de los Desamparados Rivelles Ladrón de Guevara, Amparito Rivelles or María Amparo Rivelles y Ladrón de Guevara was a Spanish actor. She had one child, María Fernanda Rivelles.

Nadala Batiste

Nadala Batiste (December 26, 1925 Barcelona-) a.k.a. Nadala Batista or Nadala Batiste i Llorens is a Spanish actor.

Asunción Balaguer

Asunción Balaguer (November 8, 1925 Barcelona-) a.k.a. Assumpció Balaguer, Asunción Balaguer Golobart, María Asunción Balaguer or Asuncion Balaguer is a Spanish actor. Her children are called Benito Rabal and Teresa Rabal.

María Asquerino

María Asquerino (November 25, 1925 Madrid-February 27, 2013 Madrid) also known as Maruja Asquerino, Maruja Asqueino, María Serrano Muro or Maria Asquerino was a Spanish actor.

Josefina Güell

Josefina Güell (April 9, 1925 Barcelona-) is a Spanish actor.

Maya Plisetskaya

Maya Plisetskaya (November 20, 1925 Moscow-) also known as Maya Mikhailovna Plisetskaya, Majja Michajlovna Pliseckaja, Maya Mikhaylovna Plisetskaya, Maija Plisetskaja, Maia Plissetskaia, M. Plisetskaya or Maïa Plissetskaïa is a Spanish ballet dancer, actor, ballet master, choreographer and costume designer.
