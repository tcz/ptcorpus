Argentine actors who were born in 1943

Here are 13 famous actors from Argentina were born in 1943:

Víctor Laplace

Víctor Laplace (May 30, 1943 Tandil-) a.k.a. Victor Laplace or Víctor Andrés Laplace is an Argentine actor, film director and screenwriter. He has one child, Damián Laplace.

Emilio Disi

Emilio Disi (August 2, 1943 San Cristóbal, Buenos Aires-) a.k.a. Emilio Roberto Parada is an Argentine actor.

Carlos Roffé

Carlos Roffé (August 1, 1943 Buenos Aires-December 31, 2005 Buenos Aires) also known as Carlos Alberto Roffe was an Argentine actor.

Víctor Bó

Víctor Bó (April 8, 1943 Argentina-) also known as Víctor Armando Bó or Víctor Bó is an Argentine actor and film producer. His child is called Armando Bo.

José Pablo Feinmann

José Pablo Feinmann (March 10, 1943 Buenos Aires-) also known as J.P. Feinmann or JPF is an Argentine playwright, writer, presenter, philosopher and actor. He has two children, Verónica Feinmann and Virginia Feinmann.

Axel Pauls

Axel Pauls (March 30, 1943 Buenos Aires-October 24, 2009 Buenos Aires) also known as Axel Harding was an Argentine actor and film producer. His children are called Alan Pauls, Cristian Pauls, Gastón Pauls, Nicolás Pauls and Ana Pauls.

Héctor Malamud

Héctor Malamud (August 9, 1943 Buenos Aires-December 17, 2008 Buenos Aires) also known as Héctor Adolfo Malamud was an Argentine actor.

Adolfo Aristarain

Adolfo Aristarain (October 19, 1943 Buenos Aires-) otherwise known as Adolfo M. Aristarain y Taburri or "A master filmmaker" is an Argentine film director, screenwriter, actor, film producer and television director.

Hugo Arana

Hugo Arana (July 23, 1943 Buenos Aires-) also known as Ricardo Hugo Arana is an Argentine actor. He has one child, Juan Gonzalo Arana.

Eduardo Rodrigo

Eduardo Rodrigo (June 15, 1943 San Juan Province, Argentina-) is an Argentine actor and film score composer. His children are called María Rabal and Luis Rodrigo Rabal.

Arturo Bonín

Arturo Bonín (November 11, 1943 Villa Urquiza-) a.k.a. Arturo Bonin, Arturo José Bonini or Arturo Jose Bonini is an Argentine actor. His child is called Mariano Bonín.

Pachi Armas

Pachi Armas (March 18, 1943 Buenos Aires-November 10, 2010 Buenos Aires) also known as Francisco Ángel Rivas was an Argentine actor.

Jorge Acuña

Jorge Acuña (July 4, 1943 Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine actor and film editor.
