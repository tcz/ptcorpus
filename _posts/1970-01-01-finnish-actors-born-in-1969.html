Finnish actors born in 1969

Here are 6 famous actors from Finland were born in 1969:

Petteri Summanen

Petteri Summanen (March 20, 1969 Säkylä-) also known as Jukka Petteri Summanen or Julmahuvi is a Finnish screenwriter, actor, television director, television producer and film director.

Janne Reinikainen

Janne Reinikainen (March 10, 1969 Kangasniemi-) otherwise known as Janne Olavi Reinikainen or Julmahuvi is a Finnish screenwriter, actor and television director.

Santeri Kinnunen

Santeri Kinnunen (August 20, 1969 Helsinki-) also known as Santeri Karl-Henrik Kinnunen is a Finnish actor.

Aleksi Mäkelä

Aleksi Mäkelä (November 20, 1969 Helsinki-) a.k.a. Tane Aleksanteri Mäkelä, Aleksi Makela, Alex Mäkelä, Tane Mäkelä or Tane Aleksanteri ”Aleksi” Mäkelä is a Finnish film director, television director, actor, screenwriter and television editor.

Jukka Rasila

Jukka Rasila (August 12, 1969 Oulu-) a.k.a. Jukka Jugi Rasila or Julmahuvi is a Finnish actor, screenwriter and voice actor.

Hannu-Pekka Björkman

Hannu-Pekka Björkman (February 11, 1969 Kannonkoski-) a.k.a. Hannu-Pekka Juhani Björkman or H-P Björkman is a Finnish actor and teacher. He has two children, Eliel Björkman and Luukas Björkman.
