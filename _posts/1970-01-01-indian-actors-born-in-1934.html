Indian movie stars born in 1934

Here are 11 famous actors from India were born in 1934:

Vijay Anand

Vijay Anand (January 22, 1934 Gurdaspur-February 23, 2004 Mumbai) also known as Goldie, Goldie Anand, Vijay or Vijay Anand was an Indian actor, film director, screenwriter, film editor and film producer. He had one child, Vaibhav Anand.

Ruskin Bond

Ruskin Bond (May 19, 1934 Kasauli-) is an Indian writer, actor, poet, author and songwriter.

Gulzar

Gulzar (August 18, 1934 Dina, Pakistan-) also known as Gulzar Saab, Gulzar Bhai, Gulzar Deenvi, Sampooran Singh Kalra or Gulazara is an Indian poet, lyricist, film director, screenwriter, actor, mechanic, television director, film score composer, film producer and author. He has one child, Meghna Gulzar.

Cho Ramaswamy

Cho Ramaswamy (October 5, 1934 Mylapore-) otherwise known as Cho, Srinivasa Iyer Ramaswamy or Cho Ramasawamy is an Indian journalist, playwright, politician, comedian, commentator, actor, lawyer and editor. His children are called Sriram Ramaswamy and Sindhuja.

K. Balaji

K. Balaji (August 5, 1934 Chennai-May 2, 2009 Chennai) also known as Balajee, Balaje, Balaji, K. Balaji Late or K. Balajee was an Indian film producer and actor. His children are called Suresh Balaje, Suchitra Mohanlal and Sujatha Balaji.

Achyut Potdar

Achyut Potdar (August 22, 1934 Jabalpur-) also known as Achut Puddar, Achut Poddar, Achyut Poddar, Achut Potdar or Achut Potdarn is an Indian actor.

Sujit Kumar

Sujit Kumar (February 7, 1934 Varanasi-February 5, 2010 Mumbai) also known as Sujeet Kumar, Sujit or Shamsher Singh was an Indian actor and film producer. He had two children, Heena Kumar and Jatin Kumar.

Sarat Pujari

Sarat Pujari (August 8, 1934 Khandapada-May 12, 2014 Bhubaneswar) was an Indian actor, film director, screenwriter and film producer. He had one child, Soumen Pujari.

Ramesh Mehta

Ramesh Mehta (June 23, 1934 Gondal, India-May 11, 2012 Rajkot) was an Indian actor and film producer.

Shyamanand Jalan

Shyamanand Jalan (January 13, 1934 Muzaffarpur-May 24, 2010 Kolkata) was an Indian actor and theatre director.

Chi. Udaya Shankar

Chi. Udaya Shankar (February 18, 1934-July 1, 1993) a.k.a. Chi. Udayashankar, Chi Udayashankar, Sahitya Ratna or Chi Udayshankar was an Indian screenwriter, lyricist, actor and writer. He had one child, Chi. Guru Dutt.
