Turkish actors born in 1974

Here are 14 famous actors from Turkey were born in 1974:

Mehmet Emin Toprak

Kenan İmirzalıoğlu (June 18, 1974 Üçem-) also known as Kenan Imirzalioglu is a Turkish actor and model.
