Greek movie stars born in 1966

Here are 9 famous actresses from Greece were born in 1966:

Sophia Aliberti

Sophia Aliberti (March 3, 1966 Corfu-) otherwise known as Sophia Parlama or Sofia Aliberti is a Greek singer, model, actor and presenter.

Daniela Amavia

Daniela Amavia (March 4, 1966 Athens-) a.k.a. Daniela Elle or Daniela Lunkewitz is a Greek model and actor.

Athina Rachel Tsangari

Athina Rachel Tsangari (April 2, 1966 Athens-) also known as Rachel Reinhardt, Athina Tsangari, Rachel Tsangari, Rachael Reinhardt, Rachael Tsangari-Reinhardt or A R Tsangari is a Greek film producer, film director, screenwriter, film editor, projection designer, actor, television director and cinematographer.

Bessie Malfa

Bessie Malfa (September 26, 1966-) also known as Bessy Malfa, Panayota Malfa, Panayota Vasilopoulou or Panagiota Vasilopoulou is a Greek actor. She has three children, Olga Skiadaresi, Nikolitsa Skiadaresi and Giangos Skiadaresis.

Renia Louizidou

Renia Louizidou (July 18, 1966 Thessaloniki-) also known as Irini Louizidou or Argiro Louizides is a Greek actor.

Vassia Panagopoulou

Vassia Panagopoulou (October 6, 1966 Mytilene-) otherwise known as Vasia Panagopoulou is a Greek actor.

Vanna Barba

Vanna Barba (March 13, 1966 Ioannina-) also known as Vassiliki Barba or Vana Barba is a Greek actor. Her child is called Faidra-Theodora Barba.

Ioanna Tsirigouli

Ioanna Tsirigouli (December 2, 1966 Athens-) is a Greek actor.

Margarita Varlamou
