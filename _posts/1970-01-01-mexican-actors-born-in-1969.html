Mexican actors who were born in 1969

Here are 8 famous actors from Mexico were born in 1969:

Luis García Postigo (June 1, 1969 Mexico City-) also known as Luis Garcia Postigo, Luis García, el niño artillero or The Artillery Boy is a Mexican football player and actor.

Juan José Saravia

Juan José Saravia (October 8, 1969 Mexico City-) also known as Juan Jose Sarabia is a Mexican cinematographer and actor.

Julio Mannino

Julio Mannino (May 20, 1969 Toluca-) also known as Julio Giovanni Mannino Pichardo is a Mexican actor.

Pedro Sicard

Pedro Sicard (January 11, 1969 Havana-) a.k.a. Pedro Luis Sicard Sánchez is a Mexican actor.

Diego Schoening

Diego Schoening (August 5, 1969 Mexico City-) is a Mexican actor, singer and television presenter.

Israel Jaitovich

Israel Jaitovich (August 12, 1969-) is a Mexican actor.

Esteban Soberanes

Esteban Soberanes (March 15, 1969 Mexico City-) is a Mexican actor.

Pedro Fernández
