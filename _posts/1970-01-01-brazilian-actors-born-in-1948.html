Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1948

Here are 8 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1948:

Pedro Paulo Rangel

Pedro Paulo Rangel (June 29, 1948 Rio de Janeiro-) is a Brazilian actor.

Tonico Pereira

Tonico Pereira (June 22, 1948 Campos dos Goytacazes-) also known as Antônio Carlos de Sousa Pereira or Antônio Carlos de Souza Pereira is a Brazilian actor. He has four children, Daniela Pereira, Thaia Pereira, Antônio Nicolau Pereira and Nina Sofia Pereira.

Tony Ramos

Tony Ramos (August 25, 1948 Arapongas-) also known as Antônio de Carvalho Barbosa or Antônio Carvalho Barbosa Ramos is a Brazilian actor and poet. He has two children, Rodrigo Ramos and Andréa Ramos.

Emiliano Ribeiro

Emiliano Ribeiro (February 12, 1948 Petrópolis-July 10, 2011 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Emiliano Ribeiro de Almeida Neto was a Brazilian film director, film editor, screenwriter, actor and film producer. He had one child, Carla Dutra.

Marco Nanini

Marco Nanini (May 31, 1948 Recife-) otherwise known as Marco Antônio Barroso Nanini is a Brazilian actor, film producer, theatre director, comedian, dramaturge and theatrical producer.

Marcelo Picchi

Marcelo Picchi (October 8, 1948 São Carlos-) a.k.a. José Picchi, José Dionísio Marcello Salles Picchi or José Dionísio Marcelo Salles Picchi is a Brazilian actor. He has four children, Thiago Picchi, Diogo Picchi, Tadeu Picchi and Ciro Picchi.

Walter Breda

Walter Breda (December 25, 1948 Recife-) is a Brazilian actor and voice actor.

Kito Junqueira

Kito Junqueira (May 15, 1948 São Paulo-) otherwise known as Heráclito Gomes Pizano is a Brazilian actor and camera operator. He has one child, Natalia Alvarez Pizano.
