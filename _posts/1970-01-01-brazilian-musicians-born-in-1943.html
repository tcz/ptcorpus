Brazilian musicians born in the year 1943

Here are 11 famous musicians from Brazil were born in 1943:

Eumir Deodato

Eumir Deodato (June 22, 1943 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Deodato, Eunir Deodato, Eumir Deodata, Eumir Deodato de Almeida or Eumir Deodato Almeira is a Brazilian jazz pianist, record producer and music arranger. His child is Kennya Baldwin.

His most recognized albums: Inútil paisagem, Live At Felt Forum - In Concert (Remasters), Artistry, In Concert, Whirlwinds, First Cuckoo, Very Together, Love Island, Night Cruiser and Happy Hour. Genres: Film score, Jazz and Rhythm and blues.

Marília Pêra

Marília Pêra (January 22, 1943 Rio de Janeiro-) also known as Marilia Pera, Marília Marzullo Pêra, Marila Pera or Marília Pera is a Brazilian actor, singer, theatre director, choreographer and theatrical producer. Her children are Ricardo Graça Mello, Esperança Motta and Nina Moreno.

Macalé

Macalé (March 3, 1943 Tijuca-) also known as Jards Macale, Jards Anet da Silva or Jards Macalé is a Brazilian actor, film score composer, composer and singer.

His albums include Jards Macalé, 4 Batutas &amp; 1 Coringa, Macao, Macalé Canta Moreira and Aprender a Nadar.

Turibio Santos

Turibio Santos (March 7, 1943 São Luís-) also known as Turibio Santos, Turíbio Santos or Turibio Soares Santos is a Brazilian film score composer, performer, guitarist and teacher. He has four children, Ricardo Assunção Santos, Manuela Assunção Santos , Julio Clemente Santos and Alberto Clemente Santos.

His albums: Villa Violão, Heitor Villa-Lobos, Um Violão Binacional: Agustin Barrios por Turibio Santos, Guitare Festival (collection bonsai), Le Charme de la guitare, Romanceiro, Johann Sebastian Bach visita a Mata Atlântica and Concierto de Aranjuez / Fantasía para un gentilhombre. Genres he performed include Classical music.

Dori Caymmi

Dori Caymmi (August 26, 1943 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Dori Cammy, Dorival Tostes Caymmi or Dori is a Brazilian musician, songwriter, singer, music arranger, record producer and film score composer. He has one child, João Vítor Caymmi.

His albums include Brasilian Serenata, Cinema, Dori Caymmi, If Ever..., Dois em Um, Kicking Cans, Tome conta do meu filho, que eu também já fui do mar ..., Contemporâneos, Influências and Rio Bahia. Genres: Bossa nova, Jazz, Música popular brasileira and Latin jazz.
