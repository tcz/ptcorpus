Hridayanath Mangeshkar

Hridayanath Mangeshkar (October 26, 1937 Mumbai-) a.k.a. Hridaynath Mangeshkar, Mangeshkar, Hridayanath, Balasaheb, Baal or Pandit Hridaynath Mangeshkar is an Indian singer, composer and film score composer. His children are Radha Mangeshkar, Vaijnath Mangeshkar and Aadinath Mangeshkar.
