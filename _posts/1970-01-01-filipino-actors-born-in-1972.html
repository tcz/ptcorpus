Filipino actors who were born in 1972

Here are 5 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1972:

Zoren Legaspi

Zoren Legaspi (January 30, 1972 Manila-) also known as Zoren is a Filipino actor, television director and film producer. He has two children, Cassandra Muñiz Legaspi and Maverick Muñiz Legaspi.

Romnick Sarmenta

Romnick Sarmenta (April 28, 1972 Quezon City-) otherwise known as Romeo Nicolas Sarmenta Tejedor, Romnick, Romnick Sarmienta or Dupong is a Filipino actor.

D-Styles

D-Styles (July 6, 1972 Philippines-) also known as Dave Cuasito is a Filipino musician and actor.

Garry Boy Garcia

Garry Boy Garcia (January 24, 1972 Manila-) also known as Garry "Boy" Garcia, Rodolfo "Boy" Garcia Jr. or Garry Clint Adriano Garcia is a Filipino actor.

Wally Bayola

Wally Bayola (May 3, 1972 Naga, Camarines Sur-) a.k.a. Walter James Bayola is a Filipino actor, comedian, presenter and singer.
