South Korean movie actresses born in the year 1961

Here are 8 famous actresses from South Korea were born in 1961:

Na Young-hee

Na Young-hee (September 20, 1961 Boeun County-) a.k.a. Bang Suk-hui, Na Yŏnghŭi, Na Yeong-hui, Pang Sukhŭi, Young-hee Na, Yeong-hee Nah, Yeong-hie Na, Nam Gyeong-Hee, Na Young Hee, Nah Yeong-hee, Na Younghee or Nah Young Hee is a South Korean actor.

Mi-kyeong Yang

Mi-kyeong Yang (July 25, 1961 Busan-) also known as Mi-gyeong Yang, Yang Mi-kyeong, Yang Mi-kyung, Yang Mi Kyung or Mi-kyeong Yang is a South Korean actor.

Bu-seon Kim

Bu-seon Kim (July 10, 1961 Namjeju County-) also known as Kim Boo-seon, Kim Bu-Sun or Kim Boosun is a South Korean actor. Her child is called Mi-so Lee.

Yang Geum-Seok

Yang Geum-Seok (January 22, 1961 Asan-) a.k.a. Yang Keum-Seok or Geum-Seok Yang is a South Korean actor.

Kim Hye-jung

Kim Hye-jung (February 14, 1961 South Korea-) a.k.a. Kim Hye-Jung, Hye-Jeong Kim, Hye-Jung Kim or Kim Hye-jeong is a South Korean actor.

Mi-Young Lee

Mi-Young Lee (March 16, 1961 South Korea-) a.k.a. Lee Mi-young is a South Korean actor. She has three children, Jeon Bo Ram, Jeon Wooram and Jeon Yu-bin.

Seo Kap Sook

Seo Kap Sook (July 16, 1961 Daegu-) also known as Kap-sook Seo, Suh Gap Sook, SEO Kap sook, Kap-suk Seo, Kap Sook Seo, Seo Kap-sook, Seo Kab-Sook or Seo Gap Suk is a South Korean actor.

Eun-Ha Lee

Eun-Ha Lee (March 29, 1961 Seoul-) a.k.a. Lee, Eun-Ha is a South Korean actor and singer.
