Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1958

Here are 12 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1958:

César Charlone

César Charlone (April 19, 1958 Montevideo-) a.k.a. Cesar Charlone is a Brazilian cinematographer, film director, screenwriter and actor.

Cazuza

Cazuza (April 4, 1958 Rio de Janeiro-July 7, 1990 Rio de Janeiro) otherwise known as Agenor Miranda Araújo Neto or Agenor de Miranda Araújo Neto was a Brazilian singer, songwriter, film score composer and actor.

Pedro Bial

Pedro Bial (March 29, 1958 Rio de Janeiro-) is a Brazilian journalist, television director, writer, presenter, television producer, actor, film director and film producer. He has five children, Théo Bial, José Pedro Bial, Ana Bial, João Bial and Marina Bial.

Edson Celulari

Edson Celulari (March 20, 1958 Bauru-) otherwise known as Celulari, Edson, Edson Francisco Celulari, Édson Cellulari or Édson Celulari is a Brazilian actor. His children are called Sophia Raia and Enzo Celulari.

Victor Fasano

Victor Fasano (September 2, 1958 São Paulo-) otherwise known as Victor Augusto Duarte Fasano or Victoriano Augusto Duarte Fasano is a Brazilian actor, film director, screenwriter, poet, theatre director and playwright.

Rickson Gracie

Rickson Gracie (November 20, 1958 Rio de Janeiro-) is a Brazilian actor. He has one child, Kron Gracie.

José Vitor Castiel

José Vitor Castiel (October 16, 1958 Porto Alegre-) also known as Jose Castiel, Victor Castiel, Zé Victor Castiel, Zé Vitor Castiel or Zé Vitor is a Brazilian actor.

Paulo César Grande

Paulo César Grande (February 11, 1958 Rinópolis-) also known as Paulo Cesar Grande is a Brazilian actor. He has two children, Carolina Grande and Pedro Grande.

Eduardo Dussek

Eduardo Dussek (January 1, 1958 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Eduardo Gabor Dusek or Eduardo Dusek is a Brazilian singer-songwriter, composer and actor.

Eduardo Lago

Eduardo Lago (August 31, 1958 Rio de Janeiro-) is a Brazilian actor.

Marcelo Madureira

Marcelo Madureira (May 24, 1958 Curitiba-) also known as Marcelo Garmatter Barreto or Marcelo Barreto Garmatter is a Brazilian actor, screenwriter and comedian. He has three children, Roberto Garmatter Barreto, Fernanda Garmatter Barreto and Patrícia Garmatter Barreto.

Cláudio Manoel

Cláudio Manoel (December 25, 1958 Salvador-) also known as Claudio Manoel Mascarenhas Pimentel, Claudio Manoel or Claude Mañel is a Brazilian film director, actor and screenwriter.
