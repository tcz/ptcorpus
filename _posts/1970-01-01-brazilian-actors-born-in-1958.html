Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1958

Pedro Bial

Pedro Bial (March 29, 1958 Rio de Janeiro-) is a Brazilian journalist, television director, writer, presenter, television producer, actor, film director and film producer. He has five children, Théo Bial, José Pedro Bial, Ana Bial, João Bial and Marina Bial.

Marcelo Madureira
