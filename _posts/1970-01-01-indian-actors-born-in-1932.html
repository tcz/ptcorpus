Indian movie stars born in 1932

Here are 7 famous actors from India were born in 1932:

Amrish Puri

Amrish Puri (June 22, 1932 Lahore-January 12, 2005 Mumbai) a.k.a. Amrish Lal Puri, Amrish Singh Puri, Mogambo, Mola Ram or Amrish Lal Singh Puri was an Indian actor, voice actor and playback singer. He had two children, Rajiv Puri and Namrata Puri.

Yash Chopra

Yash Chopra (September 27, 1932 Lahore-October 21, 2012 Mumbai) also known as Yash, Shri Yash Chopra, S. Chopra, Yash Uncle or Yash Raj Chopra was an Indian film director, film producer, screenwriter and actor. He had two children, Uday Chopra and Aditya Chopra.

Mehmood Ali

Mehmood Ali (September 29, 1932 Mumbai-July 23, 2004 Pennsylvania) also known as Mehmood Ali, Mahmud, Mehmoood, Mehmood Bhaijaan, Mahmood Ali, Mahmood, Mehmood Ali Khan, Mahemood Ali Khan, محمود علی), محمود, Mehmood/Malabari Mahmood, Mehmud or Mehmood was an Indian actor, film director, film producer, singer and screenwriter. His children are called Lucky Ali, Manzoor Ali, Masood Ali, Maqdoom Ali, Masoom Ali, Mansoor Ali and Baby Ginni.

Johnny Whisky

Johnny Whisky (February 8, 1932 Hyderabad-December 7, 2006 Mumbai) also known as Jhony Whisky, Johney Whisky, Johny Whisky, Johnny Whiskey, Jhonny Whisky, Sheikh Zennul Abdeen or Johnny Wiskey was an Indian actor.

Ameen Sayani

Ameen Sayani (December 21, 1932 India-) also known as Amin Sayani is an Indian actor and radio jockey.

Shilendra Kumar Singh

Shilendra Kumar Singh (January 24, 1932-December 1, 2009 Delhi) a.k.a. S.K. Singh or S.K.Singh was an Indian actor. He had two children, Kanishka Singh and Shashank Singh.

Nerella Venu Madhav

Nerella Venu Madhav (December 28, 1932 Warangal-) a.k.a. Venu Madhava Rao Nerella is an Indian actor.
