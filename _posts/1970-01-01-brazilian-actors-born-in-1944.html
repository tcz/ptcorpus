Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1944

Here are 11 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1944:

Chico Buarque

Chico Buarque (June 19, 1944 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Francisco Buarque de Hollanda, Chico Buarque de Hollanda, Julinho da Adelaide or Francisco "Chico" Buarque de Hollanda is a Brazilian writer, singer, poet, songwriter, playwright, guitarist, composer, film score composer, screenwriter and actor. He has three children, Sílvia Buarque, Helena Buarque de Hollanda and Luísa Buarque de Hollanda.

Frei Betto

Frei Betto (August 25, 1944 Belo Horizonte-) a.k.a. Frei Beto or Carlos Alberto Libânio Christo is a Brazilian politician, friar and actor.

Nelson Motta

Nelson Motta (October 29, 1944 São Paulo-) also known as Nelson Mota, Nelson Cândido Motta Filho or Nelsinho Motta is a Brazilian writer, journalist, record producer, songwriter, film score composer, screenwriter, actor and television director. He has three children, Esperança Motta, Nina Moreno and Joana Motta.

José Wilker

José Wilker (August 20, 1944 Juazeiro do Norte-April 5, 2014 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Jose Wilker or José Wilker de Almeida was a Brazilian actor, television director, film critic and film director. His children are called Isabel Wilker, Mariana Wilker and Madá Wilker.

João Acaiabe

João Acaiabe (May 14, 1944 Espírito Santo do Pinhal-) a.k.a. Joao Acaiabe or Joao Acayabe is a Brazilian actor.

Marcelo Gastaldi

Marcelo Gastaldi (October 20, 1944 São Paulo-August 3, 1995 São Paulo) also known as Maga was a Brazilian actor.

Ney Latorraca

Ney Latorraca (July 27, 1944 Santos, São Paulo-) also known as Antônio Ney Latorraca is a Brazilian actor.

Adriano Stuart

Adriano Stuart (February 19, 1944 Quatá-April 15, 2012 São Paulo) a.k.a. Adriano Roberto or Adriano Roberto Canales was a Brazilian actor, film director, screenwriter and television director.

Cláudio Tovar

Cláudio Tovar (July 9, 1944 Vitória, Espírito Santo-) also known as Cláudio Antonio Ferreira Tovar or Claudio Tovar is a Brazilian actor, set decorator and costume designer. He has one child, Beatriz Tovar.

Sérgio Santeiro

Sérgio Santeiro (December 20, 1944 Rio de Janeiro-) is a Brazilian actor.

André José Adler

André José Adler (June 21, 1944 Budapest-December 9, 2012 São Paulo) also known as Endre József Adler, Bandi or TouchDown was a Brazilian actor, screenwriter, film director, television director, television producer, sports commentator and journalist.
