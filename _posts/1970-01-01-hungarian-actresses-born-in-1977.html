Hungarian actresses born in 1977

Szonja Oroszlán (May 19, 1977 Budapest-) is a Hungarian actor.

Gabriella Szaksz (June 15, 1977 Dunaújváros-) otherwise known as Szaksz Gabi is a Hungarian actor.
