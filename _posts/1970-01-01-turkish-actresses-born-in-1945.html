Turkish actresses born in 1945

Here are 7 famous actresses from Turkey were born in 1945:

Türkân Şoray

Türkân Şoray (June 28, 1945 Eyüp-) a.k.a. Turkan Soray, Türkan Soray, Sultan or Türkan Şoray is a Turkish actor. She has one child, Yağmur Ünal.

Meral Çetinkaya

Meral Çetinkaya (March 5, 1945 Bursa-) also known as Meral Cetinkaya is a Turkish actor.

Gülsen Tuncer

Gülsen Tuncer (May 29, 1945 Adana-) otherwise known as Gulsen Tuncer is a Turkish actor.

Ayşen Gruda

Ayşen Gruda (November 30, 1945 Yeşilköy-) a.k.a. Aysen Gruda Erman, Aysen Guruda, Aysen Gruda, Ayşen Guruda or Ayşen Erman Gruda is a Turkish actor.

Emel Sayın

Emel Sayın (November 20, 1945 Sivas-) also known as Emel Sayin is a Turkish singer, singer-songwriter, musician and actor.

Işıl Yücesoy

Işıl Yücesoy (October 2, 1945 Kırklareli-) also known as Isil Yücesoy, Isil Yucesoy or Yücesoy, Işıl is a Turkish actor. Her child is called Meneviş Cılızoğlu.

Emel Göksu

Emel Göksu (February 25, 1945 Malatya-) is a Turkish actor.
