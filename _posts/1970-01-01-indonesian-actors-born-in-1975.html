Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1975

Here are 6 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1975:

Anjasmara

Anjasmara (November 13, 1975 Blitar-) a.k.a. Anjasmara Prasetya or Anjas is an Indonesian actor. He has four children, Sasikirana Zahrani Asmara, Arka Setya Andipa Asmara, Amanda Annette Syariff and Aldi Syariff.

Surya Saputra

Surya Saputra (July 5, 1975 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor, singer and presenter.

Verdi Solaiman

Verdi Solaiman (March 14, 1975-) is an Indonesian actor and businessperson.

Ibrahim Imran

Ibrahim Imran (May 31, 1975 Hong Kong-) also known as Baim is an Indonesian singer and actor. His children are called Sarah Ebiela Ibrahim and Dayana Zoelie Ibrahim.

Uya Kuya

Uya Kuya (April 4, 1975 Bandung-) is an Indonesian actor, singer, magician, comedian and presenter.

Irfan Hakim

Irfan Hakim (October 15, 1975 Bandung-) a.k.a. Irvan Hakim is an Indonesian actor, model, presenter and comedian. His children are called Aisha Maydina Hakim, Rakana Hakim and Raina Hakim.
