Spanish movie actresses born in the year 1930

Here are 7 famous actresses from Spain were born in 1930:

Chus Lampreave

Chus Lampreave (December 11, 1930 Madrid-) a.k.a. María Jesús Lampreave, Mª Jesús Lampreave, Chus or Maria Jesus Lampreave is a Spanish actor.

Emma Penella

Emma Penella (March 2, 1930 Madrid-August 27, 2007 Madrid) also known as Manuela Ruiz Penella was a Spanish actor. Her children are called Emma Piedra, Lola Piedra and Emiliana Piedra.

Carmen Sevilla

Carmen Sevilla (October 16, 1930 Seville-) also known as Carmelita Sevilla or María del Carmen García Galisteo is a Spanish singer, presenter, actor and dancer. She has one child, Augusto Jose Algueró Garcia.

Montserrat Carulla

Montserrat Carulla (September 19, 1930 Barcelona-) otherwise known as Montserrat Carulla i Ventura is a Spanish actor. Her children are called Vicky Peña, Isabel Peña, Marina Penya-Carulla and Roger Peña.

Irene Gutiérrez Caba

Irene Gutiérrez Caba (April 25, 1930 Madrid-July 4, 1995 Madrid) otherwise known as Irene Gutierrez Caba, Irene G. Caba or Irene Caba was a Spanish actor. She had one child, José Luis Escolar.

Marivi Bilbao

Marivi Bilbao (January 22, 1930 Bilbao-April 3, 2013 Bilbao) a.k.a. Mariví Bilbao, Mariví Bilbao Goyoaga, Maria Victoria Bilbao, Marivi Bilbao-Goioaga, María Victoria Bilbao Goyoaga, Maribi Bilbao, María Victoria Bilbao-Goyoaga Álvarez or Mariví was a Spanish actor. She had one child, Elvira Urkijo.

Mercedes Pascual

Mercedes Pascual (December 25, 1930 Madrid-) a.k.a. Mercedes Pascual Acuña is a Spanish actor. She has one child, Mercedes Olea.
