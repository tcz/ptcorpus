Indian movie stars born in 1914

Here are 8 famous actors from India were born in 1914:

G. P. Sippy

G. P. Sippy (September 14, 1914 Hyderabad-December 25, 2007 Mumbai) a.k.a. Gopaldas Parmanand Sippy or Gopaldas Parmanand Sipahimalani was an Indian film producer, film director and actor. He had three children, Ramesh Sippy, Suresh Sippy and Sunita Sippy.

Agha

Agha (March 21, 1914 Pune-April 30, 1992 Pune) also known as Ahga, Aga, Agah or Aghajan Baig was an Indian actor, film producer and singer. He had three children, Jalal Agha, Agha Ahmed and Shahnaz Vahanvaty.

A.K. Hangal

A.K. Hangal (February 1, 1914 Sialkot-August 26, 2012 Mumbai) a.k.a. A. K. Hangal, Avtar Kishan Hangal, Padmabhushan Avtar Krishna Hangal or Hangal was an Indian actor and tailor. His child is called Vijay Hangal.

Dhumal

Dhumal (March 29, 1914-February 13, 1987) also known as Dhoomal was an Indian actor.

Mikkilineni

Mikkilineni (July 7, 1914 Kolavennu-February 23, 2011 Vijayawada) a.k.a. Radha Krishna Murthy Mikkilineni or Mikkilineni Radhakrishna Murthy was an Indian actor.

T. S. Balaiah

T. S. Balaiah (August 23, 1914 Sundankottai-July 22, 1972) also known as S. Balaiah, Balaich, Tirunelveli S. Balaiah, T.S. Balaiah, T. S. Balaiah, Balaiyah, Balaiya, T.S. Balaiya or T.S.Balaiya was an Indian actor.

Anil Biswas

Anil Biswas (July 7, 1914 Barisal-May 31, 2003 New Delhi) also known as Biswas, Anil or Anil Krishna Biswas was an Indian playback singer, film score composer and actor. He had five children, Utpal Biswas, Amar Biswas, Shikha Vohra, Pradeep Biswas and Amit Biswas.

Roop K. Shorey

Roop K. Shorey (January 28, 1914 Quetta-June 3, 1973 Mumbai) also known as Roopkishore, Roop K. Shori or R.K. Shoery was an Indian actor, film director and film producer.
