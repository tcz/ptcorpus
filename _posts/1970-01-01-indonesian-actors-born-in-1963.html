Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1963

Here are 7 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1963:

Matt Miller

Matt Miller (July 23, 1963 Sumatra-) also known as Matthew Clayton Miller is an Indonesian actor.

Tio Pakusadewo

Tio Pakusadewo (September 2, 1963 Jakarta-) also known as Irwan Susetyo Pakusadewo, Tio Pakusodewo or Kanjeng Raden Tumenggung Irwan Susetyo Pakusadewo is an Indonesian actor. He has one child, Nagra Kautsar Pakusadewo.

Helmy Yahya

Helmy Yahya (March 6, 1963 Palembang-) is an Indonesian actor, presenter, screenwriter and television producer. He has four children, Rendy Faraitody Yahya, Amerigo Salim Yahya, Saskia Rachelly Yahya and Rahma Azzura Yahya.

Tukul Arwana

Tukul Arwana (October 16, 1963 Semarang-) a.k.a. Tukul Riyanto is an Indonesian actor, comedian and presenter. He has two children, Novita Eka Afriana and Wahyu Jovan Utama.

Hengky Tornando

Hengky Tornando (April 29, 1963 Cianjur-) a.k.a. Hengky Tarnando is an Indonesian actor. He has three children, Amira Nazelia, Khadafy and Farah.

Denny Sakrie

Denny Sakrie (July 14, 1963 Ambon, Maluku-) is an Indonesian actor, writer, screenwriter and radio personality.

Sandro Tobing

Sandro Tobing (March 17, 1963 Medan-) is an Indonesian actor and singer. He has one child, Togar Hatopan.
