Thai movie stars born in 1961

Penpak Sirikul

Savitree Samipak

Savitree Samipak (October 31, 1961-) a.k.a. Tong, Narumon Samipak or Sawitree Samipak is a Thai actor and presenter.

Rossarin Jantra (October 12, 1961-) a.k.a. Toom is a Thai actor.

Wassamon Wattarodom (December 23, 1961-) otherwise known as Mee is a Thai actor.
