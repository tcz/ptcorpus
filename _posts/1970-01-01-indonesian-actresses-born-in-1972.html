Indonesian movie actresses born in the year 1972

Here are 5 famous actresses from Indonesia were born in 1972:

Indy Barends

Indy Barends (January 15, 1972 Bogor-) also known as Indi Barends is an Indonesian actor and presenter. Her children are called Raphael Sarmanella and Manuel Tobias Sarmanella.

Maya Hasan

Maya Hasan (January 10, 1972 Hong Kong-) also known as Maya Christina Hasan is an Indonesian actor and harpist. Her children are called Alexandra Natasha Worotikan, Andrea Christina Worotikan and Austin George Worotikan.

Viva Westi

Viva Westi (September 21, 1972 Manokwari-) also known as Viva Westi Datoek or Viva Westi Datuk is an Indonesian actor, film director and screenwriter. She has one child, Damar Bintang.

Novia Kolopaking

Novia Kolopaking (November 9, 1972 Bandung-) also known as Novia Ainun Nadjib or Novia Sanganingrum Saptarea Kolopaking is an Indonesian singer and actor. She has four children, Ainayya Al-Fatihah, Aqiela Fadia Haya, Anayallah Rampak Mayesha and Jembar Tahta Aunillah.

Venna Melinda

Venna Melinda (July 20, 1972 Surabaya-) is an Indonesian actor, model and singer. She has two children, Verrell Bramasta and Athalla Naufal.
