Thai movie stars born in 1977

Here are 11 famous actors from Thailand were born in 1977:

Jesdaporn Pholdee

Jesdaporn Pholdee (June 5, 1977 Bangkok-) a.k.a. Tik, ติ๊ก, Jesadaporn Pholdee, Tik Jadsadaporn or Chetsadaphon Phondi is a Thai actor, model and presenter. He has one child, Jesbodin Pholdee.

Thongthong Mokjok

Thongthong Mokjok (January 25, 1977 Nakhon Ratchasima Province-) otherwise known as Thong is a Thai actor.

Theeradej Wongpuapan

Theeradej Wongpuapan (December 3, 1977 Bangkok-) a.k.a. Ken or ธีรเดช วงศ์พัวพันธ์ is a Thai actor and model. His children are called Khunatum Wongpuapan and Thippatai Wongpuapan.

Panomkorn Tangtatswas

Panomkorn Tangtatswas (June 6, 1977 Bangkok-) also known as Panomkorn Tungtatsawa or San is a Thai presenter, singer and actor.

Pakchanok Wo-Onsri

Pakchanok Wo-Onsri (April 11, 1977 Thailand-) a.k.a. Pakhachon Woonsri, Frank or Pakachon Voonsri is a Thai actor and television show host.

Ramit Romon

Ramit Romon (October 5, 1977 Bangkok-) is a Thai actor.

Suebsak Pansueb

Suebsak Pansueb (December 14, 1977 Ratchaburi Province-) is a Thai actor and police officer.

Vittaya Wasukraipaisan

Vittaya Wasukraipaisan (July 18, 1977 Bangkok-) a.k.a. Withaya Wasukraipaisan is a Thai actor.

Kett Thantup

Kett Thantup (May 27, 1977 Thailand-) also known as Kate Thunthup or Khed Tarntup is a Thai actor.

Suraphan Chaopaknam

Suraphan Chaopaknam (April 6, 1977 Thailand-) also known as A is a Thai actor.

Panu Suwanno

Panu Suwanno (January 9, 1977 Surat Thani Province-) otherwise known as Bhanu Suwanno, Punu Suwanno or Aou is a Thai actor.
