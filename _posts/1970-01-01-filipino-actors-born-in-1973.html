Filipino actors who were born in 1973

Here are 11 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1973:

Raymart Santiago

Raymart Santiago (July 20, 1973 Manila-) a.k.a. Raymond Martin Santiago or Raymond Martin (Raymart) Santiago is a Filipino actor and comedian. His children are called Rodrigo Santino Santiago and Sabina Natasha Santiago.

John Lapus

John Lapus (July 7, 1973 Manila-) a.k.a. John Lapuz, John 'Sweet' Lapus, John 'Sweet' Lapuz, Lovelyness, Jonathan Anthony Solis Lapus, Sweet or Jonathan Anthony Lapus is a Filipino presenter, comedian, actor and researcher.

Rico Blanco

Rico Blanco (March 17, 1973 Manila-) a.k.a. Blanco, Rico, Rico Rene Granados Blanco or Rico Rene G. Blanco is a Filipino singer and actor.

Keempee de Leon

Keempee de Leon (January 8, 1973 Manila-) also known as Joseph Joakim De Leon, Joseph Joachim or Joseph Joachim de Leon is a Filipino actor, comedian, singer-songwriter and presenter.

Epi Quizon

Epi Quizon (January 23, 1973 Manila-) also known as Epi, Epy Quizon, Jeffrey S. Quizon, Jeffrey Smith Quizon, Epy Quezon, Jeffrey "Epi" Quizon, Jeffrey Smith-Quizon, Jeffrey Quizon or Jeffrey Epi Smith Quizon is a Filipino comedian, actor and screenwriter. His children are called Sandro Quizon and Eleana Mishael Quizon.

Khavn De La Cruz

Khavn De La Cruz (December 23, 1973 Quezon City-) a.k.a. Khavn Nicolas de la Cruz, KHAVN, Khavn Dela Cruz or Khavn de la Cruz is a Filipino screenwriter, pianist, film producer, songwriter, film director, singer, poet, film score composer, actor and cinematographer.

John Estrada

John Estrada (June 13, 1973 Quezon City-) also known as Jonathan Estrada, John Anthony S. Estrada or Joaquin Mikael Prieto Estrada is a Filipino actor and model. He has five children, Ina Estrada, Moira Estrada, Kyla Estrada, Yuan Estrada and Sammanta Anechka Estrada.

Darrell James

Darrell James (April 8, 1973 Manila-) also known as Darrell James Coligado Laxamana is a Filipino record producer, composer, actor, model, entrepreneur, songwriter, singer and voice actor.

Paolo Montalbán

Paolo Montalbán (May 21, 1973 Manila-) a.k.a. Paolo Montalban or Paolo S. Montalban is a Filipino actor and singer.

John Apacible

John Apacible (January 22, 1973 Pulilan-March 20, 2011 Quezon City) a.k.a. Enrique R. Apacible III, Jon Apacible, Enrique Rustia Apacible or Enrique Rustia Apacible III was a Filipino actor.

Pekto

Pekto (June 14, 1973 Cebu-) also known as Mike Nacua, Mike 'Pekto' Nacua or Michael 'Pekto' Nacua is a Filipino actor.
