Filipino actors who were born in 1962

Here are 9 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1962:

Cesar Montano

His children are called Christian Angelo Manhilot, Angela Manhilot, Angeline Isabelle Manhilot, Angel Francheska Manhilot, Samantha Angeline Manhilot and Carlos Diego Loyzaga.

Lou Diamond Phillips

Lou Diamond Phillips (February 17, 1962 U.S. Naval Base Subic Bay-) a.k.a. Louis Upchurch, Lou Diamond Upchurch, Lou Diamond or Star Keeper is a Filipino actor, television director, film director, songwriter and screenwriter. He has four children, Isabella Patricia Phillips, Grace Moorea Phillips, Lili Jordan Phillips and Indigo Sanara Phillips.

Alvin Anson

Alvin Anson (October 20, 1962 Manila-) a.k.a. Alvin Clifford Lorenz Anson or Alvin L. Anson is a Filipino actor and model.

His children are called Claudia Isabelle Barretto, Aynrand Daniele Barretto, Leon Marcus Barretto, Alexandra Nicole Barretto, Luis Padilla, Diane Padilla and Julia Barretto.

Keno

Keno (June 16, 1962-) is a Filipino singer and actor.

Daniel Fernando

Daniel Fernando (May 12, 1962 Guiguinto-) also known as Cesar Fernando Ramirez, Daniel R. Fernando, Cesar Ramirez or Daniel B. Fernando is a Filipino actor and politician.

Martin Nievera

Martin Nievera (February 5, 1962 Manila-) a.k.a. Martin Ramon Nievera, Nievera, Martin or Martin Ramon Razon Nievera is a Filipino singer, songwriter and actor. His children are called Robin Nievera, Ram Nievera and Santino Nievera.

Michael Stone Forrest

Michael Stone Forrest (May 5, 1962 Clark Air Base-) also known as Michael L. Forrest is a Filipino actor.
