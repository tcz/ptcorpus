Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1962

Branco Mello

Branco Mello (March 16, 1962 São Paulo-) a.k.a. Claudio Corrêa de Mello Júnior or Joaquim Cláudio Correia de Mello Junior is a Brazilian singer, songwriter, composer, actor, record producer, musician and film score composer. He has two children, Bento Mello and Joaquim Mello.

Carlinhos Brown (November 23, 1962 Brotas-) a.k.a.
