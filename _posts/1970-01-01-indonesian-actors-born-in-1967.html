Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1967

Here are 8 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1967:

Adjie Massaid

Adjie Massaid (August 7, 1967 Jakarta-February 5, 2011 Jakarta) was an Indonesian actor and politician. He had three children, Zahwa Rezi Massaid, Aaliyah Massaid and Keanu Jabaar Massaid.

Adi Bing Slamet

Adi Bing Slamet (March 6, 1967 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor, child actor and singer. He has three children, Ratna Kharisma Adzana, Keino Abijan and Ratna Kaidah Sarati.

Fachrul Rozy

Fachrul Rozy (April 4, 1967 Jakarta-) a.k.a. Fahryl Rozi is an Indonesian actor.

Sonny Tulung

Sonny Tulung (December 12, 1967 Jakarta-) also known as Sonny Valentino Tulung is an Indonesian television presenter and actor.

Harry Pantja

Harry Pantja (February 1, 1967 Bandung-) also known as Harry Panca is an Indonesian actor and presenter.

Ferry Salim

Ferry Salim (January 8, 1967 Palembang-) is an Indonesian actor, model and businessperson. His children are called Brandon Nicholas Salim, Brenda Nabila Salim and Raoul Sebastian Salim.

Joe Project P

Joe Project P (May 6, 1967 Bandung-) also known as Joe P Project, Juhana Sutisna or Joe Project P. is an Indonesian actor, comedian and presenter.

Edo Kondologit

Edo Kondologit (August 5, 1967 Sorong-) is an Indonesian actor and singer. He has one child, Itamar Lemuela Barbalina.
