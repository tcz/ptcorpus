Thai movie stars born in 1965

Here are 5 famous actresses from Thailand were born in 1965:

Sinjai Plengpanich

Sinjai Plengpanich (January 21, 1965 Bangkok-) also known as Nok, Sinjai Hongtai, Sinjai Hongthai, Sinchai Plengphanit or Sinchai Hongthai is a Thai actor. She has three children, Sitthichoke Plengpanich, Thichakorn Plengpanich and Peeradon Plengpanich.

Alisa Kajornchaiyakul

Alisa Kajornchaiyakul (October 18, 1965 Chonburi Province-) also known as อลิสา ขจรไชยกุล is a Thai actor.

Chintara Sukapatana

Chintara Sukapatana (January 22, 1965 Phra Pradaeng District-) also known as Chittima Sukapatana, Chittima Sukkhaphat, Jittima Sukkapat, Mam, Jintara Sukaphat or Jintara Sukphat is a Thai actor and businessperson.

Jariya Anfone

Jariya Anfone (December 21, 1965 Bangkok-) is a Thai actor and singer.

Aphiradi Phawaphutanon

Aphiradi Phawaphutanon (September 23, 1965 Bangkok-) also known as Kaew Apiradee, Kaew, Aphiradi Tossaporn or Aphiradi Phawaphutanon Na Mahasarakarm is a Thai actor. She has three children, Papon Thossaporn, Passakorn Thossaporn and Leilani Thossaporn.
