Danish movie stars born in 1936

Malene Schwartz

Malene Schwartz (August 10, 1936 Frederiksberg-) is a Danish actor. She has one child, Christina Lillelund.

Birthe Wilke

Birthe Wilke (March 19, 1936 Denmark-) a.k.a. Wilke, Birthe is a Danish singer and actor.

Marie-Louise Coninck (December 8, 1936 Copenhagen-) also known as Marie Louise Conick or Marie Louise Coninck is a Danish actor. Her children are called Rebekka Owe, David Owe, Benjamin Owe and Anja Owe.

Lise Henningsen

Susanne Bruun-Koppel (September 1, 1936 Copenhagen-) is a Danish actor.
