Danish movie stars born in 1928

Here are 10 famous actors from Denmark were born in 1928:

Bertel Lauring

Bertel Lauring (January 11, 1928 Frederiksberg-January 28, 2000 Denmark) was a Danish actor. He had one child, Sophie Louise Lauring.

Axel Strøbye

Axel Strøbye (February 22, 1928 Frederiksberg-July 12, 2005 Charlottenlund) also known as Axel Strobye, Axel Strøby Jacobsen or Aksel Strøbye was a Danish actor. He had two children, Michäela Strøbye and Thomas Strøbye.

Jens Østerholm

Jens Østerholm (April 25, 1928 Denmark-November 13, 2006) was a Danish actor.

Henning Moritzen

Henning Moritzen (August 3, 1928 Taarbæk-August 11, 2012 Frederiksberg) also known as Henning Bolvig Moritzen was a Danish actor and film director. He had two children, Michael Moritzen and Marianne Moritzen.

Søren Elung Jensen

Søren Elung Jensen (July 7, 1928 Denmark-) also known as Søren Elung is a Danish actor. His children are called Martin Elung-Jensen and Mille Elung-Jensen.

Erik Bruhn

Erik Bruhn (October 3, 1928 Copenhagen-April 1, 1986 Toronto) also known as Erik Belton Evers Bruhn was a Danish writer, actor, ballet dancer, choreographer and author.

Peter Ronild

Peter Ronild (September 25, 1928 Frederiksberg-June 1, 2001 Denmark) otherwise known as Peter Vilhelm Ronild was a Danish actor.

Ole Schmidt

Ole Schmidt (July 14, 1928 Copenhagen-March 6, 2010) was a Danish film score composer and actor.

John Mogensen

John Mogensen (May 5, 1928 Copenhagen-April 10, 1977 Dragør) otherwise known as Mogensen, John was a Danish pianist, singer-songwriter, actor and composer. He had one child, Mina Mogensen.

Ib Hansen

Ib Hansen (December 22, 1928 Vanløse-February 24, 2013) also known as Hansen, Ib was a Danish actor.
