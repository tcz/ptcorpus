Turkish actors born in 1957

Here are 6 famous actors from Turkey were born in 1957:

Uğur Yücel

Uğur Yücel (May 26, 1957 Istanbul-) also known as Ugur Yücel or Ugur Yucel is a Turkish screenwriter, actor, film director, film producer and film score composer. He has one child, Can Yücel.

Bedri Baykam

Bedri Baykam (April 26, 1957 Ankara-) is a Turkish artist, actor and visual artist. He has one child, Suphi Baykam.

Mehmet Ali Erbil

Mehmet Ali Erbil (February 8, 1957 Istanbul-) is a Turkish actor and comedian. He has three children, Yasmin Erbil, Sezin Erbil and Ali Sadi Erbil.

Osman Soykut

Osman Soykut (January 1, 1957 Ankara-) also known as Ozman Sirgood, Oscar Oden or Oz is a Turkish actor.

Remzi Evren

Remzi Evren (February 25, 1957 Bolu-) is a Turkish actor.

Bülent Polat

Bülent Polat (June 11, 1957 Turgutlu-) also known as Bulent Polat is a Turkish actor.
