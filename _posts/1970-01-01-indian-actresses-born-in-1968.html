Indian movie stars born in 1968

Here are 12 famous actresses from India were born in 1968:

Varsha Usgaonkar

Varsha Usgaonkar (February 28, 1968 Goa-) also known as Varsha Usgaokar, Rani Lakshmibai or Wondergirl Varsha Usgaonkar is an Indian actor and model.

Mrinal Dev-Kulkarni

Mrinal Dev-Kulkarni (June 21, 1968 Pune-) is an Indian actor. She has one child, Virajas Kulkarni.

Amala Akkineni

Amala Akkineni (September 12, 1968 West Bengal-) also known as Amala Mukherjee, Amala Akkineni or Amala is an Indian actor and activist. She has one child, Akhil Akkineni.

Ananya Khare

Ananya Khare (March 16, 1968 Ratlam-) a.k.a. Preeti Khare or प्रीती खरे is an Indian actor.

Neelam Kothari

Neelam Kothari (March 3, 1968 Hong Kong-) a.k.a. Neelam Kothari-Sethia, Neelam Kothari-Soni, Neelam Samir Soni, Neelam Rishi Sethia, Sangeeta or Neelam is an Indian actor and jewelry designer. She has one child, Ahana Soni.

Satabdi Roy

Satabdi Roy (October 5, 1968 Agarpara-) also known as Shatabdi Roy, Satabdi or Shatabdi is an Indian actor, politician, film director and film producer. She has two children, Samyoraj Roy and Zumi Roy.

Farha Naaz

Farha Naaz (December 9, 1968 Hyderabad-) also known as Farah, Farah Naz, Farha, Farah Naaz, Farhah or Farah Naaz Hashmi is an Indian actor. She has one child, Fateh Randhawa.

Dolly Minhas

Dolly Minhas (February 8, 1968 Chandigarh-) a.k.a. Dolly Anit Mattoo, Dolly Minhas Mattoo or Dolly Mattoo is an Indian actor and model.

Bindu Panicker

Bindu Panicker (April 21, 1968 Kozhikode-) also known as Bindu Panikkar or Bindu Panickkar is an Indian actor. She has one child, Arundathi Panicker.

Rohini

Rohini (March 12, 1968 Anakapalle-) also known as Baby Rohini, R. Rohini or N. Rohini is an Indian actor, lyricist, voice actor, screenwriter and film director. She has one child, Sai Rishivaran.

Mini Mathur

Mini Mathur (August 21, 1968 Mumbai-) is an Indian actor, presenter and fashion model. Her children are called Sanya Khan and Vivaan Khan.

Krutika Desai Khan

Krutika Desai Khan (February 23, 1968 India-) a.k.a. Kritika Desai, Kruttika Desai or Krutika Desai is an Indian actor.
