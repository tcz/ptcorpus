Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1949

Here are 8 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1949:

Antônio Fagundes

Antônio Fagundes (April 18, 1949 Rio de Janeiro-) also known as Antônio da Silva Fagundes Filho, Fafá or Antonio Fagundes is a Brazilian actor. He has four children, Bruno Fagundes, Diana Fagundes, Dinah Fagundes and Antônio Fagundes Neto.

Flávio Galvão

Flávio Galvão (July 30, 1949 São Paulo-) also known as Flávio José Galvão de França is a Brazilian actor.

José Mayer

José Mayer (October 3, 1949 Jaguaraçu-) otherwise known as José Mayer Drumond is a Brazilian actor, film director, presenter and writer. His child is called Julia Fajardo.

Raimundo Fagner

Raimundo Fagner (October 13, 1949 Ceará-) otherwise known as Raimundo Fagner Cândido Lopes or Fagner is a Brazilian singer, composer, musician, actor and record producer.

Luiz Fernando Guimarães

Luiz Fernando Guimarães (November 20, 1949 Rio de Janeiro-) also known as Luiz Fernando Guimaraes or Luís Fernando Guimarães is a Brazilian actor.

Marcos Wainberg

Marcos Wainberg (January 27, 1949 Porto Alegre-) also known as Marcos Weinberg is a Brazilian actor.

Camilo Beviláqua

Camilo Beviláqua (September 11, 1949 Gaurama-) a.k.a. Camilo Bevilacqua is a Brazilian actor.

Lionel Fisher

Lionel Fisher (September 6, 1949 Rio de Janeiro-) otherwise known as Lionel Fischer is a Brazilian actor.
