Spanish movie actors born in the year 1942

Here are 12 famous actors from Spain were born in 1942:

Juan Diego

Juan Diego (December 14, 1942 Bormujos-) also known as Juan Diego Ruiz Montero, Juan D. Ruiz, Juan Diego Ruíz or Juan Diego Ruíz Montero is a Spanish actor and voice actor. He has one child, Diego Ruíz.

Emilio Gutiérrez Caba

Emilio Gutiérrez Caba (September 26, 1942 Valladolid-) also known as Emilio Gutierrez Caba, Emil Cape, Emilio Gutiérrez, Emilio G. Caba, E. Gutierrez Caba or Emilio Gutierrez Cava is a Spanish actor.

Ovidi Montllor

Ovidi Montllor (February 4, 1942 Alcoy, Spain-March 10, 1995 Barcelona) also known as Ovidi Montllor Mengual, Ovidi Monllor or Ovidi Montllor i Mengual was a Spanish singer-songwriter, actor and film score composer.

José Nieto

José Nieto (March 1, 1942 Madrid-) also known as Pepe Nieto or Jose Nieto is a Spanish film score composer, actor, musician and composer.

Humberto Serrano

Humberto Serrano (May 21, 1942 Spain-January 21, 2013 Buenos Aires) was a Spanish actor.

José Yepes

José Yepes (April 9, 1942 Sagunto-October 30, 2012 Sagunto) also known as José Yepes Cardo, Joseph Yepes, Jose Yepes or Pepe Yepes was a Spanish actor.

Félix Rotaeta

Félix Rotaeta (April 1, 1942 Madrid-November 23, 1994 Barcelona) a.k.a. Felix Rotaeta or Félix de Rotaeta y Otegui was a Spanish actor, film director and screenwriter.

Antonio Drove

Antonio Drove (November 1, 1942 Madrid-September 24, 2005) a.k.a. Antonio Drove Fernández-Shaw was a Spanish screenwriter, film director and actor.

Manolo García

Manolo García (September 17, 1942-) a.k.a. Manuel García Colas, Manuel Garco, Manolito García, Manuel García, Manolo Garcia or Manuel García Colás is a Spanish actor and voice actor. He has two children, Manuel García Guevara and Dani García.

Marcos Mundstock

Marcos Mundstock (May 25, 1942 Santa Fe-) also known as Marcos Mundstock Finkelstein or Les Luthiers is a Spanish musician, comedian, actor, film director and screenwriter.

Manolo Otero

Manolo Otero (June 25, 1942 Madrid-June 1, 2011 São Paulo) also known as Manuel Otero Aparicio, Manuel Otero, Manuel Otéro, Manuel "Manolo" Otero Aparicio, Manolo Otero Aparicio or Manolo was a Spanish actor, singer, film director, screenwriter and film editor. He had one child, Manuel Otero.

Juan Mari Arzak

Juan Mari Arzak (July 31, 1942 Donostia / San Sebastián-) a.k.a. Juan María Arzak or Juan Mari Arzak is a Spanish chef and actor. He has one child, Elena Arzak.
