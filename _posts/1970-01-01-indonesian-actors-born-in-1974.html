Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1974

Here are 5 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1974:

Restu Sinaga

Restu Sinaga (September 21, 1974 Indonesia-) is an Indonesian actor, model and businessperson.

Andre Taulany

Andre Taulany (September 17, 1974 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian singer, actor and comedian. He has three children, Ardio Raihansyah Taulany, Arkenzy Salmansyah Taulany and Arlova Carissa Taulany.

Ferry Maryadi

Ferry Maryadi (March 23, 1974 Indonesia-) is an Indonesian actor.

Marcellino Lafrand

Marcellino Lafrand (July 19, 1974 Manado-) also known as Marcelino Lefrandt or Marcellino Lefrandt is an Indonesian actor. He has one child, Marcell Brineth Reyney Lefrand.

Indra Brasco

Indra Brasco (October 26, 1974 Malang-) is an Indonesian actor. His children are called Davina Syafa Felisa, Barata Rahadian Nezar and Syanala Kania Salsabila.
