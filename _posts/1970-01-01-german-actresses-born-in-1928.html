German actresses who were born in 1928

Here are 9 famous actresses from Germany were born in 1928:

Leah Rabin

Marianne Kiefer

Marianne Kiefer (September 3, 1928 Dresden-January 4, 2008 Kreischa) was a German actor.

Eva Ingeborg Scholz

Eva Ingeborg Scholz (February 16, 1928 Berlin-) otherwise known as Eva-Ingeborg Scholz or Eva I. Scholz is a German actor. She has one child, Katharina Seyferth.

Ingrid Rentsch

Ingrid Rentsch (January 3, 1928 Berlin-) is a German actor. Her child is called Florian Martens.

Helen Vita

Helen Vita (August 7, 1928 Hohenschwangau-February 16, 2001 Berlin) otherwise known as Helene Elisabeth Reichel, Helene Vita, Helen Vita Elizabeth Reichel or Helen Vita Elizabeth Reichel Baumgartner was a German actor, singer and comedian.

Ursula Lingen

Ursula Lingen (February 9, 1928 Berlin-October 20, 2014) also known as Uschi Lingen was a German actor.

Edith Hancke

Edith Hancke (October 14, 1928 Berlin-) also known as Edith Hanke is a German actor and voice actor.

Ruth Glöss

Ruth Glöss (August 2, 1928 Dresden-) otherwise known as Ruth Gloess or Ruth Glöß is a German actor.

Ruth Niehaus

Ruth Niehaus (July 11, 1928 Krefeld-September 24, 1994 Hamburg) a.k.a. Ruth Lissner was a German actor. Her child is called Imogen.
