Pius Njawé (March 4, 1957 Babouantou-June 12, 2010 Chesapeake) was a Cameroonian journalist.

Njawé faced numerous arrests and detentions over the course of his career, including a six-month sentence in 1997 for publishing articles critical of President Paul Biya.
