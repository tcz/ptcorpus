Argentine actors who were born in 1937

Here are 10 famous actors from Argentina were born in 1937:

Ulises Dumont

Ulises Dumont (April 7, 1937 Belgrano, Buenos Aires-November 29, 2008 Almagro, Buenos Aires) a.k.a. Ulises Oscar Dumont was an Argentine actor. His child is called Enrique Dumont.

Aldo Barbero

Aldo Barbero (February 13, 1937 Santa Fe-October 27, 2013 Buenos Aires) was an Argentine actor.

Rodolfo Ranni

Rodolfo Ranni (October 31, 1937 Trieste-) also known as Tano is an Argentine actor.

Martín Andrade

Martín Andrade (July 20, 1937 Puerto Natales-) a.k.a. Juan Manuel Costa Andrade is an Argentine actor. He has one child, Antonella Costa.

Hugo Blanco

Rodolfo Machado

Rodolfo Machado (August 23, 1937 Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine actor. His children are called Juan Ignacio Machado and Pablo Machado.

Gerardo Sofovich

Gerardo Sofovich (March 18, 1937 Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine film director, screenwriter, actor, comedian and businessperson. His child is called Gustavo Sofovich.

Tristán Díaz Ocampo

Tristán Díaz Ocampo (October 27, 1937 Pergamino-) a.k.a. Tristan or Tristan Diaz Antonio Ocampo is an Argentine actor.

Pepe Novoa
