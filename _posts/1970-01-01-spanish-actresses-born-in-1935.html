Spanish movie actresses born in the year 1935

Here are 11 famous actresses from Spain were born in 1935:

Núria Espert

Lola Herrera

Teresa Berganza

Teresa Berganza (March 16, 1935 Madrid-) a.k.a. Maria Teresa Berganza, Maria Berganza or María Teresa Berganza is a Spanish singer, actor and opera singer. She has one child, Cecilia Lavilla Berganza.

Esperanza Roy

Esperanza Roy (November 22, 1935 Madrid-) also known as Esperanza Fuentes Roy, Esperanza Roig, Esther Ray or Esther Roy is a Spanish actor.

Luz Márquez

Luz Márquez (December 12, 1935 Madrid-) also known as María de la O Martínez García Soler or Luz Marquez is a Spanish actor.

Miriam Mehler

Miriam Mehler (September 15, 1935 Barcelona-) is a Spanish actor. Her child is called Rodrigo Salles.

Alicia Rodríguez

Alicia Rodríguez (May 2, 1935 Málaga-) also known as Alicia Rodriguez 'Pipa' is a Spanish actor.

María Galiana

María Galiana (May 31, 1935 Seville-) otherwise known as María Galiana Medina or Maria Galiana is a Spanish actor.

Lolita Sevilla

Lolita Sevilla (March 20, 1935 Seville-December 16, 2013 Madrid) a.k.a. Ángeles Moreno Gómez was a Spanish actor and singer.

Mikaela
