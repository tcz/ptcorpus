Turkish actors born in 1961

Here are 12 famous actors from Turkey were born in 1961:

Metin Kaçan (November 15, 1961 Kayseri-January 8, 2013 Istanbul) also known as Metin Kacan was a Turkish writer and actor.

Birol Ünel

Birol Ünel (August 18, 1961 Silifke-) a.k.a. Birol Unel, The Turkish Klaus Kinski or Byrol Uenel is a Turkish actor.

Burak Sergen

Burak Sergen (February 9, 1961 Ankara-) also known as Burak Reis Sergen is a Turkish actor. His child is called Cansın Sergen.

Uğur Polat

Uğur Polat (September 4, 1961 Istanbul-) also known as Ugur Polat is a Turkish actor.

Mehmet Aslantuğ

Mehmet Aslantuğ (September 25, 1961 Samsun-) also known as Mehmet Aslantug is a Turkish actor. He has one child, Can Aslantuğ.

Mustafa Presheva

Mustafa Presheva (October 11, 1961 Mitrovica, Kosovo-) also known as Mustafa Preseva, Mustafa Preşeva or Mustafa Preševa is a Turkish film editor and actor.

Zafer Algöz

Zafer Algöz (August 30, 1961 Kars-) is a Turkish actor.

Ezel Akay

Ezel Akay (January 20, 1961 İnegöl-) otherwise known as Ezop is a Turkish film producer, screenwriter, film director and actor.

Bülent Emin Yarar

Bülent Emin Yarar (January 1, 1961 Ankara-) also known as Bulent Yarar, Bülent Yarar or Bulent Emin Yarar is a Turkish actor. He has one child, Ada Yarar.

İsmail Güneş

İsmail Güneş (June 10, 1961 Samsun-) also known as Ismail Günes is a Turkish film director, screenwriter, film producer and actor.

Levent Beceren

Levent Beceren (September 2, 1961 Istanbul-) is a Turkish actor.

Murat Karasu

Murat Karasu (March 8, 1961 Akyazı-) is a Turkish actor, theatre director and playwright.
