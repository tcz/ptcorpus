Filipino musicians who were born in 1966

Here are 5 famous musicians from Philippines were born in 1966:

Sharon Cuneta

Sharon Cuneta (January 6, 1966 Manila-) also known as Sharon Cuneta-Pangilinan, Sharon Gamboa Cuneta, Shawi, Mega Star, DJ's Pet, Mega, Cuneta, Sharon, Sharon Gamboa Cuneta Pangilinan, The Megastar, Shawie or Sharon Gamboa Cuneta-Pangilinan is a Filipino singer, presenter and actor. She has four children, KC Concepcion, Simone Francesca Emmanuelle Pangilinan, Mariel Daniella Sophia Pangilinan and Miguel Samuel Mateo Pangilinan.

Discography: 25 Years Hits (disc 2), All I Ever Want, Sharon Cuneta, The Very Best of Sharon, Volume 1, The Very Best of Sharon, Volume 2, Sinasamba Kita, , Sharon Sings Alcasid, Isn't It Romantic and Mega Up Close. Genres she performed include Manila Sound, Easy listening, Pop music, Music of the Philippines and Adult contemporary music.

Pops Fernandez

Pops Fernandez (December 12, 1966 Quezon City-) also known as Maria Cielito Lukban Fernandez, Fernandez, Pops, Maria Cielito "Pops" Lukban Fernandez, Pipay, Concert Queen or The Concert Queen is a Filipino singer, actor, songwriter, model, entertainer, entrepreneur and presenter. Her children are called Robin Nievera and Ram Nievera.

Genres: Pop music, Rock music, Music of the Philippines and Dance music.

Ariel Rivera

Ariel Rivera (September 1, 1966 Manila-) otherwise known as Rivera, Ariel, Jose Ariel Jimenez Rivera, Ariel A. Rivera or Ariel Abril Rivera is a Filipino singer, singer-songwriter and actor. He has two children, Joaquin Andres Rivera and Julio Alessandro Rivera.

Discography: Ariel Rivera, Greatest Hits, Aawitin Ko Na Lang, Simple Lang, Paskong Walang Katulad, Once Again, Getting to Know, Photograph, In My Life and Platinum Hits Collection. His related genres: Music of the Philippines and Pop music.

Agot Isidro

Agot Isidro (July 20, 1966 Manila-) otherwise known as Isidro, Agot, Maria Margarita Amada Fteha Isidro or Aggot Isidro is a Filipino singer, actor, presenter and tv personality.

Her discography includes: Best in Me and Sa Isip Ko.

Jamie Rivera

Jamie Rivera (August 29, 1966-) a.k.a. Mary Jane C. Mendoza is a Filipino singer, record producer, songwriter and actor.

Her albums include Songs for a Purpose Driven Life. Her related genres: Gospel music, Pop music, Easy listening and Music of the Philippines.
