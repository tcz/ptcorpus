Indian movie stars born in 1957

Here are 11 famous actresses from India were born in 1957:

Mira Nair

Mira Nair (October 15, 1957 Rourkela-) is an Indian film director, film producer, actor, teacher and writer. Her child is called Zohran Mamdani.

Tina Ambani

Tina Ambani (February 11, 1957 Mumbai-) also known as Tina Anil Ambani, Tina, Tina Munium, Tina Ambani, Niruvati Munim or Tina Munim is an Indian actor. She has two children, Jai Anmol Ambani and Jai Anshul Ambani.

Dimple Kapadia

Dimple Kapadia (June 8, 1957 Mumbai-) also known as Dimple Chunnibhai Kapadia, Dimple Jatin Khanna or Dimple is an Indian actor. She has two children, Twinkle Khanna and Rinke Khanna.

Nafisa Ali

Nafisa Ali (January 18, 1957 Mumbai-) also known as Nafisa Ali Sodhi is an Indian politician, actor and model. Her children are called Ajit Sodhi, Armana Sodhi and Pia Sodhi.

Deepti Naval

Deepti Naval (February 3, 1957 Amritsar-) a.k.a. Dipti Naval is an Indian actor and film director. Her children are called Disha Jha and Priyaranjhan Jha.

Reena Roy

Reena Roy (January 7, 1957 Mumbai-) also known as Reena Mohsin Khan, Roopa Rai or Zaroorat Girl is an Indian actor. She has one child, Sanam Khan.

Jayachitra

Jayachitra (September 9, 1957 Chennai-) also known as Lakshmi Krishnaveni Rohini Parvathi Devi, Lakshmi or Rohini is an Indian actor. She has one child, Amresh.

Seema

Seema (May 22, 1957 Choolaimedu-) a.k.a. Shanthi, Shanthakumari or Seema Sasi is an Indian actor. She has two children, Anu Sasi and Ani Sasi.

Umashree

Umashree (May 10, 1957 Nonavinakere-) also known as Umashri or Umasri is an Indian actor, politician and comedian. Her children are called Gayathri and Vijayakumar.

Priti Sapru

Priti Sapru (December 24, 1957 Mumbai-) also known as Priti D. Sapru, Preeti Sapru, Preethi Sapru, Priti Utpal Ahluwalia, Priti, Priti Sapru Ahluwalia or Priti Sapru Walia is an Indian actor, screenwriter, film director and film producer. Her children are called Riya Walia and Rene Walia.

Vinodini Sasimohan

Vinodini Sasimohan (October 21, 1957 Chennai-) also known as Baby vinodini or Vinodini Baby is an Indian actor. She has two children, S Gopimadhav and S Mathangi.
