Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1953

Here are 5 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1953:

Emha Ainun Nadjib

Emha Ainun Nadjib (May 27, 1953 Jombang Regency-) also known as Muhammad Ainun Nadjib or Cak Nun is an Indonesian poet, author, actor, social activist, composer and religious leader. He has five children, Sabrang Mowo Damar Panuluh, Ainayya Al-Fatihah, Aqiela Fadia Haya, Jembar Tahta Aunillah and Anayallah Rampak Mayesha.

Afrizal Anoda

Afrizal Anoda (April 11, 1953 Sawahlunto-) a.k.a. Bang AFA is an Indonesian actor and journalist. He has two children, Namida Puti Mayangsuri and Akita Chinta.

Hendro Tangkilisan

Hendro Tangkilisan (September 26, 1953 Samarinda-) is an Indonesian actor, production designer and film art director.

Robby Tumewu

Robby Tumewu (December 4, 1953 Bandung-) is an Indonesian actor, singer and fashion designer.

Cok Simbara

Cok Simbara (November 12, 1953 South Tapanuli Regency-) otherwise known as Ucok Hasyim Batubara is an Indonesian actor.
