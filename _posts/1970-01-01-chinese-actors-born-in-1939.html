Chinese actors who were born in 1939

Here are 7 famous actors from China were born in 1939:

Richard Ng

Richard Ng (December 17, 1939 Guangdong Province-) also known as Yaohan Wu, Richard Woo, Yil-Hon Ng, Richard, 吴耀汉, 吳耀漢, Wú Yào Hàn, Richard Ng Yiu-Hon, Yiu-Hon Ng or Ng4 Yiu6-hon3 is a Chinese actor and screenwriter. His children are called Carl Ng, Zoe Ng, Louise Ng and Alex Ng.

Wu Tianming

Wu Tianming (October 19, 1939 Sanyuan County-March 4, 2014 Beijing) a.k.a. Tian-Ming Wu, Tianming Wu, Tian Ming Wu, Wú Tiānmíng, Wu Tian-Ming, Wu Tian Ming, Ng Tin Ming, Ng Tin-Ming or Ng Tien-Ming was a Chinese film producer, film director, actor and screenwriter. His child is called Yanyan Wu.

Yu Chenghui

Yu Chenghui (August 16, 1939 Qingdao-) also known as Chen-Wei Yue, Sing-Wai Yue, Chenghui Yu, Yue Sing-wai, Yú Chénghuì, Jyu4 Sing4 Wai6, Cheng-Hui Yu, Yu Ching-Wai, Hui Ching-Wai or Yu Cheng-Hui is a Chinese actor, martial artist, film director and screenwriter.

Ko Hsiao Pao

Ko Hsiao Pao (February 2, 1939 Suzhou-) a.k.a. Ker Hsiao-Po, Kou Shao Po or Hsiao Pao Ko is a Chinese actor.

Michael Chow

Michael Chow (February 6, 1939 Shanghai-) a.k.a. Zhōu Yīnghuá, 周英华 or 周英華 is a Chinese actor, interior designer and restaurateur. He has three children, China Chow, Maximillian Chow and Asia Chow.

Eric Flynn

Eric Flynn (December 13, 1939 Hainan-March 4, 2002 Pembrokeshire) also known as Eric William Flynn was a Chinese singer and actor. He had five children, Jerome Flynn, Daniel Flynn, Johnny Flynn, Kerry Flynn and Lillie Flynn.

Li Lian Yi

Li Lian Yi (March 1, 1939 Baoding-) is a Chinese actor.
