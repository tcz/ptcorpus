Finnish actors born in 1934

Here are 12 famous actors from Finland were born in 1934:

Risto Jarva

Risto Jarva (July 15, 1934 Helsinki-December 16, 1977 Helsinki) a.k.a. Risto Antero Jarva was a Finnish screenwriter, film producer, film director, film editor and actor.

Gustav Wiklund

Gustav Wiklund (May 26, 1934 Helsinki-) a.k.a. Gustav Adolf Fingal Wiklund or Gustaf Wiklund is a Finnish actor, film director, screenwriter and film producer.

Jaakko Pakkasvirta

Jaakko Pakkasvirta (November 28, 1934 Rautjärvi-) also known as Pierre Froid, Jaakko Juhani Pakkasvirta or James Pakkasvirta is a Finnish actor, screenwriter, film director, film producer, film editor and sound recordist.

Pertti Palo

Pertti Palo (November 11, 1934 Helsinki-August 13, 2010 Helsinki) was a Finnish actor. He had two children, Sanna-Kaisa Palo and Maiju Palo.

Taneli Rinne

Taneli Rinne (April 14, 1934 Helsinki-) a.k.a. Taneli Iivar Rinne is a Finnish actor.

Stig Fransman

Stig Fransman (May 30, 1934 Helsinki-) also known as Stig Holgersson Fransman is a Finnish actor and singer. His children are called Sanna Fransman and Minna Lavikainen.

Lasse Mårtenson

Lasse Mårtenson (September 24, 1934 Helsinki-) also known as Lasse Mårtenson Studio-orkesteri, Lasse Martenson, Mårtenson, Lasse, Lars Anders Fredrik "Lasse" Mårtenson, Dixieland Band, Lasse Mårtenson och trio or Lars Anders Fredrik Mårtenson is a Finnish singer, film score composer and actor. His children are called Tove Christel Malmkvist and Kasper Martenson.

Ensio Suominen

Ensio Suominen (January 2, 1934 Askainen-September 1, 2003 Pirkkala) also known as Pertti Ensio Suominen was a Finnish film art director, cinematographer and actor.

Antti Peippo

Antti Peippo (September 10, 1934 Lahti-June 29, 1989 Helsinki) also known as Antti Eerikki Peippo, Antti Eerikki Chaffinch or Antti Chaffinch was a Finnish screenwriter, cinematographer, film director, film producer, actor and film editor.

Pauli Virtanen

Pauli Virtanen (December 27, 1934 Helsinki-August 9, 2003 Helsinki) also known as Pauli Armas Virtanen was a Finnish actor, screenwriter, film director and television director.

Aarre Elo

Aarre Elo (September 21, 1934 Helsinki-) also known as Elo, Aarre is a Finnish television director, screenwriter, television producer, actor and presenter. He has one child, Maria Jungner.

Pertti Nättilä

Pertti Nättilä (February 1, 1934 Vyborg-) is a Finnish film director, television director, screenwriter and actor.
