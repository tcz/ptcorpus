Serbian movie actors born in the year 1949

Here are 7 famous actors from Serbia were born in 1949:

Branko Djuric

Branko Djuric (October 4, 1949 Šabac-) also known as Branko Djuric-Djura, Branko Djuric Djuro, Branko Duric, Branco Duric or Branco Djuric is a Serbian actor.

Vojislav Brajović

Vojislav Brajović (May 11, 1949 Belgrade-) otherwise known as Vojislav Brajovic, Voja Brajovic, Voja Brajevic, Vojislav Brajevic or Vojislav "Voja" Brajović is a Serbian politician and actor. His children are called Iskra Brajovic and Vukota Brajovic.

Miodrag Krivokapic

Miodrag Krivokapic (April 22, 1949 Peć-) also known as Miodrag Brik Krivokapic, Brik or Miodrag Krivokapić is a Serbian actor. His child is called Bojan Krivokapic.

Ivan Klemenc

Ivan Klemenc (August 18, 1949 Novi Sad-) also known as Ivica Klemenc is a Serbian actor.

Bora Nenic

Bora Nenic (November 23, 1949 Subotište-) a.k.a. Boro Nenic is a Serbian actor.

Zarko Savic

Zarko Savic (July 30, 1949 Varvarin-) is a Serbian actor.

Vojin Kajganic

Vojin Kajganic (May 27, 1949 Belgrade-) also known as V. Kajganic or Vojislav Kajganic is a Serbian actor.
