Argentine actors who were born in 1933

Here are 10 famous actors from Argentina were born in 1933:

Alfredo Landa

Alfredo Landa (March 3, 1933 Pamplona-May 9, 2013 Madrid) also known as Alfredo Landa Areitio or Alfredo Landa Areta was an Argentine actor. His children are called Ainhoa Landa, Idoia Landa and Alfredo Landa.

Luis Felipe Noé

Luis Felipe Noé (May 26, 1933 Buenos Aires-) also known as Yuyo is an Argentine writer, artist, actor, painter, teacher and visual artist. He has one child, Gaspar Noé.

Enzo Viena

Enzo Viena (February 16, 1933 Rosario-November 25, 2007 Rosario) a.k.a. Felipe Enzo Viena was an Argentine actor.

Geoffrey Horne

Geoffrey Horne (August 22, 1933 Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine actor, acting coach and film director. His children are called Kimberly Horne and William Horne.

Luis Politti

Luis Politti (April 8, 1933 Mendoza-July 14, 1980 Madrid) also known as Luis Politi was an Argentine actor. His children are called Sergio Politti, Andrea Politti, Carolina Politti and Livia Politti.

Sergio Renán

Sergio Renán (January 30, 1933 Argentina-) also known as Renán or Samuel Kohan is an Argentine screenwriter, film director, actor, violinist and music director.

Eduardo Pavlovsky

Eduardo Pavlovsky (December 10, 1933 Argentina-) also known as Tato Pavlovsky is an Argentine actor and screenwriter.

Emilio Alfaro

Emilio Alfaro (January 20, 1933 Buenos Aires-July 18, 1998 Buenos Aires) also known as Emilio Villariño Alfaro was an Argentine actor.

Juanito Belmonte

Juanito Belmonte (January 3, 1933 Rosario-June 23, 2012 Buenos Aires) also known as Felix Juan Verón or Juan Belmonte was an Argentine actor.

Alberto Olmedo

Alberto Olmedo (August 24, 1933 Rosario-March 5, 1988 Mar del Plata) also known as Alberto Orlando Olmedo, El Negro, Negro Olmedo or Capitan Piluso was an Argentine comedian and actor. He had one child, Alberto Olmedo Jr..
