Dillon began his acting career on stage, starring in several productions in London's West End. He then transitioned into voice acting, lending his voice to numerous video games and animated films. In addition to his voice acting work, Dillon continues to take on roles in live-action films and television shows. He is known for his versatility as an actor and his commitment to his craft. Off-screen, Dillon is also an avid musician, playing drums and guitar in his spare time.
