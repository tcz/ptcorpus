Thai movie stars born in 1956

Here are 6 famous actors from Thailand were born in 1956:

Tanit Jitnukul

Tanit Jitnukul (November 24, 1956 Songkhla Province-) also known as Pued or Thanit Jitnukun is a Thai film director, screenwriter, film producer and actor.

Kriengkrai Oonhanun

Kriengkrai Oonhanun (August 3, 1956 Bangkok-) is a Thai actor and businessperson.

Dilok Thongwattana

Dilok Thongwattana (June 3, 1956 Chainat Province-) is a Thai actor and master of ceremonies.

Yingsak Chonglertjetsadawong

Yingsak Chonglertjetsadawong (March 29, 1956 Thonburi-) is a Thai actor and chef. His children are called Marina Chonglertjetsadawong and Kawin Chonglertjetsadawong.

Surachai Sombutchareon

Surachai Sombutchareon (August 25, 1956 Suphan Buri Province-) also known as Surachai Sombacharoen is a Thai actor and musician. His children are called Sitapa Sombatcharoen, Shinapa Sombatcharoen, Kamonchanok Sombutchareon and Surabodin Sombacharoen.

Yodrak Salakjai

Yodrak Salakjai (February 6, 1956 Taphan Hin District-August 9, 2008 Bangkok) also known as Nipon Phaiwan or Aew was a Thai actor. His child is called Kriangsak Phaiwan.
