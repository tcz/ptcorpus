Finnish actors born in 1974

Here are 6 famous actors from Finland were born in 1974:

Juho Milonoff

Juho Milonoff (February 26, 1974 Helsinki-) is a Finnish actor.

Carl-Gustaf Wentzel

Carl-Gustaf Wentzel (October 15, 1974 Helsinki-) a.k.a. Carl-Gustav Wentzel, Carl-Gustaf Georg Wentzel or Carl Gustaf Wentzel is a Finnish actor.

Ville Tiihonen

Ville Tiihonen (February 23, 1974 Helsinki-) is a Finnish actor.

Jaakko Kuusisto

Jaakko Kuusisto (January 17, 1974 Lahti-) otherwise known as Kuusisto, Jaakko is a Finnish actor, film director, violinist, composer and conductor.

Tomi Putaansuu

Tomi Putaansuu (February 15, 1974 Rovaniemi-) also known as Lordi, Mr., Tomi Petteri Putaansuu, Lordi or Mr. Lordi is a Finnish singer, musician, songwriter, actor, screenwriter and painter.

Janne Kinnunen

Janne Kinnunen (July 25, 1974 Kontiolahti-) a.k.a. Janne Jaakko Tapani Kinnunen is a Finnish actor.
