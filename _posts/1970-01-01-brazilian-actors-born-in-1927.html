Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1927

Here are 13 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1927:

Adhemar da Silva

Adhemar da Silva (September 29, 1927 São Paulo-January 12, 2001 São Paulo) also known as Ademar Da Silva or Adhemar Feirrera Da Silva was a Brazilian actor.

Armando Nogueira

Armando Nogueira (January 14, 1927 Xapuri-March 29, 2010 Rio de Janeiro) was a Brazilian film director, actor, screenwriter, journalist, writer, film producer and television director.

Nelson Dantas

Nelson Dantas (November 17, 1927 Rio de Janeiro-March 18, 2006 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Nelson Hannequim Dantas Filho or Nélson Dantas was a Brazilian actor. He had two children, Daniel Dantas and Andrea Dantas.

Canarinho

Canarinho (December 29, 1927 Salvador-) a.k.a. Aluísio Ferreira Gomes or Aluisio Ferreira Gomes is a Brazilian actor.

Fernando Torres

Fernando Torres (November 14, 1927 Guaçuí-September 4, 2008 Rio de Janeiro) a.k.a. Fernando Monteiro Torres was a Brazilian actor, television director, voice actor, film director, film producer, television producer, theatrical producer and theatre director. His children are called Fernanda Torres and Cláudio Torres.

Cláudio Correia e Castro

Cláudio Correia e Castro (February 27, 1927 Rio de Janeiro-August 16, 2005 Niterói) also known as Cláudio Luís Miguel Corrêa e Castro, Claudio Correa e Castro or Cláudio Corrêa e Castro was a Brazilian actor. He had three children, Guilherme Correia e Castro, Gabriel Correia e Castro and João Pedro Correia e Castro.

Carlos Coimbra

Carlos Coimbra (August 13, 1927 Campinas-February 14, 2007 São Paulo) was a Brazilian screenwriter, film director, film editor, actor, film producer and production designer. He had one child, Cláudio Coimbra.

Antônio Carlos Pires

Antônio Carlos Pires (January 1, 1927 Rio de Janeiro-February 28, 2005 Rio de Janeiro) also known as Antônio Carlos was a Brazilian actor. He had two children, Glória Pires and Linda Pires.

Lúcio Mauro

Lúcio Mauro (March 14, 1927 Belém-) also known as Lúcio de Barros Barbalho is a Brazilian actor and television director. He has five children, Gilberto Salles, Alexandre Barbalho, Lúcio Mauro Filho, Luciane Maria Barbalho and Luana Araujo Barbalho.

João Bennio

João Bennio (April 11, 1927 Mutum-June 18, 1984 Goiânia) also known as João Bennio Batista was a Brazilian film producer, actor, screenwriter and film director.

João Dias

João Dias (October 12, 1927 Campinas-November 27, 1996 Rio de Janeiro) a.k.a. João Dias Rodrigues Filho was a Brazilian actor.

Décio Pignatari

Décio Pignatari (August 20, 1927 Jundiaí-December 2, 2012 São Paulo) was a Brazilian author and actor.

Marthus Mathias

Marthus Mathias (May 1, 1927 Itajubá-) is a Brazilian actor.
