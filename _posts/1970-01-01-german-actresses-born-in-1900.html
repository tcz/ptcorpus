German actresses who were born in 1900

Here are 13 famous actresses from Germany were born in 1900:

Hede Massing

Hede Massing (January 6, 1900 Vienna-March 8, 1981 Washington Square Park) a.k.a. Hedwig Tune, Hede Gumperz, Redhead, Hede Eisler or Hedwig Thune was a German journalist and actor.

Helene Weigel

Helene Weigel (May 12, 1900 Vienna-May 6, 1971 Berlin) was a German actor. She had two children, Stefan Brecht and Barbara Brecht-Schall.

Maria Bard

Maria Bard (July 7, 1900 Schwerin-April 8, 1944 Potsdam) also known as Migo Bard or Maria Luise Bard was a German actor.

Erna Berger

Erna Berger (October 19, 1900 Dresden-June 14, 1990 Essen) also known as Berger, Erna was a German singer, actor and professor.

Eleonora von Mendelssohn

Eleonora von Mendelssohn (January 12, 1900 Berlin-January 24, 1951 New York City) otherwise known as Eleonora Mendelssohn, Eleonora Forster or Eleonore von Mendelssohn was a German actor.

Lissy Arna

Lissy Arna (December 20, 1900 Berlin-January 22, 1964 Berlin) otherwise known as Elisabeth Arndt, Lissi Arna or Lissy Arndt was a German actor.

Ursula Krieg

Ursula Krieg (October 10, 1900 Berlin-October 11, 1984 West Berlin) a.k.a. Ursula Krieg-Buesche or Ursula Kriegk was a German actor.

Anneliese Würtz

Anneliese Würtz (August 4, 1900 Lübeck-April 1, 1981) also known as Annaliese Würtz was a German actor and voice actor.

Charlotte Kramm

Charlotte Kramm (March 15, 1900 Berlin-November 21, 1971 Hamburg) also known as Charlotte Goldschmidt or Charlotte Maertens was a German actor and voice actor. Her child is called Peter Maertens.

Eva Fiebig

Eva Fiebig (May 21, 1900 Berlin-October 21, 1984 Hamburg) was a German actor.

Elisabeth Pinajeff

Elisabeth Pinajeff (April 17, 1900 Germany-December 31, 1995 Villemoisson-sur-Orge) otherwise known as Lily Dorell, Elisabeth Pinajewa or Elisabeth von Pinajeff was a German actor.

Annemarie Hase

Annemarie Hase (June 14, 1900 Berlin-February 22, 1971 West Berlin) a.k.a. Annita Hirsch or Annemarie Hasse was a German actor, singer and cabaret artist.

Charlotte Küter

Charlotte Küter (January 17, 1900 Berlin-June 2, 1983 Weimar) was a German actor.
