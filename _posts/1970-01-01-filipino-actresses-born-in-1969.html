Filipino actresses who were born in 1969

Here are 8 famous actresses from Philippines were born in 1969:

Jaya

Jaya (March 21, 1969 Manila-) also known as Maria Luisa Ramsey, Maria Luisa Ramsey Buffington, The Philippine's Queen of Soul, Queen of Soul or Soul Diva is a Filipino singer, record producer, comedian, presenter, actor, composer, rapper and dancer. She has two children, Sabriya Gotidoc and Dylan Gotidoc.

Amy Perez

Amy Perez (September 5, 1969 Manila-) otherwise known as Ma. Armida Parale Sarte-Perez, Tyang Ami, Maria Armida Parale Sarte Perez, Tyang Amy, Amay or Maria Armida Parale "Amy" Sarte-Perez is a Filipino actor, radio personality, presenter and businessperson. She has one child, Adi Perez.

Dawn Zulueta

Dawn Zulueta (March 4, 1969 Manila-) a.k.a. Marie Rachel Salman Taleon, Marie Rachel Salman-Taleon-Lagdameo or Marie Rachel Salman is a Filipino presenter, actor and model. Her children are called Jacobo Antonio Lagdameo and Ayisha Madlen Lagdameo.

Jean Garcia

Jean Garcia (August 22, 1969 Angeles-) also known as Rosario Maitim or Jessica Rodriguez Maitim is a Filipino actor. She has one child, Jennica Garcia.

Marmee Cosico

Marmee Cosico (November 10, 1969 San Pablo, Laguna-) also known as Marmee Cabildo Cosico or Marms is a Filipino actor, psychologist and pharmacy technician.

Maritoni Fernandez

Maritoni Fernandez (March 31, 1969 Philippines-) also known as Antonita Moynihan or Maritone Fernandez is a Filipino actor, model and entrepreneur. Her children are called Lexi Fernandez and Liam Moynihan.

Alice Dixson

Alice Dixson (July 28, 1969 Coral Gables-) also known as Alice C. Dixson, Jessie Alice Celones Dixson, Alice Dixon or Alice is a Filipino model, actor and real estate broker.

January Isaac

January Isaac (January 5, 1969 Philippines-) also known as Sandra Gomez or Lumen is a Filipino actor.
