Iranian actresses born in 1972

Leila Hatami (October 1, 1972 Tehran-) also known as Leylâ Hâtami is an Iranian actor. She has two children, Mani Mosaffa and Asal Mosaffa.

Hedieh Tehrāni

Hedieh Tehrāni (June 25, 1972 Tehran-) otherwise known as Hedyeh Tehrani, Hedieh Tehrani, Hediyeh Tehrāni, Hedye Tehrani, Hedyeh Tehrāni or H. Tehrani is an Iranian actor and film art director.

Shaghayegh Farahani

Shaghayegh Farahani (July 24, 1972 Tehran-) a.k.a. شقایق فراهانی is an Iranian actor.

Vishka Asayesh

Mahnour Shadzi
