Czech movie actresses born in the year 1973

Here are 8 famous actresses from Czech Republic were born in 1973:

Eva Herzigová

Eva Herzigová (March 10, 1973 Litvínov-) also known as Eva Herzigova or Eva Hertzigova is a Czech model and actor. She has three children, George Marsiaj, Philipe Marsiaj and Edward Marsiaj.

Pavlína Němcová

Pavlína Němcová (February 18, 1973 Děčín-) a.k.a. Nemcova Pavlina, Paulina Rocio, Paulina Nemcova, Paulina Bakarova, Pavlina Nemcova-Bakarová or Paulína Bakarová is a Czech model, actor, television producer, film producer and lawyer.

Zuzana Stivínová

Zuzana Stivínová (July 24, 1973 Prague-) also known as Z. Stivínová is a Czech actor.

Simona Babcakova

Simona Babcakova (February 21, 1973 Šumperk-) a.k.a. Simona Babcáková or Simona Babčáková is a Czech actor.

Adéla Gondíková

Adéla Gondíková (August 19, 1973 Sokolov-) also known as Adéla Gondíková Brousková or A. Gondíková is a Czech actor.

Jitka Schneiderová

Jitka Schneiderová (March 23, 1973 Znojmo-) a.k.a. Jitka Schneidrová is a Czech actor. Her child is called Sofie Anna Švehlíková.

Sárka Vojtková

Sárka Vojtková (May 6, 1973 Brno-) is a Czech actor.

Eva Nosálková

Eva Nosálková (February 27, 1973 Olomouc-) is a Czech actor.
