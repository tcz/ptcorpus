Argentine actresses who were born in 1984

Here are 8 famous actresses from Argentina were born in 1984:

Celeste Cid

Celeste Cid (January 19, 1984 San Cristóbal, Buenos Aires-) also known as Maria Celeste Cid or María Celeste Cid is an Argentine actor. She has one child, André Horvilleur.

Brenda Gandini (August 8, 1984 Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine model and actor. She has one child, Eloy Heredia.

Sofía Reca

Sofía Reca (March 31, 1984 Palermo, Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine presenter, actor and singer.

Jorgelina Guadalupe Airaldi

Jorgelina Guadalupe Airaldi (April 17, 1984 Santa Fe-) is an Argentine actor.

Belén Rodríguez
