Mexican actors who were born in 1966

Here are 7 famous actors from Mexico were born in 1966:

Carlos Gallardo (June 22, 1966 Acuña Municipality-) otherwise known as Gallardo is a Mexican screenwriter, film producer, actor and film director.

Fernando Colunga

Fernando Colunga (March 3, 1966 Mexico City-) also known as Fernando Colunga Olivares is a Mexican actor.

Sergio Mayer

Julio Preciado

Julio Preciado (December 1, 1966 Mazatlán-) a.k.a. Preciado, Julio, Julio César Preciado Quevedo or Julio Covarrubias is a Mexican singer and actor.

Oliverio Gareli

Oliverio Gareli (September 13, 1966 Monterrey-) otherwise known as Oli is a Mexican actor and accountant.

Felix Cordova

Felix Cordova (December 6, 1966 Veracruz-) is a Mexican actor.

Sergio Quiñones
