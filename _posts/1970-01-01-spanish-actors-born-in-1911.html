Spanish movie actors born in the year 1911

Here are 9 famous actors from Spain were born in 1911:

Antonio Casas

Antonio Casas (November 11, 1911 A Coruña-February 14, 1982 Madrid) also known as Antonio Casas Barros was a Spanish actor and football player.

Alfredo Mayo

Alfredo Mayo (May 17, 1911 Barcelona-May 19, 1985 Palma, Majorca) a.k.a. Alfredo Fernández Martínez, Alfredo Majo, Alfred Mayo, Alfred May or Alfredo Fernandez Martinez was a Spanish actor and military officer.

José Luis Sáenz de Heredia

José Luis Sáenz de Heredia (April 10, 1911 Madrid-November 4, 1992 Madrid) a.k.a. Jose Luis Saenz de Heredia, José Luis Sáenz de Heredia y Osio, J.L. Sáenz de Heredia, José L. Sáenz de Heredia, Luis de Heredia, José Luis Sáenz De Heredia, J. Luis Saenz de Heredia, J.L. Saenz de Heredia or Jose L. Saenz de Heredia was a Spanish film director, writer and actor. He had one child, Ricardo Sáenz de Heredia.

Rafael Durán

Rafael Durán (December 15, 1911 Madrid-February 12, 1994 Seville) a.k.a. Rafael Duran or Rafael Durán Espayaldo was a Spanish actor. His child is called María Durán.

Rafael Luis Calvo

Rafael Luis Calvo (December 30, 1911 Madrid-November 5, 1988 Barcelona) a.k.a. Rafael Luis Calvo Muñoz, Raphael Luis Calvo or Rafael L. Calvo was a Spanish actor.

Joaquín Portillo

Joaquín Portillo (February 26, 1911 Madrid-August 14, 1994 Madrid) a.k.a. Joaquín Portillo 'Top', Joaquin Portillo, Tip y Top or Top was a Spanish actor, comedian and drafter.

Raúl Cancio

Raúl Cancio (September 18, 1911 Donostia / San Sebastián-October 23, 1961 Donostia / San Sebastián) also known as Ceferino Cancio, Raul Cancio or Ceferino Cancio Amunárriz was a Spanish actor.

Pepe Blanco

Pepe Blanco (March 19, 1911 Logroño-December 17, 1981 Madrid) also known as José Blanco Ruiz, Jose Blanco Ruiz or Marchenita was a Spanish actor and singer. He had one child, Jose Blanco.

Vincente Gomez

Vincente Gomez (July 8, 1911 Madrid-December 23, 2001 Los Angeles) was a Spanish composer, actor and guitarist.
