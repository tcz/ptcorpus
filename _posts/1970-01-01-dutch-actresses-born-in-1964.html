Dutch actresses who were born in 1964

Here are 7 famous actresses from Netherlands were born in 1964:

Linda de Mol

Linda de Mol (July 8, 1964 Hilversum-) also known as Margaretha de Mol, Mol, de, Linda or Linda Margaretha de Mol is a Dutch presenter, actor and writer. Her children are called Julian Vahle and Noah Vahle.

Kiki Classen

Kiki Classen (February 19, 1964 Rotterdam-) is a Dutch actor.

Corinne Boon

Corinne Boon (April 23, 1964 Krommenie-) is a Dutch actor. She has one child, Julian Boon.

Lou Lou Rhemrev

Lou Lou Rhemrev (January 14, 1964 Leiden-) otherwise known as Loulou Rhemrev or Lou-Lou Rhemrev is a Dutch actor.

Jip Wijngaarden

Jip Wijngaarden (July 5, 1964 Amsterdam-) a.k.a. Janneke Wijngaarden or Yanneke Wijngaarden is a Dutch actor and painter.

Oda Spelbos

Oda Spelbos (November 9, 1964-) is a Dutch actor.

Yolanda Foster

Yolanda Foster (January 11, 1964 Papendrecht-) also known as Yolanda van den Herik-Hadid, Yolanda H. Foster or Yolanda Hadid is a Dutch actor, model and interior designer. She has three children, Gigi Hadid, Anwar Hadid and Bella Hadid.
