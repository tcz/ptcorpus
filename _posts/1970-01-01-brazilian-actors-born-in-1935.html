Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1935

Here are 9 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1935:

Carlos Eduardo Imperial

Carlos Eduardo Imperial (November 24, 1935 Cachoeiro de Itapemirim-November 4, 1992 Rio de Janeiro) a.k.a. Carlos Eduardo da Corte Imperial, Carlos Imperial, Carlos Eduardo Corte Imperial or Dad was a Brazilian actor, record producer, songwriter, presenter, screenwriter, film director, film producer, film editor and film score composer. He had two children, Maria Luiza Imperial and Marco Antonio Imperial.

Plínio Marcos

Plínio Marcos (September 29, 1935 Santos, São Paulo-November 29, 1999 São Paulo) otherwise known as Plínio Marcos de Barros was a Brazilian writer, actor, playwright, screenwriter and theatre director. He had three children, Ana de Barros, Kiko de Barros and Leo de Barros.

Roberto Maya

Roberto Maya (January 5, 1935 Campinas-) is a Brazilian actor.

Geraldo Vandré

Geraldo Vandré (September 12, 1935 João Pessoa, Paraíba-) also known as Geraldo Vandré, Vandré, Geraldo, Geraldo Pedrosa de Araujo Dias or Geraldo Vandre is a Brazilian film score composer, actor and singer.

Renato Aragão

Renato Aragão (January 13, 1935 Sobral-) also known as Renato Aragao, Antônio Renato Aragão, Didi, Didi Mocó Sonrisépio Colesterol Novalgino Mufumbo or Didi Mocó Sonrisal Colesterol Novalgina Mufumu is a Brazilian actor, screenwriter, film producer, comedian, writer, film score composer, film art director, voice actor, singer, historian and presenter. He has five children, Livian Aragão, Juliana Aragão, Renato Aragão Jr., Ricardo Aragão and Paulo Aragão Neto.

Tarcísio Meira

Tarcísio Meira (October 5, 1935 São Paulo-) otherwise known as Tarcisio Meira or Tarcísio Magalhães Sobrinho is a Brazilian actor. He has one child, Tarcísio Filho.

Juca de Oliveira

Juca de Oliveira (March 16, 1935 São Paulo-) a.k.a. Juca da Oliveira or José de Oliveira Santos is a Brazilian actor. He has one child, Isabela Santos.

Antonio Augusto Fagundes

Antonio Augusto Fagundes (November 4, 1935 Alegrete-) a.k.a. Antônio Augusto Fagundes or Antônio Augusto da Silva Fagundes is a Brazilian actor, screenwriter, film director, film producer and costume designer.

Older Cazarré

Older Cazarré (January 16, 1935 Pelotas-February 26, 1992 Rio de Janeiro) was a Brazilian actor.
