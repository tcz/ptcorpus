Danish movie stars born in 1965

Here are 8 famous actresses from Denmark were born in 1965:

Connie Nielsen

Connie Nielsen (July 3, 1965 Frederikshavn-) also known as Connie Inge-Lise Nielsen or Connie Nielson is a Danish actor. Her children are called Bryce Thadeus Ulrich-Nielsen and Sebastian Sartor.

Ann Eleonora Jørgensen

Ann Eleonora Jørgensen (October 16, 1965 Hjørring-) a.k.a. Ann Eleanora Jørgensen is a Danish actor.

Anne-Grethe Bjarup Riis

Anne-Grethe Bjarup Riis (November 21, 1965 Herning-) a.k.a. Anne Grethe Bjarup Riis is a Danish actor, screenwriter and film director.

Helle Dolleris

Helle Dolleris (August 5, 1965 Aabybro-) a.k.a. Helle Charlotte Dolleris, Helle Dories or Emmas Dilemma is a Danish actor and screenwriter.

Mette Horn

Mette Horn (January 11, 1965 Charlottenlund-) also known as Mette Agnete Horn, Mette Agnethe Horn Christensen, Mette Agnethe Horn, Emmas Dilemma or Mette A. Horn is a Danish actor and screenwriter.

Helene Egelund

Helene Egelund (February 16, 1965 Denmark-) is a Danish actor.

Laura Kamis Wrang

Laura Kamis Wrang (June 30, 1965 Denmark-) also known as Laura Justine Kamis Vrang, Laura J.K. Wrang or Laura Wrang is a Danish actor.

Andrea Vagn Jensen

Andrea Vagn Jensen (August 22, 1965 Odder-) a.k.a. Andrea Jensen or Chalotte Vagn Jensen is a Danish actor. She has two children, Harald Toksværd and Felix Toksværd.
