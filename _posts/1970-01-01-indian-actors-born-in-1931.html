Indian movie stars born in 1931

Here are 13 famous actors from India were born in 1931:

Shammi Kapoor

Shammi Kapoor (October 21, 1931 Mumbai-August 14, 2011 Mumbai) also known as Shamsher Raj Kapoor, Shammi Kapur, Shri Shammi Kapoor, Elvis Presley of India, Shamsher Raj Prithviraj Kapoor, Shammi, Rebel Star or Shamsher Raj "Shammi" Kapoor was an Indian actor and film director. His children are called Aditya Raj Kapoor and Kanchan Ketan Desai.

C. V. Sreeraman

C. V. Sreeraman (February 7, 1931 Cheruthuruthi-October 10, 2007 Thrissur) also known as C.V. Sriraman was an Indian writer, actor and screenwriter.

O. N. V. Kurup

Kurup or Ottaplakkal Nambiyadikkal Velu Kurup is an Indian writer, poet, teacher, lyricist and actor. He has two children, Mayadevi Kurup and Rajeevan Kurup.

Singeetam Srinivasa Rao

Singeetam Srinivasa Rao (September 21, 1931 Gudur-) also known as Singeetam, Srinivasa Rao Singeetham, Shingitham Srinivasa Rao, Shinghitham Sreenivasa Rao, Singeetham Sreenivasa Rao or Singeetham Srinivasa Rao is an Indian writer, film director, screenwriter, actor and film score composer.

Rabi Ghosh

Rabi Ghosh (November 24, 1931 Kolkata-February 4, 1997 Kolkata) also known as Robi Ghosh Dostidar, Ekalavya, Robi Ghosh, Robi Ghose or Rabi Ghosh Dostidar was an Indian actor and film director.

Basavaraju Venkata Padmanabha Rao

Basavaraju Venkata Padmanabha Rao (August 20, 1931 Simhadripuram-February 20, 2010 Chennai) otherwise known as Padmanabham, Venkata Padmanabham Basavaraju, Basavaraju Venkata Padmanabha Rao or B. Padmanabham was an Indian actor, film producer and film director.

Raavi Kondala Rao

Raavi Kondala Rao (January 1, 1931 Srikakulam-) also known as Ravi Kondal Rao or Sukumar is an Indian actor, writer, film producer and screenwriter.

Vaali

Vaali (October 29, 1931 Srirangam-July 18, 2013 Chennai) also known as T. S. Rangarajan or Valli was an Indian actor, writer, screenwriter, poet and lyricist.

Kamu Mukherjee

Kamu Mukherjee (June 14, 1931-December 6, 2003) also known as Shri Kamu Mukherjee, Kamu Mukherji or Kamu Mukhopadhyay was an Indian actor.

Irshad Panjatan

Irshad Panjatan (September 7, 1931 Hyderabad-) also known as Irshad Panjtan is an Indian actor and mime artist.

Tarun Kumar

Tarun Kumar (February 24, 1931 Kolkata-October 27, 2003 Kolkata) was an Indian actor.

Mulraj Rajda

Mulraj Rajda (November 13, 1931 Kalbadevi-September 23, 2012 Mumbai) also known as Mool Raj Rajda was an Indian actor. He had one child, Sameer Rajda.

Dilip Mukherjee

Dilip Mukherjee (November 24, 1931 Bhagalpur-March 31, 1990 India) also known as Dilip Mukherji, Dilip Mukhopadhyay or Yatrik was an Indian actor and film director.
