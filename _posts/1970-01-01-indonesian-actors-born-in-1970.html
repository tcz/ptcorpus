Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1970

Here are 10 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1970:

Iwa K

Iwa K (October 25, 1970 Bandung-) also known as Iwa Kusuma or Iwa K. is an Indonesian rapper, presenter and actor.

Aksan Sjuman

Aksan Sjuman (September 22, 1970 Indonesia-) also known as Sri Aksana or Sri Aksan Sjuman is an Indonesian film score composer, musician and actor. He has one child, Miyake Shakuntala.

Abdel Achrian

Abdel Achrian (September 27, 1970 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor, comedian and presenter.

Ari Wibowo

Ari Wibowo (December 26, 1970 Berlin-) is an Indonesian actor, singer and model. He has two children, Kenzo Wibowo and Marco Wibowo.

Komeng

Komeng (August 25, 1970 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian comedian, presenter and actor.

Eko Patrio

Eko Patrio (December 30, 1970 Nganjuk Regency-) is an Indonesian actor, comedian, presenter, politician and film producer.

Adrianto Sinaga

Adrianto Sinaga (November 6, 1970 Pematangsiantar-) is an Indonesian production designer, actor, film director and film art director.

Teuku Edwin

Teuku Edwin (July 14, 1970 Banda Aceh-) a.k.a. Edwin is an Indonesian actor, singer and presenter. His children are called Cut Mala, Cut Audrey, Teuku Raka and Euro Syahputra.

Ryan Hidayat

Ryan Hidayat (September 19, 1970 Jakarta-February 8, 1997 Jakarta) was an Indonesian model and actor.

Agastya Kandou

Agastya Kandou (January 1, 1970 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor.
