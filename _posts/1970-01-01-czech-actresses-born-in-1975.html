Czech movie actresses born in the year 1975

Here are 7 famous actresses from Czech Republic were born in 1975:

Linda Rybová

Linda Rybová (October 15, 1975 Prague-) also known as Lynda Rybova is a Czech actor. She has three children, Rozárka Prachařová, Josefína Prachařová and František Prachař.

Petra Spalková

Petra Spalková (February 21, 1975 Brno-) also known as Petra Špalková is a Czech actor.

Lenka Geislerová

Lenka Geislerová (February 2, 1975 Prague-) also known as Lela Geislerová is a Czech actor.

Martina Preissová

Martina Preissová (October 31, 1975 Znojmo-) also known as Martina Stehlíková, Martina Válková or Martina Válková-Preissová is a Czech actor. She has one child, Matouš Preiss.

Gabriela Míčová

Gabriela Míčová (February 25, 1975 Prostějov-) also known as Gabriela Ball is a Czech actor.

Bára Nesvadbová

Bára Nesvadbová (June 14, 1975 Prague-) a.k.a. Barbara Nesvadbová or Barbora Nesvadbová is a Czech writer, screenwriter, actor and author. Her child is called Bibiana Březina.

Tereza Bebarová

Tereza Bebarová (July 8, 1975 Bohumín-) is a Czech actor.
