Filipino actors who were born in 1954

Here are 5 famous actors from Philippines were born in 1954:

Vic Sotto

Vic Sotto (April 28, 1954 Manila-) a.k.a. Marvic C. Sotto, Marvic Castelo Sotto, Bossing, Marvic Sotto, Marivic C. Sotto, Vic, Marvic "Vic" Castelo Sotto, Marvic or TVJ is a Filipino presenter, comedian, actor, film producer, singer, television producer, screenwriter, composer and writer. He has four children, Paulina Sotto, Danica Sotto, Vicco Sotto and Oyo Boy Sotto.

Ryan Cayabyab

Ryan Cayabyab (May 4, 1954 Manila-) also known as Raymundo Cipriano Pujante Cayabyab, Mr. C or Raymundo Capriano Pujante Cayabyab is a Filipino pianist, composer, singer, film score composer, musician, conductor and actor. He has two children, Cristina Maria Cayabyab and Antonio Maria Cayabyab.
