Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1966

Here are 6 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1966:

Donny Damara

Donny Damara (October 12, 1966 Indonesia-) also known as Donni Damara, Dhana, Donny Damara Prasa Dhana or Damara Donny is an Indonesian actor.

Katon Bagaskara

Katon Bagaskara (June 14, 1966 Magelang-) also known as Ignatius Bagaskoro Katon is an Indonesian singer-songwriter, musician, composer and actor. He has three children, Andhika Radya Bagaskara, Mario Arya Bagaskara and Chika Putri Bagaskara.

Derry Sudarisman

Derry Sudarisman (May 22, 1966 Lampung-) is an Indonesian actor and comedian.

Dede Yusuf

Dede Yusuf (September 14, 1966 Jakarta-) also known as Dede Yusuf Macan Effendi is an Indonesian actor and politician. He has two children, Alifiya Arkana Paramita and Kaneishia Lathifa Zahra.

Taufik Savalas

Taufik Savalas (June 9, 1966 Jakarta-July 11, 2007 Purworejo Regency) was an Indonesian actor, comedian and presenter.

Leon Lai

Leon Lai (December 11, 1966 Beijing-) also known as Lai Ming, Leon Lai Ming, Li Ming, Alexander Lai Chit, Leon Lai Ming, BBS, MH or Lai Chit is an Indonesian singer, actor, businessperson and film director.
