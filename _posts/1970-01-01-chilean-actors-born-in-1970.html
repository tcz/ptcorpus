Chilean movie stars born in 1970

Carlos Díaz

Carlos Díaz (July 21, 1970 Santiago-) also known as Carlos Patricio Diaz is a Chilean actor.

Pablo Schwarz (October 9, 1970 Santiago-) is a Chilean actor and drummer. His children are called Joaquín Schwartz and Emilio Schwartz.

Felipe Braun
