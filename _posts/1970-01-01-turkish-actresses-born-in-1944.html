Turkish actresses born in 1944

Here are 7 famous actresses from Turkey were born in 1944:

Sevda Nur

Sevda Nur (January 5, 1944 Istanbul-May 1, 2011) a.k.a. Nevval Köroglu was a Turkish actor.

Nebahat Çehre

Nebahat Çehre (March 15, 1944 Samsun-) also known as Hilal Nebahat Çehre or Nebahat Cehre is a Turkish actor, model and singer.

Feri Cansel

Feri Cansel (July 7, 1944 Nicosia-September 2, 1983 İzmir) also known as Can-Sel was a Turkish actor. Her child is called Zümrüt Cansel.

Nevra Serezli

Nevra Serezli (August 9, 1944 Ankara-) is a Turkish actor and voice actor. She has two children, Murat Serezli and Selim Serezli.

Candan Sabuncu

Candan Sabuncu (January 25, 1944 Ödemiş-) a.k.a. Candan Keresteci or Sevil Candan is a Turkish actor.

Mine Sun

Mine Sun (April 11, 1944 Giresun-) a.k.a. Bilgay Karadayi is a Turkish actor.

Eva Bender

Eva Bender (January 3, 1944 Junsele-) also known as Eva Abrahamson or Eva Kom is a Turkish actor.
