Argentine actors who were born in 1931

Here are 8 famous actors from Argentina were born in 1931:

Fernando Siro

Fernando Siro (October 5, 1931 Villa Ballester-September 4, 2006 Buenos Aires) a.k.a. Francisco Ángel Ludusid or Francisco Ángel Luksich was an Argentine screenwriter, actor and film director.

Héctor Pellegrini

Héctor Pellegrini (August 6, 1931 Buenos Aires Province-November 1, 1999 Mar del Plata) was an Argentine actor.

Héctor Olivera

Héctor Olivera (April 5, 1931 Olivos-) a.k.a. Hector Olivera is an Argentine film producer, film director, screenwriter, actor and entrepreneur.

Carlos Gandolfo

Carlos Gandolfo (March 27, 1931 Buenos Aires-January 12, 2005 Buenos Aires) was an Argentine actor and theatre director. He had two children, Matías Gandolfo and Emanuel Gandolfo.

Néstor Garay

Néstor Garay (January 13, 1931 Santa Rosa-April 11, 2003 Rome) also known as Nestor Garay, Nestor Garai or Nestore Garay was an Argentine actor.

Pepe Da Rosa

Pepe Da Rosa (May 1, 1931 Buenos Aires-April 22, 1986 Seville) also known as José Da-Rosa Villegas or José da Rosa Villegas was an Argentine actor. He had one child, Pepe Da-Rosa Jr..

Enrico Bergier

Enrico Bergier (December 28, 1931 Buenos Aires-) also known as Enrique Bergier or Henry Bergier is an Argentine actor.

Alberto Anchart

Alberto Anchart (September 24, 1931 Buenos Aires-October 31, 2011) was an Argentine actor.
