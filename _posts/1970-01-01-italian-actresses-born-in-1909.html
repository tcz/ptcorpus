Italian movie actresses born in the year 1909

Here are 9 famous actresses from Italy were born in 1909:

Isa Miranda

Isa Miranda (July 5, 1909 Milan-July 8, 1982 Garbatella) also known as Ines Isabella Sampietro or Ines Isabella Sanpietro was an Italian actor.

Nietta Zocchi

Nietta Zocchi (July 10, 1909 Rivoli-April 23, 1981 Rome) also known as Antonietta Zocchi, Lietta Zocchi or Nietta was an Italian actor.

Renata Vanni

Renata Vanni (October 12, 1909 Naples-February 19, 2004 Los Angeles) was an Italian actor. She had one child, Delia Salvi.

Anna Alberti

Anna Alberti (September 3, 1909 Cernusco sul Naviglio-) also known as Anna Lina Alberti, Anna Corrado, Lina Albert, Lia Alberti, Gladys Richener, The Troupe or Lina Alberti is an Italian actor.

Clara Calamai

Clara Calamai (September 7, 1909 Prato-September 21, 1998 Rimini) otherwise known as Clara Mais was an Italian actor.

Evelina Gori

Evelina Gori (July 10, 1909 Greve in Chianti-July 27, 2005 Rome) was an Italian actor.

Isa Pola

Isa Pola (December 19, 1909 Bologna-December 17, 1984 Milan) a.k.a. Maria Luisa Betti di Montesano was an Italian actor.

Sarah Ferrati

Sarah Ferrati (December 9, 1909 Prato-March 3, 1982 Rome) was an Italian actor. She had one child, Monica Infantino.

Dria Paola

Dria Paola (November 21, 1909 Rovigo-November 12, 1993 Rome) also known as Etra Pitteo was an Italian actor.
