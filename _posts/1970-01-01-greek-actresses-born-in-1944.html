Zoe Laskari

Eleni Anousaki

Eleni Anousaki (May 1, 1944 Athens-) a.k.a. Helen Anousouki or Helen Anousaki is a Greek actor.

Corinna Tsopei

Corinna Tsopei (June 21, 1944 Athens-) a.k.a. Kiriaki Tsopei, Corinna Tsopei Fields or Kyriaki "Corinna" Tsopei is a Greek actor and fashion model.

Mina Adamaki

Mina Adamaki (July 17, 1944 Volos-) is a Greek actor.

Sasa Kastoura

Sasa Kastoura (October 8, 1944 Thessaloniki-) also known as Sasa Castura is a Greek actor.

Mariza Koch
