Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1948

Here are 6 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1948:

A. Rafiq

A. Rafiq (March 5, 1948 Jakarta-January 19, 2013) was an Indonesian musician and actor.

Adi Kurdi

Adi Kurdi (September 22, 1948 Pekalongan-) is an Indonesian actor. He has one child, Maria Advena Victoria.

Broery

Broery (June 25, 1948 Ambon, Maluku-April 7, 2000 Cinere) also known as Broery Marantika, Broery Pesulima, Simon Dominggus Pesulima or Broery Abdullah was an Indonesian singer, musician, actor and composer.

Mark Sungkar

Mark Sungkar (October 22, 1948 Surakarta-) is an Indonesian actor and film director. He has four children, Shireen Sungkar, Zaskia Sungkar, Yusuf Averoes Sungkar and Jamilah Sungkar.

Rudy Salam

Rudy Salam (December 3, 1948 Salatiga-) a.k.a. Rudy Sutantio Abdul Salam or Clarissa's Father is an Indonesian actor. He has two children, Runa Salam and Rama Prakarsa Salam.

Arswendo Atmowiloto

Arswendo Atmowiloto (November 26, 1948 Surakarta-) is an Indonesian actor, screenwriter, film director and journalist.
