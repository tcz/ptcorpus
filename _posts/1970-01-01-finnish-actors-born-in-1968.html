Finnish actors born in 1968

Here are 6 famous actors from Finland were born in 1968:

Tommi Eronen

Tommi Eronen (September 18, 1968 Kuopio-) otherwise known as Tommi Johannes Eronen is a Finnish actor.

Tommi Korpela

Tommi Korpela (August 23, 1968 Helsinki-) also known as Julmahuvi, Tommi Petteri Korpela or Peter Tommi Korpela is a Finnish actor and screenwriter. His child is called Ilmari Korpela.

Eero Aho

Eero Aho (January 26, 1968 Oulu-) also known as Eero-Matti Aho is a Finnish actor. His child is called Ella Aho.

Samuli Edelmann

Samuli Edelmann (July 21, 1968 Pori-) also known as Samuli Edelman, Edelmann, Samuli or Samuli Casimir Edelmann is a Finnish actor and singer. He has two children, Venla Edelmann and Ilmari Edelmann.

Stan Saanila

Stan Saanila (June 27, 1968 Helsinki-) also known as Stefan Olof Saanila is a Finnish stand-up comedian, actor and screenwriter.

Janne Hyytiäinen

Janne Hyytiäinen (August 23, 1968-) otherwise known as Janne Hyytiainen is a Finnish actor.
