Thai movie stars born in 1947

Here are 6 famous actresses from Thailand were born in 1947:

Aranya Namwong

Aranya Namwong (September 4, 1947 Lopburi Province-) also known as Anchali Sirachaya, Piak, Anchali Choppradit or Aranya Namvong is a Thai actor and film producer. She has one child, Puttida Sirachaya.

Vasana Chalakorn

Vasana Chalakorn (September 18, 1947 Bangkok-) also known as Wasana Chalakorn is a Thai actor.

Jaruwan Panyopas

Jaruwan Panyopas (September 15, 1947 Sam Chuk District-) also known as Maew is a Thai actor. Her child is called Lalita Panyopas.

Pimkae Goonchorn Na Ayuthaya

Pimkae Goonchorn Na Ayuthaya (April 13, 1947 Phra Nakhon Si Ayutthaya Province-) is a Thai actor. Her child is called Pattanapol Mintakhin.

Buppa Saichol

Buppa Saichol (April 13, 1947 Chonburi Province-July 8, 1990 Chonburi Province) also known as Songkran Payonlert was a Thai actor. She had one child, Surachai Saichol.

Kwanjit Sriprajan

Kwanjit Sriprajan (August 3, 1947 Suphan Buri Province-) a.k.a. Kwanjit Sriprajun, Kwan Jit Sriprajan or Khwanchit Siprachan is a Thai actor.
