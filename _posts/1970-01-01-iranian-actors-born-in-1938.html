Iranian actors born in 1938

Here are 5 famous actors from Iran were born in 1938:

Behrouz Vossoughi

Behrouz Vossoughi (March 10, 1938 Khoy-) also known as Khalil Vossoughi, بهروز وثوقی‎, Bruce Vaughn, Behrouz Vossi or Behrooz Vossoughi is an Iranian actor. He has two children, Mahshid Vossoughi and Mahan Vossoughi.

John Tillinger

John Tillinger (June 28, 1938 Tabriz-) also known as Joachim F. Tillinger is an Iranian actor and theatre director. He has two children, Sebastian Tillinger and Emma Tillinger.

Parviz Fannizadeh

Parviz Fannizadeh (January 27, 1938 Tehran-February 24, 1980 Tehran) a.k.a. Parviz Fanizadeh was an Iranian actor.

Ali Rafie

Ali Rafie (January 11, 1938 Isfahan-) a.k.a. Ali Raffi is an Iranian theatre director, playwright, set designer and actor.

Fereydoun Farrokhzad

Fereydoun Farrokhzad (October 7, 1938 Gomrok-August 8, 1992 Bonn) a.k.a. Fereydoon Farrokhzad was an Iranian singer, poet, writer, entertainer, radio personality, television presenter and actor. His child is called Rostam Farrokhzad.
