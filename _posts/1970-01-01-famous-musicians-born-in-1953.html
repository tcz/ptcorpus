His most recognized albums: Massenet: Don Quichotte, Rachmaninov: Symphonic Dances, , The Nutcracker, Symphony no. 6, Symphony no. 6 "Pathétique" / Romeo and Juliet Fantasy Overture, , Liszt: My Piano Hero, The Rite of Spring / The Poem of Ecstasy and Sheherazade.

Christopher Franke

Christopher Franke (April 6, 1953 Berlin-) also known as Tangerine Dream is a German composer, musician, songwriter, drummer, keyboard player and film score composer.
