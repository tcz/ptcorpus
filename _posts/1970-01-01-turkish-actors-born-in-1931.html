Turkish actors born in 1931

Here are 11 famous actors from Turkey were born in 1931:

Semih Sergen

Semih Sergen (May 11, 1931 Istanbul-) is a Turkish actor. He has two children, Burak Sergen and Toprak Sergen.

Altan Günbay

Altan Günbay (January 19, 1931 Konya-March 3, 2014 Istanbul) also known as Altan Gunbay was a Turkish actor.

Zeki Müren

Zeki Müren (December 6, 1931 Bursa-September 24, 1996 İzmir) also known as Zeki Muren, Müren, Zeki or The Sun of Art was a Turkish singer, actor and composer.

Gazanfer Özcan

Gazanfer Özcan (January 27, 1931 Istanbul-February 17, 2009 Istanbul) was a Turkish actor.

Sedat Demir

Sedat Demir (March 25, 1931 Istanbul-) is a Turkish actor.

Ulvi Doğan

Yıldırım Önal

Hakkı Kıvanç

Erol Keskin

Erol Keskin (November 3, 1931 Istanbul-) is a Turkish actor.

Hüseyin Zan

Toron Karacaoğlu
