Italian movie actors born in the year 1902

Here are 13 famous actors from Italy were born in 1902:

Enzo Turco

Enzo Turco (June 8, 1902 Naples-July 7, 1983 Rome) was an Italian actor and screenwriter.

Afro Poli

Afro Poli (December 22, 1902 Pisa-February 22, 1988 Rome) was an Italian opera singer and actor.

Enzo Maggio

Enzo Maggio (October 10, 1902 Naples-July 13, 1978 Naples) a.k.a. Vincent Maggio or Vincenzo Maggio was an Italian actor and comedian.

Giorgio Capecchi

Giorgio Capecchi (August 7, 1902 Livorno-August 29, 1978 Rome) was an Italian actor and voice actor.

Erminio Macario

Erminio Macario (May 27, 1902 Turin-March 26, 1980 Turin) also known as Macario was an Italian actor, writer and comedian. He had two children, Alberto Macario and Mauro Macario.

Giulio Stival

Giulio Stival (March 4, 1902 Venice-April 1, 1953 Novara) was an Italian actor and voice actor.

Leone Jacovacci

Leone Jacovacci (April 19, 1902 Democratic Republic of the Congo-November 16, 1983 Milan) was an Italian actor and professional boxer.

Alfredo Rizzo

Alfredo Rizzo (September 29, 1902 Nice-September 6, 1991 Rome) also known as Fred Ritz or Alfred Rice was an Italian actor, screenwriter and film director.

Mario Cimino

Mario Cimino (December 7, 1902 Praiano-July 2, 1981 Los Angeles) was an Italian actor.

Gino Saltamerenda

Gino Saltamerenda (June 2, 1902 Rome-September 25, 1951 Rome) was an Italian actor.

Emilio Baldanello

Emilio Baldanello (March 3, 1902 Padua-July 21, 1952 Venice) was an Italian actor. His child is called Gianfranco Baldanello.

Giuseppe Varni

Giuseppe Varni (March 20, 1902 Warsaw-February 25, 1965 Rome) also known as Giacomo Varni was an Italian actor.

Nunzio Filogamo

Nunzio Filogamo (September 20, 1902 Palermo-January 24, 2002 Rodello) was an Italian actor and singer.
