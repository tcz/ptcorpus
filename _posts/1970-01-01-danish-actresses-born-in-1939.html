Danish movie stars born in 1939

Here are 6 famous actresses from Denmark were born in 1939:

Lone Hertz

Lone Hertz (April 23, 1939 Copenhagen-) is a Danish actor. Her children are called Steen Stig Lommer, Thomas Strøbye, Michäela Strøbye and Maria Lindhardt.

Vivi Bach

Vivi Bach (September 3, 1939 Copenhagen-April 22, 2013 Ibiza) a.k.a. Vivienne Bach, Vivi Bak, The Danish Bardot or Vivienne Bak was a Danish actor, singer, presenter, author, writer, painter and illustrator.

Charlotte Ernst

Charlotte Ernst (February 13, 1939 Copenhagen-June 11, 1973 Denmark) otherwise known as Charlotte Motzfeldt Ernst was a Danish actor. She had four children, Peter Reichhardt, Caroline Reichhardt, Frederikke Reichhardt and Henriette Reichhardt.

Jane Thomsen

Jane Thomsen (April 14, 1939 Denmark-) is a Danish actor.

Yvonne Ingdal

Yvonne Ingdal (December 10, 1939 Frederiksberg-) also known as Yvonne Ingdahl or Yvonne Birgit Ingal Jensen is a Danish actor.

Bende Harris

Bende Harris (May 23, 1939 Frederiksberg-) is a Danish actor. She has one child, Kim Harris.
