Filipino actresses who were born in 1973

Here are 6 famous actresses from Philippines were born in 1973:

Pia Guanio

Pia Guanio (October 24, 1973 Manila-) also known as Liana Pia Coronado Guanio, Liana Pia Coronado Guanio-Mago or Liana Pia Coronado Guanio Mago is a Filipino presenter and actor. She has one child, Scarlet Jenine Mago.

Mickey Ferriols

Mickey Ferriols (March 8, 1973 Philippines-) a.k.a. Michelle Forster is a Filipino actor. Her child is called Brent Marcus Alemania.

Gelli de Belen

Gelli de Belen (May 25, 1973 Quezon City-) also known as Anna Gianelli De Belen, G.D.B., Gelli, Ateng Gelli, Anna Gianelli de Belen-Rivera or GDB is a Filipino actor. She has two children, Joaquin Andres Rivera and Julio Alessandro Rivera.

Angel Aquino

Angel Aquino (February 7, 1973 Dumaguete-) also known as Angelita Velasquez Aquino is a Filipino actor and fashion model. She has two children, Thea Aquino and Iana Aquino.

Joan Almedilla

Joan Almedilla (September 19, 1973 Cebu-) otherwise known as Joan Richelle Almedilla or Joan Richelle C. Almedilla is a Filipino actor, singer and composer.

Hazel Espinosa

Hazel Espinosa (March 1, 1973 Manila-) otherwise known as Hazel Espinoza is a Filipino actor.
