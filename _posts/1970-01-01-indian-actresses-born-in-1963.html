Indian movie stars born in 1963

Here are 7 famous actresses from India were born in 1963:

Sridevi

Sridevi (August 13, 1963 Sivakasi-) also known as Shree Amma Yanger, Sridevi, Sreedevi, Baby Sridevi, Shreedevi, Sridevi Boney Kapoor, Sreedevi Kapoor, Sri Devi, Shridevi, Sridevi Ayyapan, Puppy, Shree Amma Iyenger, the first female superstar of Bollywood, Sridevi Kapoor or Shree Amma Yanger Ayyapan is an Indian actor, film producer and playback singer. She has two children, Jahnavi Kapoor and Khushi Kapoor.

Meenakshi Seshadri

Meenakshi Seshadri (November 16, 1963 Sindri-) also known as Shashikala Seshadri, Meenakshi Seshadhadri, Meenakshi Seshadhri, Sheshadri, Sherry or Meenakshi Sheshadri is an Indian actor and dancer. Her children are called Josh Mysore and Kendra Mysore.

Ambika

Ambika (November 6, 1963 Kallara, Thiruvananthapuram-) is an Indian actor. Her children are called Ram Kesav and Rishikesh.

Ratna Pathak

Ratna Pathak (March 18, 1963 Mumbai-) a.k.a. Ratna Pathak Shah is an Indian actor. Her children are called Vivaan Shah, Imaad Shah and Heeba Shah.

Sumalatha

Sumalatha (August 27, 1963 Chennai-) also known as Sumalatha Ambarish, Sumalata or Sumalata Ambarish is an Indian actor. She has one child, Abishek Ambareesh.

Jaya Seal

Jaya Seal (February 19, 1963 Guwahati-) also known as Jaya Sil or Jaya Seal Ghose is an Indian actor.

Sujatha Mohan

Sujatha Mohan (March 31, 1963 Thiruvananthapuram-) a.k.a. Sujatha or Sujata is an Indian singer, playback singer and actor. She has one child, Shweta Mohan.
