Indonesian movie actors born in the year 1973

Here are 12 famous actors from Indonesia were born in 1973:

Tora Sudiro

Tora Sudiro (May 10, 1973 Jakarta-) also known as Taura Danang Sudiro, Tora Danang Sudiro or Gusti Tora Danang Sudiro is an Indonesian actor, comedian and musician. He has three children, Jenaka Mahila Sudiro, Nayara Kanahaya Sudiro and Azzahra Nabila Sudiro.

Indra Birowo

Indra Birowo (January 9, 1973 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor and comedian. He has two children, Mahija Shafiq Birowo and Arkananta Putra Birowo.

Winky Wiryawan

Winky Wiryawan (December 9, 1973 Bandung-) otherwise known as Winky Nurayendra Irwindo, DJ Winky or Nurayendra Irwindo is an Indonesian actor, disc jockey and presenter.

Ari Sihasale

Ari Sihasale (October 5, 1973 Tembagapura-) also known as Ale or Ary Sihasale is an Indonesian film producer, film director, actor, musician and singer. His child is called Radit Christiano Zulkarnaen Sihasale.

Jefri Al Buchori

Jefri Al Buchori (April 12, 1973 Jakarta-April 26, 2013 Jakarta) was an Indonesian singer-songwriter, actor, model, dancer and preacher. He had three children, Adiba Khanza Az-Zahra, Mohammad Al-Ghifari Abidzar and Ayla Azuhro.

Rako Prijanto

Rako Prijanto (May 4, 1973 Magelang-) is an Indonesian film director and actor.

Gary M. Iskak

Gary M. Iskak (July 10, 1973 Indonesia-) also known as Gary Iskak or ary Iskak is an Indonesian actor. He has three children, Muhammad Azriel Gamitza Iskak, Rabiya Putrisyah and Muhammad Adilla Rafisya Iskak.

Azis Gagap

Azis Gagap (December 22, 1973 Jakarta-) otherwise known as Aziz Gagap is an Indonesian comedian and actor. His children are called Ahmad Fahrizal, Maulida Azis and Arya Purnama.

Yadi Sembako

Yadi Sembako (July 17, 1973 Jakarta-) also known as Suryadi Ishaq is an Indonesian actor and comedian.

Gunawan Sudradjat

Gunawan Sudradjat (January 12, 1973 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor and model.

Bucek Depp

Bucek Depp (September 9, 1973 Jakarta-) is an Indonesian actor and model. His children are called Arla Ailani Muchtar, Salmaa Chetizsa Muchtar, Salwaa Chetizsa Muchtar and Daffa Jenaro Muchtar.

Atalarik Syah

Atalarik Syah (June 2, 1973 Surabaya-) is an Indonesian model and actor.
