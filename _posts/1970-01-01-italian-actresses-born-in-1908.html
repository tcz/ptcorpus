Italian movie actresses born in the year 1908

Here are 8 famous actresses from Italy were born in 1908:

Anna Magnani

Anna Magnani (March 7, 1908 Rome-September 26, 1973 Rome) also known as La Lupa, Ann Magnani, La Magnani, Nannarella, Perennial toast of Rome, The Italian Édith Piaf, A. Magnani or living she-wolf symbol was an Italian actor. She had one child, Luca Magnani.

Maria Cumani Quasimodo

Maria Cumani Quasimodo (May 20, 1908 Castelveccana-November 22, 1995 Milan) otherwise known as Maria Cumari Quasimodo, Maria Quasimodo, Maria Quasimodo Cumani, Maria Clementina Cumani Quasimodo or Clementina Cumani was an Italian dancer and actor. Her child is called Alessandro Quasimodo.

Marcella Rovena

Marcella Rovena (January 22, 1908 Conegliano-August 9, 1998 Rome) was an Italian actor and voice actor.

Evi Maltagliati

Evi Maltagliati (August 11, 1908 Florence-April 27, 1986 Rome) a.k.a. Evy Maltagliati or Evelina Maltagliati was an Italian actor and voice actor.

Rina Morelli

Rina Morelli (December 6, 1908 Naples-July 17, 1976 Rome) also known as Elvira Morelli was an Italian actor and voice actor.

Suzet Maïs

Suzet Maïs (January 31, 1908 Paris-January 24, 1989 Aix-en-Provence) a.k.a. Suzette Charlotte Marie Mathilde Roux was an Italian actor.

Lidia Simoneschi

Lidia Simoneschi (April 4, 1908 Rome-September 5, 1981 Rome) was an Italian actor and voice actor. She had one child, Giorgio Lehmann.

Enrica Dyrell

Enrica Dyrell (April 15, 1908 Rome-April 15, 1965 Rome) also known as Enrica Direll was an Italian actor.
