Filipino actresses who were born in 1955

Here are 5 famous actresses from Philippines were born in 1955:

Mel Tiangco

Mel Tiangco (August 10, 1955 Philippines-) also known as Carmela Tiangco Cornejo or Carmela Tiangco is a Filipino journalist and actor. She has one child, Wency Cornejo.

Charo Santos-Concio

Charo Santos-Concio (October 27, 1955 Calapan-) also known as Maria Rosario Santos, Ms. Charo Santos, Ma. Rosario N. Santos, Charo Santos Concio, Charo S. Concio, Charos Santos or Charo Santos is a Filipino actor, film producer and television producer. She has two children, Francis Concio and Martin Concio.

Dexter Doria

Dexter Doria (April 16, 1955 Metro Manila-) is a Filipino actor.

Kuh Ledesma

Kuh Ledesma (March 16, 1955 Bacolod-) also known as Maria Socorro Ledesma-Gonzales or Maria Socorro Ledesma is a Filipino singer and actor. She has one child, Isabella Gonzalez.

Raquel Monteza

Raquel Monteza (September 30, 1955-) also known as Raquel Montesa, Racquel Montesa, Racquel Montessa, Raquel Montessa or Racquel Monteza is a Filipino actor. Her children are called Ryan Soler, Symon Soler and Maffy Soler.
