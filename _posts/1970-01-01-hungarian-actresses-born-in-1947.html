Hungarian actresses born in 1947

Here are 7 famous actresses from Hungary were born in 1947:

Ildikó Kishonti

Ildikó Kishonti (April 26, 1947 Budapest-August 30, 2009 Budapest) was a Hungarian actor.

Ildiko Piros

Ildiko Piros (December 23, 1947 Kecskemét-) also known as Ildikó Piros is a Hungarian actor and voice actor.

Ildikó Bánsági

Ildikó Bánsági (October 19, 1947 Budapest-) is a Hungarian actor and voice actor. Her children are called Kata Gáspár and Gergely Gáspár.

Márta Martin

Márta Martin (November 24, 1947 Budapest-) is a Hungarian actor and voice actor.

Györgyi Andai

Györgyi Andai (May 10, 1947 Budapest-) also known as Anday Györgyi is a Hungarian actor and voice actor.

Anette Antal

Anette Antal (July 16, 1947 Budapest-) also known as Antal Anetta or Antal Anett is a Hungarian actor.

Gabi Jobba

Gabi Jobba (March 27, 1947 Szeged-August 29, 1983 Budapest) also known as Jobba Gabriella was a Hungarian actor and singer.
