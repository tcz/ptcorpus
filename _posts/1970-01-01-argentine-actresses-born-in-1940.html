Argentine actresses who were born in 1940

María Cristina Laurenz (July 9, 1940 Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine actor and singer.

Chunchuna Villafañe

Chunchuna Villafañe (April 9, 1940 Buenos Aires-) a.k.a. Chunchuna Villafane or Elva Villafañe is an Argentine actor, architect and model. She has two children, Juana Molina and Inés Molina.

Dora Baret

Dora Baret (July 7, 1940 Córdoba Province, Argentina-) also known as Dora Barrera is an Argentine actor. Her children are called Emanuel Gandolfo and Matías Gandolfo.

Nacha Guevara

Alicia Aller

Alicia Aller (November 11, 1940 Buenos Aires-December 9, 2008 Buenos Aires) was an Argentine actor.

Claudia Lapaco

Claudia Lapaco (June 25, 1940 Buenos Aires-) also known as Claudia Lapacó is an Argentine actor, singer and dancer. Her children are called Rodrigo Bebán and Diego Bebán.

Mercedes Carreras

Mercedes Carreras (September 22, 1940 Pergamino-) also known as María Mercedes Carreras is an Argentine actor. She has three children, Marisa Carreras, Victoria Carreras and Maria Carreras.
