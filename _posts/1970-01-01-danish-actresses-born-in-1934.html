Danish movie stars born in 1934

Here are 6 famous actresses from Denmark were born in 1934:

Birgitte Price

Birgitte Price (April 29, 1934 Copenhagen-July 17, 1997 Copenhagen) a.k.a. Birgitte Bruun was a Danish actor. Her children are called James Price and Adam Price.

Ulla Lock

Ulla Lock (April 19, 1934 Copenhagen-September 20, 2012) was a Danish actor.

Ilselil Larsen

Ilselil Larsen (July 1, 1934 Aarhus-) is a Danish actor.

Jytte Abildstrøm

Jytte Abildstrøm (March 25, 1934 Copenhagen-) also known as Jytte Laila Zabell Abildstrøm is a Danish actor. She has two children, Peter Mygind and Lars Mygind.

Ann Smyrner

Ann Smyrner (November 3, 1934 Frederiksberg-) a.k.a. Hanne Smyrner or Anna Smyrner is a Danish actor.

Lone Rode

Lone Rode (May 29, 1934 Denmark-) a.k.a. Lone Rodhe is a Danish actor. She has one child, Mads Ole Erhardsen.
