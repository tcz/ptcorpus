Indian movie stars born in 1947

Here are 10 famous actresses from India were born in 1947:

Raakhee Gulzar

Raakhee Gulzar (August 15, 1947 Ranaghat-) also known as Rakhee, Raakhi Gulzar, Rakhee Biswas, Rakhi, Rakhee Gulzar, Rakhee Mazumdar, Raakhee Mazumdar Gulzar, Raakhee Gulzar, Raakhee Majumdar or Raakhee is an Indian actor. Her child is called Meghna Gulzar.

Simi Garewal

Simi Garewal (October 17, 1947 Delhi-) a.k.a. Simi, Simmi Garewal, Simmi, Simi Garowal, Simi Grewal, Simrita or Simrita Garewal is an Indian actor, television director and film director.

Mumtaz

Mumtaz (July 31, 1947 Mumbai-) a.k.a. Mumtaz Askari, Mumtaz Banoo or Mumu is an Indian actor. She has two children, Natasha Madhwani and Tanya Madhwani.

Usha Uthup

Usha Uthup (November 7, 1947 Mumbai-) a.k.a. Usha Utthup, Usha Utup, Usha Iyer, Uthup, Usha, Usha Uthap, Usha Utthap, Usha Uttup, Didi or Usha Vaidyanath Someshwar Sami is an Indian singer, playback singer and actor. She has two children, Sunny Uthup and Anjali Uthup.

Brinda Karat

Brinda Karat (October 17, 1947 Kolkata-) is an Indian politician and actor.

Vidya Sinha

Vidya Sinha (November 15, 1947 Mumbai-) a.k.a. Vidhya Sinha or Vidya Singh is an Indian actor and model. She has one child, Janhavi Iyer.

Shakuntala Barua

Shakuntala Barua (April 22, 1947 Kolkata district-) also known as Kumari Shakuntala, Shakunthala or Sakuntala Barua is an Indian actor.

Rama Prabha

Rama Prabha (May 5, 1947 Madanapalle-) also known as South Rama Prabha, Ramaprabha or Sulakshana is an Indian actor.

Suhas Joshi

Suhas Joshi (July 12, 1947-) also known as Suhaas Joshi or Suhasini Joshi is an Indian actor.

Soma Dey

Soma Dey (October 18, 1947 Kolkata-) is an Indian actor.
