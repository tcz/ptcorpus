Spanish movie actors born in the year 1952

Nacho Martínez (July 8, 1952 Mieres, Asturias-July 24, 1996 Oviedo) a.k.a. Ignacio Martínez Navia-Osorio or Nacho Martinez was a Spanish actor and voice actor.

Luis Pastor (June 9, 1952 Berzocana-) a.k.a. Luis Ángel Pastor Rodríguez is a Spanish film score composer and actor.

Miguel Ángel Jenner (June 2, 1952 Lavapiés-) also known as Miquel Àngel Jenner or Miguel Ángel Jenner Redondo is a Spanish actor and voice actor. He has two children, Michelle Jenner and David Jenner.

Michael Pan
