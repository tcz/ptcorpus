Indian movie stars born in 1934

Here are 9 famous actresses from India were born in 1934:

Ruma Guha Thakurta

Ruma Guha Thakurta (November 21, 1934 Kolkata-) also known as Ruma Ghosh, Ruma Devi, Ruma Debi, Ruma Guhathakurta, Ruma Ganguly, Ruma Gangopadhyay, Shrimati Ruma Guha Thakurta or Shrimati Ruma Guhathakurta is an Indian singer, actor and dancer. She has three children, Amit Kumar, Ayan Guha Thakurta and Shramana Guha Thakurta.

Jamila Massey

Jamila Massey (January 7, 1934 Shimla-) also known as Jamilla Massey is an Indian actor.

Vijaya Mehta

Vijaya Mehta (November 4, 1934 Vadodara-) a.k.a. Vijaya Jaywant or Vijaya Mehta Farrokh is an Indian actor, film director and screenwriter. She has one child, Anahita Uberoi.

Kumari Kamala

Kumari Kamala (June 16, 1934 Mayiladuthurai-) a.k.a. Kamala Lakshman, Kamala Laxman, Kamala Kumari, Kamla Laxman, Baby Kamala, Kamala Narayan or Kamala Lakshmi Narayanan is an Indian dancer and actor. Her child is called Jainand Narayan.

Anna Kashfi

Anna Kashfi (September 30, 1934 Darjeeling-) also known as Joan O'Callaghan or Johanna O'Callaghan is an Indian actor and model. Her child is called Christian Brando.

Renu Saikia

Renu Saikia (December 10, 1934 Sripuria, Tinsukia-November 17, 2011) a.k.a. Renu Borbora was an Indian actor.

Chitra Sen

Chitra Sen (May 5, 1934 Kolkata-) also known as Chitra Mandal is an Indian actor and dancer. She has one child, Kaushik Sen.

Bhanumati Devi

Bhanumati Devi (May 15, 1934 Myanmar-January 4, 2013 Puri) a.k.a. Bhanumati was an Indian actor. She had one child, Swarnalata Panda.

Smita Sinha

Smita Sinha (December 16, 1934 Kolkata-) a.k.a. Smita Singha is an Indian actor.
