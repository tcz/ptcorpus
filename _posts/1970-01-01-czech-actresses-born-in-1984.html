Czech movie actresses born in the year 1984

Here are 5 famous actresses from Czech Republic were born in 1984:

Karolína Kurková

Karolína Kurková (February 28, 1984 Děčín-) also known as Karolina Kurkova, Karolina Isela Kurkova, KK or La Kurkova is a Czech model, supermodel, fashion model and actor. She has one child, Tobin Jack Drury.

Simona Brhlíková

Simona Brhlíková (September 14, 1984 Czechoslovakia-) a.k.a. Simona Roman is a Czech actor.

Ester Geislerová

Ester Geislerová (March 5, 1984 Prague-) also known as Eta, Ester Geislerova or Ester Kadlecová-Geislerová is a Czech actor. Her children are called Jan Etien Kadlec and Mia Rosa Kadlecová.

Anna Fixová

Anna Fixová (March 17, 1984 Prague-) is a Czech actor.

Klara Smetanova

Klara Smetanova (May 18, 1984 Czech Republic-) otherwise known as Angelina, Zoe B., Sin Cindy, Cindy, Clara, Eleni, Juliana, Zoe K., Klarazoe, Klara, Lisa, Madison, Melina, Zoe Montada, Klara S., Zoe Saint, Zoe Trope, Zoé Trope, Veronica, Zoey, Zoe, Zooey or Klára Smetanová is a Czech actor.
