Mexican actors who were born in 1940

Here are 8 famous actors from Mexico were born in 1940:

Pedro Armendáriz, Jr.

Pedro Armendáriz, Jr. (April 6, 1940 Mexico City-December 26, 2011 Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center) also known as Pedro Armendariz Jr., Pedro Armendáriz Bohr, Pedro Armendariz Marquez, Pedro Armendáriz, Pedro Ármendariz, Pedro Armendariz, Pedro Armandarez Jr., Pedro Armendáriz Pardo or Armendáriz Jr. was a Mexican actor, film producer, television producer and voice actor.

Manuel Ojeda

Manuel Ojeda (November 4, 1940 La Paz-) also known as Jesús Manuel Ojeda, Manuel Salvador Ojeda Armenta or Jesús Manuel Ojeda Ruiz de la Peña is a Mexican actor.

Ismael Perez

Ismael Perez (June 9, 1940 Mexico City-) a.k.a. Ismael Pérez 'Poncianito', 'Poncianito', Poncianito or Ismael Lopez Arce Y Siu is a Mexican actor.

Hugo Stiglitz

Hugo Stiglitz (August 28, 1940 Mexico City-) a.k.a. Hugo Stiglits, Hugo Stieglitz, Hugo Stiglietz or Stiglitz is a Mexican actor, film producer, film director and screenwriter.

Juan Gallardo

Juan Gallardo (March 15, 1940 Ixcapuzalco-September 24, 1999 Mexico City) was a Mexican actor.

Vicente Fernández

Vicente Fernández (February 17, 1940 Guadalajara-) also known as Vicente Fernandez, Vincente Fernandez, Fernández, Vicente, Vicente Fernández Gómez, Chente, The king of ranchera music, Vicente Fernandez Gomez, El Rey de Mexico or El rey de la canción ranchera is a Mexican singer, actor and film producer. He has four children, Alejandro Fernández, Vicente Fernández, Gerardo Fernández and Alejandra Fernández.

Alberto Vázquez

Alberto Vázquez (April 20, 1940 Guaymas-) also known as Alberto Vazquez or Vázquez, Alberto is a Mexican singer and actor. He has one child, Arthur Vázquez.

Cornelio Reyna

Cornelio Reyna (September 15, 1940 Parras de la Fuente-January 22, 1997 Mexico City) a.k.a. Reyna, Cornelio was a Mexican actor, composer and musician. He had one child, Cornelio Reyna Jr..
