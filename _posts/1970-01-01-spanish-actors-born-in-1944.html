Spanish movie actors born in the year 1944

Here are 13 famous actors from Spain were born in 1944:

José Luis Garci

José Luis Garci (January 20, 1944 Madrid-) a.k.a. Jose Luis Garci, José Luis García Muñoz, J.L. Garci or J.L.Garci is a Spanish presenter, film director, actor, screenwriter, film producer, film editor, author, television director and film critic. He has one child, Mercedes Garci.

Eloy de la Iglesia

Eloy de la Iglesia (January 1, 1944 Zarautz-March 23, 2006 Madrid) also known as Eloy Germán de la Iglesia Diéguez, Eloy G. de la Iglesia or Eloy Germán de la Iglesia was a Spanish screenwriter, film director and actor.

Xabier Elorriaga

Xabier Elorriaga (April 1, 1944 Maracaibo-) also known as Xavier Elorriaga, Javier González Elorriaga, Javier Elorriaga, Xavier Ellorriaga, Javier Elorriaga González or Xabier González Elorriaga is a Spanish actor.

Miguel Ríos

Miguel Ríos (June 7, 1944 Granada-) also known as Miguel Rios, Ríos, Miguel, Miguel Ríos Campaña, Mike Ríos, Miguel Punta Rios, Miguel "Punta" Rios or Rios, Miguel is a Spanish singer, actor, composer, film score composer and television director.

Enrique Irazoqui

Enrique Irazoqui (July 5, 1944 Barcelona-) is a Spanish actor.

José Sancho

José Sancho (November 11, 1944 Manises-March 3, 2013 Valencia) a.k.a. José Asunción Martínez Sancho, Jose Sancho or Pepe Sancho was a Spanish actor. His children are called Alejandro Sancho and Javier Sancho.

Maximo Valverde

Maximo Valverde (November 16, 1944 Seville-) also known as Máximo González Valverde Hortal, Max Valverde, Massimo Valverde or Máximo Valverde is a Spanish actor.

Carles Canut

Carles Canut (September 23, 1944 Barcelona-) also known as Carlos Canut or Carles Canut i Bartra is a Spanish actor.

Rafael Palmero

Rafael Palmero (September 14, 1944 Madrid-September 29, 2006 Madrid) also known as Rafael Antonio Palmero Romero or Rafael A. Palmero was a Spanish set decorator, production designer, film art director and actor. His child is called Rafael Palmero Jr..

Fernando Méndez-Leite

Fernando Méndez-Leite (May 6, 1944 Madrid-) a.k.a. D. Fernando Méndez Leite, Fernando Méndez Leite or Fernando Méndez-Leite Serrano is a Spanish film director, television director, screenwriter and actor.

Mario Pardo

Mario Pardo (April 16, 1944 Berga-) also known as Mario Pardo Rodríguez is a Spanish actor.

Carlos Romero Marchent

Carlos Romero Marchent (February 22, 1944 Madrid-August 19, 2013 Spain) also known as C. Romero Marchent, Carlos Romero-Marchent or Carlos R. Marchent was a Spanish film director and actor.

Fernando García Rimada

Fernando García Rimada (December 1, 1944 Madrid-October 14, 2012 Málaga) otherwise known as Fernando Garcia Rimada was a Spanish actor.
