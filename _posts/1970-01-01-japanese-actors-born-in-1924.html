Japanese movie stars born in 1924

Here are 15 famous actors from Japan were born in 1924:

Kihachi Okamoto

Kihachi Okamoto (February 17, 1924 Yonago-February 19, 2005 Tama-ku, Kawasaki) a.k.a. Kihachiro Okamoto was a Japanese film director, screenwriter and actor. His child is called Mami Okamoto.

Rikidōzan

Rikidōzan (November 14, 1924 Hongwon County-December 15, 1963 Tokyo) a.k.a. ももた みつひろ, キム・シルラク, 力道山, りきどうざん, 역도산, Father of Puroresu, 金 信洛, Yeokdosan, Momota Mitsuhiro, Mitsuhiro Momota, 百田 光浩 or Kim Sin-rak was a Japanese sumo wrestler, actor and wrestler. His children are called Mitsuo Momota and Yoshihiro Momota.

Kōji Tsuruta

Kōji Tsuruta (December 6, 1924 Hamamatsu-June 16, 1987 Japan) also known as Kōji Tsuruta, Tsurata Koji, おの えいいち, Ono Eiichi, 鶴田 浩二, 小野 榮一, Eiichi Ono or Koji Tsuruta was a Japanese actor and singer.

Yoichi Numata

Yoichi Numata (July 19, 1924 Yubara-April 29, 2006 Tokorozawa) a.k.a. Mikamo Masaharu, Y. Numata, Masaharu Mikamo, ぬまた よういち, 美甘 正晴, 沼田 曜一 or みかも まさはる was a Japanese actor.

Saburo Date

Saburo Date (March 27, 1924 Osaka-September 12, 1991) also known as Saburô Date, 伊達 三郎, だて さぶろう, Date Saburo, Sakura Shuntaro, だて たけし, 伊達 岳志, Takeshi Date, さくら しゅんたろう, 桜 春太郎, Shuntaro Sakura or Date Takeshi was a Japanese actor.

Hosei Komatsu

Hosei Komatsu (November 4, 1924 Nagano-July 11, 2003 Tokyo) also known as Toyoshige Komatsu, Hôsei Komatsu or Hōsei Komatsu was a Japanese actor and voice actor.

Norihei Miki

Norihei Miki (April 11, 1924 Tokyo Prefecture-January 25, 1999 Tokyo) otherwise known as Tanuma Tadashi, みき のりへい, 三木 のり平, Tadashi Tanuma, Miki Norihei, 田沼 則子 or たぬま ただし was a Japanese actor, comedian and theatre director. He had one child, Norikazu Kobayashi.

Akira Kume

Akira Kume (February 8, 1924 Tokyo-) also known as Kume Akira is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He has three children, Shusaku Kume, Daisaku Kume and Nanako Kume.

Ryoichi Tamagawa

Ryoichi Tamagawa (October 15, 1924 Maebashi-October 14, 1992) otherwise known as たまがわ りょういち, Tamagawa Ryoichi, 玉川 良一, 新井 良雄 or 玉川良太郎 was a Japanese comedian, actor and voice actor.

Masahiko Naruse

Masahiko Naruse (September 30, 1924 Bunkyō-) is a Japanese actor.

Roppeita Kita XVI

Roppeita Kita XVI (December 6, 1924-) is a Japanese actor.

Koji Uno

Koji Uno (April 21, 1924 Omuta-July 28, 2003) also known as Kôji Uno was a Japanese actor and film art director.

Nanto Yuji

Nanto Yuji (April 26, 1924 Osaka-March 19, 1973) was a Japanese actor and comedian.

Setsuo Shinoda

Setsuo Shinoda (November 3, 1924 Japan-May 22, 1995) was a Japanese voice actor and actor.

Kanta Mori

Kanta Mori (January 19, 1924 Akita-November 15, 2000) was a Japanese actor.
