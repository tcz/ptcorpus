Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1968

Here are 14 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1968:

Eduardo Moscovis

Petrônio Gontijo (July 5, 1968 Varginha-) a.k.a. Petronio Gontijo or Petrônio Gontijo de Alvarenga is a Brazilian actor.

Daniel

Daniel (September 9, 1968 Brotas-) a.k.a. José Daniel Camillo is a Brazilian singer and actor. His children are called Lara Camillo and Luiza Camillo.

Otto

Heitor Martinez Mello

Hugo Duarte

Dino Dos Santos (July 18, 1968 Londrina-) a.k.a. Dino Dossantos, Dino Frenkel, Dino Frenkiel, Dino Santos or Ednaldo dos Santos is a Brazilian actor and stunt performer.

Nicola Siri

Alex Amaral

Cazé Peçanha
