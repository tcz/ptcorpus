Serbian musicians born in the year 1976

Here are 5 famous musicians from Serbia were born in 1976:

Škabo

Škabo (December 4, 1976 Belgrade-) also known as Bosko Cirkovic, Skabo or Boško Ćirković is a Serbian journalist, record producer and rapper.

His albums: Sam, Remek delo and Čovek. Genres he performed include Hip hop music and Serbian hip hop.

Ana Popović

Ana Popović (May 13, 1976 Belgrade-) also known as Ana Popovic or Popovic, Ana is a Serbian singer, songwriter and guitarist.

Her albums: Hush!, Comfort to the Soul, ANA! Live in Amsterdam, Still Making History, Unconditional, Can You Stand The Heat, Blind for Love and An Evening at Trasimeno Lake: Live From the Heart of Italy. Genres: Blues, Blues rock, Serbian rock, Jazz, Soul music, Rhythm and blues and Funk.

Tijana Dapčević

Dara Bubamara (May 21, 1976 Novi Sad-) is a Serbian singer.

Vlado Georgiev

Vlado Georgiev (June 6, 1976 Dubrovnik-) is a Serbian singer and singer-songwriter.

His albums include Navika, Zena bez imena, Nisam ljubomoran, Do svitanja, Daljina and Hej ti. Genres: Pop music and Acoustic music.
