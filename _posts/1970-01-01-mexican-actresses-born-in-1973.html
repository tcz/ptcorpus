Mexican actresses who were born in 1973

Here are 11 famous actresses from Mexico were born in 1973:

Jaydy Michel

Jaydy Michel (December 20, 1973 Guadalajara-) also known as Jaydy Michel Brixon or Jaydy Mitchel is a Mexican model and actor. She has one child, Manuela Sánchez Michel.

Vanessa Bauche

Vanessa Bauche (February 18, 1973 Mexico City-) a.k.a. Alma Vanessa Bauche Chavira or Vanesa Bauche is a Mexican actor, editor, writer, film producer and film director.

Susana González

Alpha Acosta

Adriana Lavat

Adriana Lavat (September 7, 1973 Chilpancingo-) is a Mexican actor. Her children are called Santiago Rafael Márquez and Rafaela Márquez.

Jeannine Derbez

Jeannine Derbez (December 29, 1973 Mexico City-) also known as Jeannine Francoise Derbez Riestra is a Mexican actor.

Mónica Aragón

Maite Embil
