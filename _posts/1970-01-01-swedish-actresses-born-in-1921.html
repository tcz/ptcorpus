Swedish actresses who were born in 1921

Here are 8 famous actresses from Sweden were born in 1921:

Barbro Hjort af Ornäs

Barbro Hjort af Ornäs (August 28, 1921 Gothenburg-) a.k.a. Barbara Hart or Barbro Hjort af Ornäs is a Swedish actor.

Elsie Albiin

Elsie Albiin (December 17, 1921 Helsingborg-April 3, 2009 Virum, Denmark) a.k.a. Elsie Ford, Elsy Albin or Elsy Albiin was a Swedish actor.

Britta Holmberg

Britta Holmberg (December 21, 1921 Fors-June 3, 2004 Falsterbo) also known as Britta Alice Holmberg, Britta Olin-Holmberg, Brita Olin, Britta Olin, Britta Holmberg-Olin or Britta Alice Olin was a Swedish actor and singer. Her children are called Lena Olin and Mats Olin.

Birgitta Arman

Birgitta Arman (August 3, 1921 Stockholm-April 13, 2007 Stockholm) a.k.a. Birgitta Maria Arman was a Swedish actor.

Gertrud Fridh

Gertrud Fridh (November 26, 1921 Gothenburg-October 11, 1984 Stockholm) was a Swedish actor. She had two children, Pierre Wilkner and Pamela Wilkner.

Gunnel Wadner

Gunnel Wadner (January 8, 1921 Stockholm-August 16, 2008 Stockholm) otherwise known as Gunnel Leth, Gunnel Wadner-Leth or Gunnel Hilda Maria Wadner was a Swedish actor.

Gunnel Brostrom

Gunnel Brostrom (October 25, 1921 Stockholm-June 28, 2012 Stockholm) also known as Gunnel Ester M. Broström was a Swedish actor and film director. Her children are called Hans Olivecrona and Eva Olivecrona.

Fylgia Zadig

Fylgia Zadig (November 3, 1921 Malmö Municipality-November 5, 1994) was a Swedish actor. Her children are called Klas Eklund and Lena Eklund.
