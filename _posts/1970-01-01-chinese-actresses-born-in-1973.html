Chinese actresses who were born in 1973

Here are 10 famous actresses from China were born in 1973:

Yoyo Mung

Yoyo Mung (August 3, 1973 Hong Kong-) otherwise known as Mung Ka Wai, Meng Jia Hui, Tai Koo, Yo Yo Mong, Yoyo Mung Ka-Wai or Mung Yo Yo is a Chinese actor and model.

Bowie Tsang

Bowie Tsang (February 21, 1973 Hong Kong-) a.k.a. Zēng Bǎoyí, a*baw, Ā Bǎo or Tsang Po Yee is a Chinese singer, actor, presenter and author.

Gloria Yip

Gloria Yip (January 13, 1973 Hong Kong-) also known as Wan-Yee Yip, Gloria Yip Wan-Yee, Ye Yun-Yi, 叶蕴仪 or 葉蘊儀 is a Chinese actor, singer and sculptor. She has two children, Chen Shaozhen and Chen Shaoyan.

Pauline Chan Bo-Lin

Pinky Cheung

Haiqiong Luo

Haiqiong Luo (August 10, 1973 Lanzhou-) is a Chinese actor.

Hongbo Jiang
