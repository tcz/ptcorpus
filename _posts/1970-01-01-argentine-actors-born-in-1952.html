Argentine actors who were born in 1952

Here are 7 famous actors from Argentina were born in 1952:

Carlos Rivarola

Carlos Rivarola (February 22, 1952 Villa Ballester-) is an Argentine dancer, choreographer and actor.

Norberto Díaz

Norberto Díaz (March 1, 1952 Buenos Aires-December 18, 2010 Buenos Aires) also known as Norberto Osvaldo Díaz or El Gallego was an Argentine actor. He had one child, Manuela Díaz.

Juan Leyrado

Juan Leyrado (August 18, 1952 Barracas, Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine actor.

Mario Galvano

Mario Galvano (October 29, 1952 Buenos Aires-) is an Argentine actor.

Ricardo Alanis

Ricardo Alanis (June 30, 1952 Buenos Aires-) a.k.a. Ricardo Alaniz is an Argentine actor.

Enrique Morales

Raúl Perrone
