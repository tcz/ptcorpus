Indian movie stars born in 1952

Here are 5 famous actresses from India were born in 1952:

Aruna Irani

Aruna Irani (May 3, 1952 Mumbai-) a.k.a. Aruna, Baby Aruna, Aroona Iarani or Aroona Irani is an Indian actor, television director and television producer.

Yogeeta Bali

Yogeeta Bali (August 13, 1952 Mumbai-) otherwise known as Yogita Bali [Chakraborty], Yogeetha Bali, Yogita Bali or Yogeeta Bali Chakraborty is an Indian actor and film producer. Her children are called Mahaakshay Chakraborty, Rimoh Chakraborty, Dishani Chakraborty, Namashi Chakraborty and Ushmey Chakraborty.

Lakshmi

Lakshmi (December 13, 1952 Chennai-) also known as Laxmi Narayan, Lakhmi, Laxmi, Lakshmi Narayan, Laxme, Lakshmi Yaragudipati, S.N. Lakshmi or Lakshmi is an Indian actor, film director and tv personality. She has one child, Aishwarya Bhaskaran.

Sujatha

Sujatha (December 10, 1952 Galle-April 6, 2011 Chennai) a.k.a. Sujatha Oru Thodarkathai or Sujata was an Indian actor. She had two children, Sajith and Divya.

Vaishali Kasaravalli

Vaishali Kasaravalli (April 12, 1952 Gulbarga-September 27, 2010 Bangalore) also known as Vaishali Kasarvalli, Vrushali, Vaishali or Vaishali Kasaravalli was an Indian costume designer, actor and television director. She had two children, Apoorva Kasaravalli and Ananya Kasaravalli.
