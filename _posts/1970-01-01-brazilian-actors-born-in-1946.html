Brazilian movie actors born in the year 1946

Here are 13 famous actors from Brazil were born in 1946:

Héctor Babenco

Héctor Babenco (February 7, 1946 Mar del Plata-) otherwise known as Héctor Eduardo Babenco or Hector Babenco is a Brazilian film director, screenwriter, actor and film producer.

Júlio Bressane

Júlio Bressane (February 13, 1946 Rio de Janeiro-) a.k.a. Júlio Eduardo Bressane de Azevedo or Júlio Bressane Eduardo Azevedo is a Brazilian screenwriter, film producer, film director, film editor, cinematographer, costume designer, actor, production designer and writer.

Cláudio Cunha

Cláudio Cunha (July 29, 1946 São Paulo-) also known as Cláudio Francisco Cunha is a Brazilian film producer, screenwriter, film director and actor. His child is called Cláudio Cunha.

Rogério Sganzerla

Rogério Sganzerla (November 26, 1946 Joaçaba-January 9, 2004 São Paulo) was a Brazilian screenwriter, film director, film score composer, film producer, film editor and actor. He had two children, Djin Sganzerla and Sinai Sganzerla.

José de Abreu

José de Abreu (May 24, 1946 Santa Rita do Passa Quatro-) otherwise known as José Pereira de Abreu Júnior or José Abreu is a Brazilian actor. He has five children, Rodrigo de Abreu, Theo de Abreu, Ana de Abreu, Cristiano de Abreu and Bernardo de Abreu.

Alceu Valença

Alceu Valença (July 1, 1946 São Bento do Una-) also known as Alceu Valenca or Alceu Paiva Valença is a Brazilian songwriter, singer, composer, writer, actor and poet.

Dennis Carvalho

Dennis Carvalho (September 27, 1946 São Paulo-) otherwise known as Denis Carvalho is a Brazilian actor and television director. He has four children, Leonardo Carvalho, Guilherme Carvalho, Luiza Carvalho and Tainá Carvalho.

Jones Melo

Jones Melo (June 24, 1946 Cortês-March 22, 2013 Brejo da Madre de Deus) was a Brazilian actor.

Carlos Alberto Riccelli

Carlos Alberto Riccelli (July 3, 1946 São Paulo-) also known as Carlos A. Riccelli or Carlos Riccelli is a Brazilian actor, film producer and film director. He has one child, Kim Riccelli.

Marcelo Melo

Marcelo Melo (February 12, 1946 Campina Grande-) a.k.a. Marcello Melo is a Brazilian actor.

Clemente Viscaíno

Clemente Viscaíno (February 1, 1946 Herculândia-) also known as Clemente Viscaino is a Brazilian actor.

Zé Rico

Zé Rico (June 29, 1946 São José do Belmonte-) also known as José Rico is a Brazilian composer and actor.

Belchior

Belchior (October 26, 1946 Sobral-) a.k.a. Antônio Carlos Gomes Belchior Fontenelle Fernandes is a Brazilian singer, composer, actor and voice actor.
