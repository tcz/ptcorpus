Indian movie stars born in 1955

Here are 7 famous actresses from India were born in 1955:

Smita Patil

Smita Patil (October 17, 1955 Pune-December 13, 1986 Mumbai) a.k.a. Smita, Late Smita Patil or Smitha Patil was an Indian actor and newscaster. She had one child, Prateik Babbar.

Kirron Kher

Kirron Kher (June 14, 1955 Punjab, India-) also known as Kiron Thakursingh-Kher, Kiran Kher, Kirron, Kiran Thakar Singh, Kiron Kher, Kiran, Kiron, Kirron Kher Anupam, Kirron Kher or Kirron Anupam Kher is an Indian actor, presenter and politician. She has one child, Sikandar Kher.

Mamata Shankar

Mamata Shankar (January 7, 1955 Kolkata-) also known as Mamta Shankar is an Indian actor, dancer and choreographer. Her children are called Ratul Shankar and Rajit Shankar.

Prema Narayan

Prema Narayan (April 4, 1955 Andhra Pradesh-) is an Indian actor and dancer.

Sharon Prabhakar

Sharon Prabhakar (August 4, 1955 Mumbai-) also known as Sheron Prabhakar, Shron Prabhakar, Sharan Prabhakar or Sharon Prabhaker is an Indian singer, actor, public speaker and playback singer. She has one child, Shazahn Padamsee.

Smita Hai

Smita Hai (October 13, 1955 Mumbai-) is an Indian actor.

Usha Chavan

Usha Chavan (October 17, 1955 Pune-) is an Indian actor.
