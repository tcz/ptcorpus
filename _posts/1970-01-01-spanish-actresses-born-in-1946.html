Spanish movie actresses born in the year 1946

Here are 9 famous actresses from Spain were born in 1946:

Marisa Paredes (April 3, 1946 Madrid-) also known as María Luisa Paredes Bartolomé is a Spanish actor. She has one child, María Isasi.

Paloma Cela

Paloma Cela (March 4, 1946 Madrid-) also known as Paloma Cela Molinero or María Luisa Cela is a Spanish model and actor.

Emma Cohen

Emma Cohen (November 21, 1946 Barcelona-) also known as Emmanuela Beltrán Rahola, Emma Silva or Emmanuela Beltrán is a Spanish actor, film director, film producer and screenwriter.

Diana Sorel

Diana Sorel (August 3, 1946 San Fernando de Henares-) also known as Laura Jimeno is a Spanish actor.

Pilar Velázquez

Pilar Velázquez (February 13, 1946 Madrid-) a.k.a. Pilar Vela, Llorente Pilar Velázquez or María del Pilar Velázquez Llorente is a Spanish actor.

Josele Román

Josele Román (October 28, 1946 Valencia-) also known as María José Peralt Román, Josele Roman, Maria Jose Roman, Mª José Roman, Joséle Román, Maria José Román, María José Román or Mª José Román is a Spanish actor and ballet dancer.

Cristina Hoyos

Cristina Hoyos (June 13, 1946 Seville-) a.k.a. Cristina, Christina Hoyos or Cristina Hoyos Panadero is a Spanish actor, choreographer and flamenco dancer.

Alejandra Nilo

Alejandra Nilo (November 14, 1946 Burgos-) is a Spanish actor.

María Luisa San José

María Luisa San José (January 22, 1946 Madrid-) also known as Maria Luisa San Jose, Marisa San Jose, Mª Luisa San Jose, Mª Luisa San José, Mª Luisa Sanjose, Marisa Sanjosé or María Luisa Sanjosé is a Spanish actor.
