Turkish actors born in 1956

Here are 6 famous actors from Turkey were born in 1956:

Semir Aslanyürek

Semir Aslanyürek (August 15, 1956 Harbiye-) otherwise known as Semir Aslanyurek is a Turkish film director, screenwriter, actor, writer and sculptor. His children are called Yusuf Aslanyürek and Enis Aslanyürek.

Halil Kumova

Halil Kumova (March 23, 1956 Bursa-) is a Turkish actor.

Talat Bulut

Talat Bulut (March 23, 1956 Sarıkamış-) also known as Talât Bulut is a Turkish actor. He has one child, Hazal Bulut.

Ahmet Çadırcı

Ahmet Çadırcı (August 13, 1956 Adana-) a.k.a. Ahmet Çadirci or Ahmet Cediladirci is a Turkish actor.

Ali Rıza Soydan

Ali Rıza Soydan (February 9, 1956 Niğde-) also known as Ali Riza Soydan is a Turkish actor.

Yılmaz Zafer

Yılmaz Zafer (September 24, 1956 Istanbul-November 9, 1995 Istanbul) a.k.a. Yilmaz Zafer was a Turkish actor. His child is called Savaş Zafer.
